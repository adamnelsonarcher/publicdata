MECE 3400 - Intro To Mechanics

Credit Hours: 4
Lecture Contact Hours: 4 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHYS 2325 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in MATH 3321.
Description
Statics of bodies, analysis of deformation and stress in elastic solids subject to bending and torsion, dynamics of particles, and mechanical vibrations.
No

Note: Not for mechanical engineering majors.
