GEOL 1370 - Natural Disasters

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 1314 or MATH 1332.
Description
The scientific study of the geology, meteorology, oceanography, and climatology of common natural disasters.
