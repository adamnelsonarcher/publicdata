SYSE 3315 - Systems Architecture & Design

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGI 2304.
Corequisite: SYSE 3301.
Description
This course will study and contrast various important architectural frameworks, representations, tools, and methodologies to provide scalable and flexible approaches for enterprises operating in dynamic and complex environments. System architecting tools and framework will be discussed and demonstrated. Other topics include analysis of architectural alternatives, architecture modelling languages and the use of model-driven architectures, validation of the architecture with stakeholders.
