SCM 4385 - Supply Chain Analytics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: SCM 4330 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in SCM 4362 ; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Supply chain modeling and design using computer simulation, optimization and forecasting software/tools. Applications include service system design, demand planning, transportation planning, facility network design, scarce resource allocation and others. Focus on problem definition, modeling, data collection and preparation, computer solution, and interpreting and communicating results.
