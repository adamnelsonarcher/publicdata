COMM 4373 - Intercultural Public Relations

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COMM 3368, or consent of instructor.
Description
Enables students to understand, critique, and apply current theory and principles of practicing public relations in intercultural settings. Focus is on understanding publics with diverse cultural identities and from diverse cultural backgrounds, building relationships with these publics, and managing conflicts.
