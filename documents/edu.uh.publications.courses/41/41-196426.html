RUSS 3307 - Soviet and Post-Soviet Cinema: Collapse of the Soviet Realm and Revival of National Identity

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Description
Analysis of Soviet and post-Soviet films within their historical, cultural, and aesthetic context with emphasis on the collapse of the Soviet realm and revival of national identity. Taught in English.
