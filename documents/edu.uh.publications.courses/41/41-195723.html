MATH 1332 - Elementary Mathematical Modeling

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
Functions, graphs, rates of change, mathematics of finance, optimization, and mathematics of decision making.
Yes

Note: This course is an introduction to Mathematics for students in the Humanities, Social Sciences, and Fine Arts. May not apply to course or GPA requirements for a major or minor in the College of Natural Sciences and Mathematics. Students may not receive credit for both MATH 1314 and MATH 1332.
