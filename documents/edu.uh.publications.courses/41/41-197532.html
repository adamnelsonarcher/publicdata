ECON 4374 - Behavioral Economics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ECON 3332 or consent of instructor.
Description
Introduction to basic findings of the Behavioral Economics field, including ways in which the predictions of classical economic theory fail in the real world, and to modifications to theories to better explain and predict human behavior.
