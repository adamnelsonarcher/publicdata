MUSI 4307 - Analysis of Rhythm and Meter

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUSI 2214 and passing grade on Theory Proficiency Exam IV and Aural Skills Proficiency Exam II. Music UGRD Majors Only.
Description
The study of meter and large-scale rhythm. Repertoire includes tonal, post-tonal, and popular Western music.
