TLIM 4341 - Production & Service Operation

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly TELS 4341.
Prerequisite: University approved statistics (TMTH 3360 or MATH 1342 or equivalent), MATH 1324 or TECH 3365, and C or better in TELS 3340.
Description
Planning and control of production and service operations. Product and service design, design of work systems, forecasting, scheduling, capacity planning, and inventory control.
