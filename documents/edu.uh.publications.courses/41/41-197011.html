ARTH 4380 - Museums and the Problem of Display

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ARTS 1303 and ARTS 1304 and six additional hours of art history, or consent of instructor.
Description
Development of the culture of display and the impact of art institutions, curators and exhibitions on the understanding of artworks and their history.
