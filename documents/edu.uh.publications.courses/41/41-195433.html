INDS 3500 - Industrial Design Studio V

Credit Hours: 5
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 9
Prerequisite: INDS 2501, PHYS 1301, and successful portfolio review at the conclusion of INDS 2501.
Description
Introduction to diverse research methods of identifying human perceptions and behaviors to explore creative design ideas for user-centered design.
