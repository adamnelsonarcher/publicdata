RELS 4360 - Clash of Civilizations

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 or equivalent.
Description
Reading and critical analysis of the so called “clash of civilizations” debate focusing on how religion contributes to the clash and the putative possibility that religion might contribute to the amelioration of the problem.
