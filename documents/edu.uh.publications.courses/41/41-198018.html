CNE 3322 - Legal Aspects of Construction Engineering

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CNE 3310, ENGI 2304.
Description
Basic legal concepts pertaining to the construction industry. Contracts, dispute resolution, change orders, lien laws, delay damages, contractor liability.
