POLC 3341 - State Authority and Freedom

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Studies the role of policy-making in democratic societies, as well as the function of policy-makers through detailed exploration of the nature and proper scope of state authority and its dangers and limits.
