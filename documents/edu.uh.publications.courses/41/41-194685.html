ENGL 3370 - Modern Irish Literature

Credit Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: six semester hours in core communications courses.
Description
Literature from the Celtic Renaissance to 1950 with emphasis on Lady Gregory, Yeats, Joyce, and Synge.
