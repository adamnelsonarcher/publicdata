SPAN 3312 - Fundamentals of English/Spanish Translation

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SPAN 3301 or SPAN 3307 and SPAN 3302 or SPAN 3308 .
Description
Development of fundamental skills in translating texts from a variety of genres from English into Spanish and vice versa.
