ARCH 3328 - Technology 4

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: ARCH 3327 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in ARCH 3501 and PHYS 1302.
Description
Advanced level building science focusing on quantitative measures. Case studies of integrated building systems: site, climate, structures, envelope, and materials.
