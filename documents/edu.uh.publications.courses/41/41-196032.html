NAVY 4301 - Naval Weapons - Naval Ship Systems II

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: NAVY 1301 .
Description
Theory and employment of weapons systems. Detection, evaluation, threat analysis, weapon selection, delivery, guidance, and explosives. Physical aspects of radar and underwater sound are described in detail.
