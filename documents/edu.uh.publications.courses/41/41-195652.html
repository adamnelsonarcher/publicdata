MANA 4336 - Legal and Ethical Environment of HR Management

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MANA 3335; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
A survey of federal and state employment and labor relations laws and discussions of the ethical contexts in which organizations satisfy their human resource management needs.
