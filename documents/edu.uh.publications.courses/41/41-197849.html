ARAB 3377 - Energy, Society and the Middle East

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Examination of energy, culture and sustainability in Middle East and North Africa (MENA). Surveys literature and theories on oil curse, energy security, youth bulge, gender gap, renewables and sustainability projects.
