RELS 3370 - The Bible and Modern Science

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 or ENGL 1310.
Description
Detailed study of the reciprocal influence of modern science and the interpretation of the Bible. Critical examination of contemporary theories of their relationship. The same assignment will be used to assess all noted competency areas.
