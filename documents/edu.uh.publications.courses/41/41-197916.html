ELET 4355 - Biomedical Signal Analysis

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ELET 2300 or equivalent, ELET 3301 .
Description
Digital signal processing with application to biomedical signals. Aspects of signal acquisition, processing, and feature extraction along with practical applications and elementary signal analysis algorithms.
