PHIL 1301 - Intro To Philosophy

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly PHIL 1340
Prerequisite: ENGL 1301.
Description
Introduction to philosophy: issues such as the nature and extent of knowledge, the objectivity of scientific theories, the rationality of theistic belief, and human freedom.
