GEOL 4382 - Introduction to Petroleum Geology

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GEOL 3350 or concurrent enrollment; GEOL 3345 or concurrent enrollment.
Description
Fundamentals of petroleum geology; source rock, reservoir and trap studies. Practical exploration methods including subsurface stratigraphy and mapping.
No

Note: Credit may not be applied for both GEOL 4382 and GEOL 6381.
