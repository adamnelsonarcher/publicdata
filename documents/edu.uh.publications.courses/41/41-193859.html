CHEM 3333 - Inorganic Chemistry I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEM 1112 and CHEM 1312 with grades of at least C-.
Description
Chemical properties of atoms and elements. Categories of compounds of Main Group and d-block elements including hydrides, oxides, hydroxides, halides, and others. Syntheses, reactions, and aspects of systematic chemistry. Introduction to the biochemistry of inorganic compounds.
No

Note: May not apply toward degree until CHEM 3133 is successfully completed.
