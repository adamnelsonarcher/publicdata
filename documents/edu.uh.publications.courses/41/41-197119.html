BIOL 4356 - Genomics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BIOL 3301 Genetics.
Description
Survey of genomics, including the organization and structure of genomes; genome sequencing, assembly, and annotation; global gene expression profiling; and comparative and functional genomics.
