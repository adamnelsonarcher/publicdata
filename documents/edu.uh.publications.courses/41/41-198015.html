CNE 3310 - Construction Scheduling, Planning and Control

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CNE 2211.
Corequisite: INDE 3330.
Description
Planning and scheduling of construction activities including network diagramming. Probabilistic activity durations, Critical Path Method (CPM), PERT, resource allocation, schedule updating, risk management, computer applications. Cost-schedule integration.
