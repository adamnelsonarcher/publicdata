SCM 4380 - Enterprise Resource Planning

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: SCM 4330 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in SCM 4362 good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Theory, concepts, and practices associated with ERP systems, which integrate business processes to support decision making. Provides hands-on experience using SAP R/3 and business intelligence tools.
