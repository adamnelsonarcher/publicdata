POLS 4333 - Islam and Democracy

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2306 and GOVT 2305 or equivalents, or consent of instructor.
Description
Focuses on the evolution of democratic Islamic thinking and its compatibility with Western notions of democracy. (Comparative Politics category.)
