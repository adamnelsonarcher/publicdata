PSYC 3350 - Introduction to Cognitive Psychology

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301, PSYC 2305, PSYC 3301 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
An information-processing approach to human functioning introducing topics in memory, language, thought, judgment, and skilled performance.
