BTIS 4300 - Customs, Regulations, and Procedures

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BTIS 3300.
Description
Study of the processes and procedures for customs clearance and the associated computer applications and systems. Types of entries, surety, bonds, quotas penalties, appraisement, bonded warehouse entry, an examination of goods, harmonized tariffs, fees, quotas, immediate delivery, laws, and regulations.
