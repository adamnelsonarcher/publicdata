POLC 3313 - Contemporary Political Philosophy and Policy

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
Explores a number of contemporary political theories or ideologies that inform contemporary public policy positions. The theories include egalitarian liberalism, libertarianism, socialism, communism, communitarianism, race theory, multiculturalism, feminism, and green political thought.
