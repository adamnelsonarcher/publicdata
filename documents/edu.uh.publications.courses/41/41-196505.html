SPAN 3302 - Advanced Spanish for Non-Heritage Learners

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SPAN 2312 in the twelve months prior to enrollment or by placement exam within a semester prior to enrollment.
Description
Heritage Learners of Spanish are not eligible to enroll. Cultural and literary readings, composition, and grammar review. Expansion of oral, written, aural, and reading skills through the communicative approach.
No
