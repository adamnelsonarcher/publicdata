THEA 4306 - Educational Theatre & Program Management in Schools

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: acceptance to the THEA major by audition/portfolio review and consent of instructor.
Description
Principles of structure, organization, and management of curricular, co-curricular, and extra-curricular theatre programs in schools.
