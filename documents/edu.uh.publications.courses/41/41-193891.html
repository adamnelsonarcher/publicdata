CHEM 4373 - Survey of Physical Chemistry

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEM 2123, CHEM 2323, and MATH 2414.
Description
States of matter, thermodynamics, equilibria, kinetics, solutions, and molecular structure including biological applications.
No
