RELS 3337 - Theologies from the Margins

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Emphasizes a comparative study of liberation theologies with a focus on Christianity in the United States and on gender, race, and sexuality.
