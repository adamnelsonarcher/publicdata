RELS 2340 - Introduction to Hinduism

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Historical and conceptual introduction to Hinduism: ancient and classical forms in addition to their modern manifestations, including forms these traditions take in the United States.
