BIOE 4305 - Brain Machine Interfacing

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGI 1331 and INDE 2333 .
Corequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in BIOE 4315 and BIOE 4115 .
Description
Principles of neuroscience, machine learning, statistics and probability theory, electrical systems, and chemistry as related to brain-machine interfacing.
