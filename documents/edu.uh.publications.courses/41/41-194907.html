GEOL 4334 - Environmental Data Analysis

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: MATH 2415 and either GEOL 1302 or GEOL 1347 or GEOL 3378 or consent of instructor.
Description
Physical and mathematical basis of environmental data analysis. Topics include basic concepts of statistics, regression, filtering, and principal component analysis, etc.
No

Note: Credit cannot be applied for GEOL 4334 and GEOL 6328.
Additional Fee Y
