CHEM 4340 - Research Methods

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: CUIN 3351 and one of CHEM 1111, CHEM 1117; BIOL 1106, BIOL 1107; PHYS 1101, PHYS 2125 or equivalent, or consent of instructor.
Description
Students perform four independent inquiries, combining skills from mathematics and science to solve research problems.
