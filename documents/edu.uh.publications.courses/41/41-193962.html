CLAS 3345 - Myth&Performance in Greek Trag

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
The reshaping of myths in the tragedies performed in the fifth century BCE and in modern productions of those plays. Critical appraisal of text and performance in the respective cultural contexts.
