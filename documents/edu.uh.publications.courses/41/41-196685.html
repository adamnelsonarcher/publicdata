THEA 3385 - Scenic Design

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: THEA 2334 , and consent of instructor.
Description
Theory, practice, and history of scenic design. Development and communication of ideas, script analysis, visual research, and design concepts.
