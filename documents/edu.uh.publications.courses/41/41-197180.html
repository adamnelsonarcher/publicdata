RELS 3323 - Continuities: Jewish and Christian Texts and Traditions

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 and Major or minor in Religious Studies or consent of instructor.
Description
Detailed historical study of the evolution of Jewish rituals and beliefs and how they shaped early Christianity. This course explores the continuing relevance of Judaism, and provides the necessary tools for an in-depth understanding of fundamental Christian concepts.
