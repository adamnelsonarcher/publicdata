NURS 4419 - High Acuity Clinical

Credit Hours: 4.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 12
Prerequisite: NURS 4521 , NURS 3311 , NURS 3315 , NURS 3312 , NURS 3313 , NURS 4211 , NURS 4213 , NURS 3314 , NURS 4316 , NURS 4417 .
Corequisite: NURS 4318 , and credit for or concurrent enrollment in NURS 4215 / NURS 4217 or NURS 4219 /NURS 4221.
Description
This course provides clinical practice opportunities in the high acuity setting to enable the student to acquire advanced nursing care and leadership/management skills with patients with complex health care needs.
