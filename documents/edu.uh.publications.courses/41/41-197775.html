DIGM 4381 - Mobile Application Design

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: (ELET 2300 or COSC 1336) & (Digital Media Major or Minor) & C or better in DIGM 1350.
Description
Mobile applications design using object-oriented programming; 2D and 3D graphics; cloning mobile applications.
