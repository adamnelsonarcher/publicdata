CHIN 3350 - Chinese Culture Through Films

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Reading film critics and viewing Chinese films produced in contemporary China within the historical, cultural, thematic, and aesthetic content.
No

Note: Taught in English. Satisfies three hours requirement for the Minor in Chinese studies. May not apply as foreign language credit.
Additional Fee Y
