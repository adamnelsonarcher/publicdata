INTB 3355 - Global Environment of Business

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly INTB 3355 The Political Economy of Globalizaton
Prerequisite: Junior standing, and ECON 2301 and ECON 2302.
Description
Examination and evaluation of global environment of business and the factors that impact business in the context of globalization.
No

Note: Students may not receive credit for both INTB 3352 or INTB 3353 and INTB 3355. Students with either INTB 3352 or INTB 3353 may substitute that credit for INTB 3355.
Additional Fee Y
