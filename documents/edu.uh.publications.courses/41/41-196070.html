PETR 3310 - Petroleum Production Economics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 2415 and 3321, PETR 2313 and 3362.
Description
Estimation of initial reservoir contents; production forecasting; versus time, primary, secondary and tertiary methods; evaluation of costs and risks versus expected rewards; and various US and international contracting techniques.
