CUIN 2300 - Introduction to STEM Teaching

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Major in the College of Natural Science and Mathematics or permission from TEACH Houston faculty advisor.
Corequisite: CUIN 3350.
Description
Introduction to secondary mathematics and science teaching with a focus on basic pedagogical concepts explored through classroom observations and teaching of STEM lessons.
