CIS 3367 - Cloud Computing Architecture and Implementation

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CIS Major and Grade of “C” or better in CIS 3347 .
Description
Architecting and building flexible, scalable, and reliable web-based systems capable of supporting millions of simultaneous requests. Provisional computing power, storage options, networking, and databases. Industry best practices and design patterns for high availability, event-driven scaling, web-scale storage, and full automation and decoupling of the infrastructure.
