CNE 3321 - Construction Engineering Management

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CNE 3310.
Description
Project and company organization. Manpower planning and procurement. Material and equipment procurement. Job site layout. Construction operation planning, supervision, measurement, analysis, improvement.
