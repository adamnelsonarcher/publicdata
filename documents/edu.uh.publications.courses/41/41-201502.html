POLC 3322 - Public Policy Analysis: Theory and Practice

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2306 and GOVT 2305, or equivalent.
Description
Introduces the processes of public policy-making and provides students the empirical and analytical tools of policy evaluations and the models of policymaking and policy analysis.
