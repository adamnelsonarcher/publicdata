CHIN 3359 - Chinese Culture through History

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
This course is restricted to students in the Chinese study abroad program. Studies of Chinese culture and history via course readings, Chinese museums and historical sites in China.
No

Note: Taught in English. May not apply as foreign language credit.
