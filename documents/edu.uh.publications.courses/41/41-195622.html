KIN 4691 - Internship in Sport Administration 2

Credit Hours: 6.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0.0 Lab Contact Hours: 6.0
Prerequisite: Instructor Approval and KIN 4190
Corequisite: Senior Status and KIN 4690
Description
Supervised internship with community, interscholastic, intercollegiate, amateur, or professional sport industry organization. Supervised by a university faculty member with support from sport industry agency personnel.
