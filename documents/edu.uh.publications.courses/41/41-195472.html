INDE 4372 - Operation Control

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: INDE 3381 , INDE 3382 , and INDE 4369 .
Description
Design of systems to control production, inventories, and other processes in industry. Development of planning, coordination, and control of specific operations, with an emphasis on computerized data processing.
