POLS 3370 - State Gov and Politics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2306 and GOVT 2305 or equivalent, or consent of instructor.
Description
Problems facing the states in the 20th century; roles of the legislature and governor; party systems, finances, and taxation; nature of governmental policies. (American Politics category.)
