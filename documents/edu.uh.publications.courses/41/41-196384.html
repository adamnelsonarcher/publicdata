RELS 1301 - Introduction To Religious Studies

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Comparative, interdisciplinary, and thematic approach to the study of religion, including analysis of Western and non-Western rituals, art, language, systems of thought and practices.
