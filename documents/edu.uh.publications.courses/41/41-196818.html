ACCT 5375 - Internal Auditing and the Entity Control Environment

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ACCT 2301 or equivalent with a minimum grade of C, a baccalaureate degree, good academic standing; pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Control and auditing procedures within companies.
