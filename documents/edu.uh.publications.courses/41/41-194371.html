DIGM 4372 - Costing in Digital Media

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Formerly GRTC 4372
Prerequisite: Grade of C or better in DIGM 2357, DIGM 3351, and DIGM 3354. (DIGM 3354 waived for Graphic Communications Technology Minors).
Description
Principles and techniques of cost estimation for digital media production.
