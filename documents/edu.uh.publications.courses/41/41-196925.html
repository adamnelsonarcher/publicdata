PETR 4301 - Reservoir Characterization and Modeling

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEE 3363 and PETR 3362 .
Description
Modern reservoir characterization techniques including geostatistical methods designed to capture the influence of geology on fluid flow storage. Derivation of the system of PDEs for fluid flow in porous media.
