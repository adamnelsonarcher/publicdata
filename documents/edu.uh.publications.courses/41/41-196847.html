KIN 4301 - Workplace Wellness

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly KIN 4397 Workplace Wellness
Prerequisite: KIN 1352 and KIN 3301.
Description
Strategies for changing employee health behaviors and worksite cultural norms. Implementation, marketing, cost-effectiveness and cost-benefit analysis of worksite wellness programs.
