SPEC 2350 - Registered Behavior Technician Training

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
This is a training class that satisfies the 40-hour training to become a Registered Behavioral Technican (RBT). A RBT is directly responsible for implementing interventions in Applied Behavior Analysis. This course covers all of the tasks in the RBT Task List (2nd ed), the RBT Ethics code, and at least one hour of supervision.
