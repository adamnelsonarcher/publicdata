MATH 3339 - Statistics for the Sciences

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 2414.
Description
Graphical and descriptive methods in statistics, probability, random variables and distributions, sampling, estimation, hypothesis testing, regression, analysis of variance, exploratory and diagnostics, statistical computing.
Yes

Note: Students may not receive credit for both MATH 3339 and MATH 3349.
Additional Fee Y
