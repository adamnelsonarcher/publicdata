ENRG 3311 - Fundamentals of Sustainability

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Overview of sustainable design, including definitions of sustainability and net zero, and evaluation of sustainability from the environmental, economic, and human perspectives.
