CHIN 3347 - Chinese Language, Pragmatics, and Communication

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
This course discusses the pragmatics of Chinese, the characteristics of Chinese communication, as well as general principles in intercultural communication.
