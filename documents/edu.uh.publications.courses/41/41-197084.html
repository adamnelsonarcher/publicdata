ECON 3361 - Economics of Globalization

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ECON 2301 or consent of instructor.
Description
Analysis of modern day economic globalization, focusing on capital market integration and its effect on economic growth and development, income inequality and labor markets.
No
