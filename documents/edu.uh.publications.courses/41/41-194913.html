GEOL 4343 - Atmospheric Instrumentation

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: GEOL 3342 or GEOL 3382.
Description
Overview of contemporary methods used in atmospheric research, including absorption spectroscopy, mass spectrometric, chromatographic, and radiometric methods. Hands-on laborartory where students set-up, operate, and collect field data.
