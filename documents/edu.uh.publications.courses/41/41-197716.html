CIVE 5375 - Digital Photogrammetry

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CIVE 4381 and COSC 1304 C Programming, COSC 1305 C++ Programming, COSC 1410 Introduction to Computer Science 1, COSC 1437 Introduction to Programming, ENGI 1331 Computing for Engineers, or other programming course approved by instructor.
Description
Principles of photography and image measurement techniques; fundamentals of image coordinates to ground coordinates; digital photography and mapping, orthophotography, structure from motion.
