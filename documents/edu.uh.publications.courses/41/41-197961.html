POLS 3352 - Immigration: Politics and Policy in the U.S.

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2306 and GOVT 2305.
Description
Provides a general understanding of the importance of immigration as a national interest. Discusses the theories, institutions, and actors that shape policy in the U.S. Also, addresses historical and contemporary debates on policy and changing public opinion.
