KIN 4340 - Sport Governance

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: junior standing, KIN 3360 , and KIN 4355 .
Description
Identifying governing bodies in amateur/professional sports, their organizational structure, authority, requirements for membership, sanctions and appeals, and the influence of outside political organizations.
