SOC 3362 - Sociology of Religion in India

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Sociological approaches to the study of religion in Indian society. Spiritual thought in Hindu society, native belief systems and imported religions and their consequences for socio-political integration.
