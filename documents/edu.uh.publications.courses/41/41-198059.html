MECT 3366 - Lean Manufacturing

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECT 1364.
Description
Principles and practices of manufacturing methodologies based on maximizing value and minimizing waste in the manufacturing processes.
