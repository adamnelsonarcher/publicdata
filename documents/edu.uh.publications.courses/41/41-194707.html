ENGL 4385 - Fiction Forms

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Admission to the Creative Writing Concentration and 3 hours in 3000-/or 4000-level courses in fiction.
Description
Analysis and writing of fictional narrative with focus on craft (characters, dialogue, plot, setting, and point of view). Traditional and contemporary examples.
May be repeated once for credit.
