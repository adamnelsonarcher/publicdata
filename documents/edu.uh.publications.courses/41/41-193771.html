BIOE 5323 - Introduction to Regenerative Medicine and Stem Cell Engineering

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly BIOE 5323 Fundamentals of Tissue Engineering
Prerequisite: BIOE 3340 and MATH 3321 , or consent of instructor.
Description
Fundamental principles as applied to tissue and organ fabrication, including cell sourcing, biomaterial synthesis, tissue fabrication technology, bioreactor design and vascularization.
