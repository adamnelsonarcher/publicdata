POLS 3362 - Political Campaign Management

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Description
A nuts-and-bolts of how to begin and manage a political campaign throughout and up until election day. The applied elements will include real world examples and experiences.
