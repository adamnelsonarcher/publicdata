SPAN 3308 - Written Communication for Hispanic Heritage Learners

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SPAN 2308 or placement test. Credit for both SPAN 3302 and SPAN 3308 may not apply to a degree.
Description
Designed for students from a Spanish-speaking background. Intensive practice in the writing of Spanish in formal and informal styles.
