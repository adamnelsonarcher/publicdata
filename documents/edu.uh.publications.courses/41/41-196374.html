PSYC 4498 - Independent Study

Credit Hours: 4
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301, PSYC 2305, PSYC 3301 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Independent student projects supervised by a faculty member. Forty-five hours of work on the project are expected for each semester hour of credit.
May be repeated, but no more than six semester hours may count toward psychology major 30-hour requirement.

Additional Fee Y
