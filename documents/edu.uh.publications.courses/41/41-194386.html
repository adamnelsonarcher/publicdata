ECON 3332 - Intermediate Microeconomics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ECON 2302 or Math major with sophomore standing in the Option in Mathematical Finance program.
Description
Rigorous introduction to resource allocation in a market economy including consumer behavior, firm behavior, supply and demand, efficiency and the determination of prices.
