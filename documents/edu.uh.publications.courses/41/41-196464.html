SOC 3350 - Sociology of the Body

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOCI 1301 or consent of instructor.
Description
Theoretical and empirical literature on the social body, including social constructivist interpretations and research on body work, deviance, resistance, and medicalization.
