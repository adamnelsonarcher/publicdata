DIGM 4379 - Transmedia Marketing

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: Grade of C or higher in DIGM 2357, DIGM 3351, and DIGM 3354.
Description
The technology, production, and management of creative story-telling authored across multiple media distribution platforms.
