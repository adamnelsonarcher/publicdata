MUSI 4379 - History of Jazz II

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUSI 3364 .
Description
Survey of performance practices after 1940 including Bebop, Cool, Hard Bop, Post-Bop, the Avant-garde, and Jazz-Rock Fusion.
