COMM 4302 - Latinx Media Studies

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COMM 2320 and COMM 3376, or consent of instructor.
Description
A senior-level intensive writing course that explores Latinx media systems via six main themes: production, circulation, distribution, reception, representations and effects.
