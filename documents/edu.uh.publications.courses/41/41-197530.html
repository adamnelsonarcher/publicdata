ECON 3342 - Global Issues in Economic Development

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ECON 2302 or consent of instructor.
Description
Comparative study of populations that have experienced a dramatic transformation and improvement in the conditions of their life and those that have not; lectures and reading materials to facilitate a better understanding of the world, its politics, its people, the problems facing the global environment and the changing global economy.
