PHAR 5582 - Therapeutics III

Credit Hours: 5.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 5 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHAR 5581

A continuation of Therapeutics II. Requires integration and application of pharmacological and biopharmaceutical principals to the treatment of specific disease states through clinical pharmacy practice and therapeutics.
