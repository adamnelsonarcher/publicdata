ELCS 8313 - Critical Issues for Urban Education Administration, Leadership, & Policy

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

For school administrators, central office leaders, and aspiring researchers to extend their knowledge and understanding surrounding a series of issues challenging public schools and districts in urban settings. This course will analyze policies addressing education reform, organizational improvement, and education policy and politics as well as discuss other important concerns related to multiple urban student populations.
