MATH 7326 - Dynamical Systems

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 6320 or consent of instructor.

Ergodic theory, topological and symbolic dynamics, statistical properties, infinite-dimensional dynamical systems, random dynamical systems, and themodynamic formalism.
