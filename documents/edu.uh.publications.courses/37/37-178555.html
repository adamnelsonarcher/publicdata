CUIN 7305 - Design, Development, & Evaluation of Presentations

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: none.

Conceptual framework for understanding presentation design as well as opportunities for hands-on experiences in designing, developing, and evaluating educational presentations using a variety of strategies and tools.
