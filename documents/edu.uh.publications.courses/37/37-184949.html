ELET 6356 - Health Analytics and Visualization

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

Covers fundamentals of communication and concepts for effective data presentation, principles of visual design, with specific emphasis on interpretation and story telling. Software tools such as Tableau and R are used for data visualization.
