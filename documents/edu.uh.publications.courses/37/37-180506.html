PHCA 8301 - Behavioral Theories and Models in Pharmacy and Health

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHCA 7305 and PHCA 7306 , graduate standing, or consent of instructor.

Discuss and appraise models and theories in pharmacy administration and management research focusing on social and behavioral aspects of pharmaceutical care.
