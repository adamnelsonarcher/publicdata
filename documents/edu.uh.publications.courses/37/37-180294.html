PCEU 7355 - Regulatory Affairs

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing or consent of instructor.

Regulatory affairs in clinical pharmacokinetic evaluation and FDA drug approval; clinical trial regulation, good laboratory/manufacturing practice, quality control/assurance, new drug applications and evaluating contract analytical labs.
