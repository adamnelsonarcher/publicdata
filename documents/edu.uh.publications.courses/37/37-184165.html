BZAN 6356 - Database Management Tools for Business Analytics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and completion of BZAN 6354 .

Course covers tools for data repositories such as SQL, NoSQL, Hadoop, MapReduce, Pig, and Hive.
