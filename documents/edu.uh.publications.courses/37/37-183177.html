IDNS 6392 - History of 20th Century Science

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and credit for or registration in 12 hours of natural science courses

This course analyzes central issues in the evolution of science during the past century.
