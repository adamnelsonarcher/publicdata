PHLS 6314 - Helping Relationships

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

This course is designed to deepen students understanding of counseling relationships. Counselors help people in a variety of ways and utilize many techniques and processes to do so. This course will cover many of the common aspects of counseling that will be useful to students as they prepare to enter the workforce.
