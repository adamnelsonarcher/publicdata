LAW 6360 - Trial Advocacy for Non-Litigators

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: N/A.

Trial Advocacy for Non-Litigators provides students who do not have any trial experience with a chance to gain basic litigation training. This class is ideal for students interested in transactional law who may encounter litigators in the course of their career.
Note: Course may be repeated with permission from the Law Center Office of Admissions.
