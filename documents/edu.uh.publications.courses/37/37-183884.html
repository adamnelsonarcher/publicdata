ACCT 7337 - Oil & Gas Taxation

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing.

This course examines the fundamental property concepts governing oil and gas taxation. Topics include geological and geophysical costs, intangible drilling costs, equipment costs, dry hole costs, and abandonment.
