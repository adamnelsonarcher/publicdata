ARLD 6370 - Introduction to Museum and Gallery Management

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Consent of instructor.

This course provides an overview of nonprofit museums and for-profit galleries and the people who create, govern, and operate them. Students will learn about the different types of exhibition spaces and their structures. Students will explore the theory and practice of museum and gallery management as well as the current and emerging trends in the field.
