PHAR 5269 - Complex Problems

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Third year standing in the curriculum.

Corequisite: PHAR 5169.

This course will incorporate knowledge and skills gained from foundational, clinical, and administrative/ behavioral sciences to promote application in solving complex problems in pharmacy and healthcare.
