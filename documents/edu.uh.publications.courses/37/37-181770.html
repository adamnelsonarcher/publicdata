COSC 6372 - Computer Graphics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COSC 4370 or consent of instructor.

In-depth study of selected areas in computer graphics such as 3D modeling, hidden surfaces, surface reconstruction, shading and texturing, computer animation, and other recent developments in computer graphics.
