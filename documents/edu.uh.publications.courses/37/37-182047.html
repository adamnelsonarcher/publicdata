CIS 6357 - Control Systems Security

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: CIS 6321

Application of security principles to industrial control systems and their networks. Hands-on laboratory exercised with SCADA devices.
