BTEC 6304 - Computational Methods in BTEC

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing or consent of instructor.

Application of computers to solve problems in biology and medicine. Statistical analysis and inference (experimental design and analysis), biological modeling and simulation, and biological imaging.
