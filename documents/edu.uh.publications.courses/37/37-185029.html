SOCW 7312 - Advanced Clinical Practice: Suicide Assessment and Treatment

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Successful completion of Foundation.

Provides theoretical content and empirically supported knowledge to develop evidence-based clinical skills in the risk identification, assessment, and treatment of suicide behavior across the life spectrum and within the larger social, racial, economic environment
