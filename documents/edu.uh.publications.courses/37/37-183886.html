ACCT 7387 - Oil & Gas Accounting 2

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and ACCT 7386 .

Oil & Gas Accounting 2 builds on knowledge from Oil & Gas Accounting 1. The course covers financial accounting and auditing issues in the upstream, midstream, downstream, and oilfield services. Subject-matter experts and professionals provide in-depth lectures and discussions about current events in the energy sector.
