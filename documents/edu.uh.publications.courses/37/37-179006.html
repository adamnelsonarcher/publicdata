PETR 6350 - Natural Gas Engineering

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 6328 , 6351, 6362 or BS in Petroleum Engineering and PETR Graduate Standing or Consent of Program.

Focuses on natural gas engineering, the situation today and the technologies of tomorrow. Covering the supply of natural gas, including exploration, production, unconventional resources, transportation, processing, conversation and fuel cells.
