CIS 6395 - Information Systems Security Integration Project

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CIS 6391 and completion or concurrent enrollment CIS 6324 , TEPM 6302 , TEPM 6303 and TEPM 6304 .

Students demonstrate their ability to structure and complete an integrative project that draws upon the skills developed in the project management common core courses and the students specialization. Students report the results of their efforts in written and oral form.
