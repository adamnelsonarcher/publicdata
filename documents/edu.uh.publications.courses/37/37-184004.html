SOCW 7376 - Social Work with LGBTQ Communities

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Successful completion of MSW foundation.

This course outlines knowledge and specific skill sets related to effective social work practice with individuals who may identify as Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender or Queer (LGBTQ). Knowledge for social work with Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender or Queer (LGBTQ) communities.
