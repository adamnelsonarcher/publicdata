COSC 7384 - Advanced Real-Time Systems

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COSC 6384 or consent of instructor.

Advanced study of the current research in the theory and techniques for the design and development of intelligent, highly fault-tolerant, and distributed real-time systems.
