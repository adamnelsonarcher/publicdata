IART 6070 - Interdisciplinary Fieldwork

Credit Hours: 0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: IART 6370 .

This is the laboratory component of the Interdisciplinary Masterclass. Requires approval of Director of Interdisciplinary Initiatives. This component takes place off campus and is intended for intensive short-term research in the field.
Note: Fieldwork associated with IART 6370 .
