CIS 6324 - Information Sys Sec Risk

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CIS 6322 or CIS 6323 or consent of graduate faculty advisor.

This course focuses on the organizational issues of risk analysis in the legal context of the Internet. Organizational problems involving reliability, safety, security, privacy, and human well-being are addressed.
