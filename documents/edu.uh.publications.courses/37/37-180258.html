PHOP 6152 - Basic Physiological Optics and Visual Sciences: Laboratory

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Formerly/Same as: PHOP 6152 - Basic Visual Processes Lab.
Prerequisite: Bachelors degree or equivalent.

Corequisite: Corequisite(s): PHOP 6241 and 6242.

Laboratory demonstrations and experiments on the anatomy, biochemistry, molecular biology and physiology of the eye, retina, and visual pathways, and fundamental sensory and motor aspects of monocular and/or binocular vision.
