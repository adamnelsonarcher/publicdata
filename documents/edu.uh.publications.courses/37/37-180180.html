OPTO 5233 - Adv Human Anatomy and Hist

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: concurrent enrollment in OPTO 5133 .

Advanced human anatomy for eye-care professionals and vision scientists with emphasis on the gross anatomy of the head and neck and the histology of human tissues and organ systems.
