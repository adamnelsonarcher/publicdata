PHAR 5457 - Institutional Introductory Pharmacy Practice Experience

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 4
Prerequisite: Successful completion of all second year courses in the professional curriculum.

The institutional Introductory Pharmacy Practice Experience is a 4 week experience for students to apply and reinforce knowledge, skills and attitudes in a direct patient care institutional setting.
