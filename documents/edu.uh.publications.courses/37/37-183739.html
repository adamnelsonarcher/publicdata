NURS 6351 - Evidence-Based Practice Project

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: NURS 6301 , NURS 6332 , Capstone course final semester of MSN program. Student must have a 3.0 GPA in MSN program to take this course.

Synthesizes knowledge from the core and track specific courses in the development of an evidence-based project based on a practice concern identified. Students prepare the project for implementation, publication, grant submission, or professional presentation.
