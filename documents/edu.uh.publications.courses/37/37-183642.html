HRMA 7334 - Pricing and Revenue Management in Hospitality

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: HRMA 6334 Pricing & Revenue Management
Prerequisite: None
