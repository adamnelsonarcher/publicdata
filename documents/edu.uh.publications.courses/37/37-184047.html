LAW 6330 - Real Estate Transactions, Practical Drafting

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: N/A.

This is a “nuts & bolts” practical course that will consider the basic building blocks of real estate transactions, a survey of a wide range of various common real estate transaction, and related matters.
Note: Course may be repeated with permission from the Law Center Office of Admissions.
