CUIN 8381 - Research Methods

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Admission to Ph.D. or Ed.D.

This course is a doctoral seminar in which students investigate various psychological theories as they relate to educational curriculum and practice.
