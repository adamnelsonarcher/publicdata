CUIN 7381 - Instructional Evaluation

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: CUIN 6378 Instructional Eval
Prerequisite: NONE.

Development of understanding and skills for evaluating student progress, student achievement, and instructional effectiveness in the classroom setting. Emphasis on collecting information, former judgments, and making instructional decisions.
