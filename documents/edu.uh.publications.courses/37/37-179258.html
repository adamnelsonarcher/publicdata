MECE 6320 - Composite Materials

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Fiber, whisker and particulate reinforcements; polymer-, metal- and ceramic composite materials; principles of second-phase stiffening, strengthening and toughening; interface/interphase stress transfer and load partition; composite material constitutive equations; composite laminate beam and plate analyses; failure theories and criteria.
