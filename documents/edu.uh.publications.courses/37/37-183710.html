SOCW 8424 - Statistics and Data Analysis I

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: Doctoral standing or permission from the instructor.

Explores the application of descriptive and inferential statistics in social and behavioral sciences research via a weekly 3-hour lecture and 1-hour computer lab.
