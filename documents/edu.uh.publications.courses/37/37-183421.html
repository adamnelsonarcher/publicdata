PETR 6336 - Petroleum Energy Markets

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 6351 , 6364 or BS in Petroleum Engineering and PETR Graduate Standing or Consent of Program.

Analysis of the past, present and future Energy Markets combining a business-historical perspective with an engineering-science evaluation.
