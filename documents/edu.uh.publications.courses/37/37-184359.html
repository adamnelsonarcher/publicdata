SOCW 8695 - Pre-Dissertation Research

Credit Hours: 6
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Doctoral standing in social work and successful completion of all required coursework.

This course provides opportunities to continue preparation for qualifying paper, the identification of a dissertation topic and methodology, and/or the dissertation proposal.
