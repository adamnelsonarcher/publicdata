CUIN 6358 - Perspectives of Museum Education

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: none.

Survey of cultural, historical and theoretical contexts of museums. Exploration of museum education practices, critical analysis of museum education and community engagement, and current museum issues impacting education.
