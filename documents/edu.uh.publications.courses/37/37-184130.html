BIOE 6310 - Drug Design and Delivery

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing or consent of instructor.

Understanding the fundamentals of drug delivery, including physiology, pharmacokinetics/pharmacodynamics, drug diffusion and permeation, control release, distribution and biomaterials used in drug fabrications.
