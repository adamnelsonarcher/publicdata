COSC 6396 - Internship in Computer Science

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: 18 hours of graduate coursework and consent of the department.

To include real world experience complementing the academic education. Internship is initiated by the student, approved and monitored by graduate director. Requires a written report submitted by the student after internship.
