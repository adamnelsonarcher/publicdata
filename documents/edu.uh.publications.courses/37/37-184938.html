ECE 6374 - State-Space Estimation with Physiological Applications

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Probability theory, Signal processing, and MATLAB.

State-space modeling, state-space estimation, Kalman filtering, theory of point processes, estimation of point processes, maximum likelihood estimation, expectation maximization, point process filtering and smoothing, and sparse signal processing.
