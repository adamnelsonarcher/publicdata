BIOL 6384 - Developmental Biology

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BIOL 3301,Graduate standing

Cellular differentiation, growth, and morphogenesis of developing biological systems. Students must complete a written assignment on aspects of the course content.
