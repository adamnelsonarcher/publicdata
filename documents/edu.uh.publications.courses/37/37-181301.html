SOCW 8323 - Research Methods III: Qualitative Research for Evidence-Based Social Work

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Doctoral standing or permisson from instructor.

Examines the range of methods involved in formulating and conducting qualitative social research, with an emphasis on building empirical knowledge for evidence -based social work.
