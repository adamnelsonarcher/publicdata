COSC 6349 - Intermediate Game Art and Animation

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COSC 6348 or consent of instructor.

Advanced video game concept arts and computer animation. Covers advanced topics of modeling game assets and animation, using the latest game software and hardware.
