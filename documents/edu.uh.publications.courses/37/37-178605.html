CUIN 7376 - New Tools for Creating Online Educational Materials

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: CUIN 7376 - Design of Online Educational Resources II.
Prerequisite: None.

Focuses on the exploration, use and evaluation of a range of web-based tools and programs that may be used to support instruction.
