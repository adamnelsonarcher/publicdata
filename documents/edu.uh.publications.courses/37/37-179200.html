INDE 6323 - Economics of Disaster

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: graduate standing and/or consent of instructor.

Advanced tools necessary to evaluate, measure, and compare contributions of economic principles to disaster research, mitigation, and planning: Deterministic and probabilistic methods in developing objective and sound engineering decisions.
