MECE 6365 - Semiconductor Materials and Photonic and Electronic Devices

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

Concepts of semiconductor materials including electronic band structures, generation, recombination, and transfer of free carriers, p-n and metal-semiconductor junctions. Operating principles of semiconductor devices for photonic, electronic, and energy applications.
