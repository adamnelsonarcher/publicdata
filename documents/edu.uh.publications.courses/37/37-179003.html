PETR 6330 - Fundamental of Hydraulic Fracturing

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0

Formerly/Same as:


Prerequisite: PETR 6351 , 6362, 6364, or BS in PETR. PETR 6314 , 6368 and PETR Graduate Standing or Consent of Program.

Reasons for fracturing, fundamentals of fracture initiation and extension, fracture geometry design, proppant transport, and materials and techniques used for industrial fracturing treatments.
