NURS 6230 - Diagnostic Tests & Procedures

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: NURS 6330

Advanced Diagnostic Physical ExaminationPrepares students for analysis and interpretation of diagnostic data in development of appropriate differential diagnosis. Cost, invasiveness, acceptability, and efficacy of appropriate diagnostic and therapeutic interventions emphasized.
