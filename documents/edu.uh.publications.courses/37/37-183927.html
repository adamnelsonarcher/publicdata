GEOL 6326 - Remote Sensing for Atmospheric Sciences

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: PHYS 1322 and GEOL 3378 , or PHYS 1322 and GEOL 3342 OR equivalent.

Remote sensing techniques, their physical principles and applications in atmospheric sciences. Analysis of meteorological variables, atmospheric trace gases, and surface properties.
