PHAR 5214 - Oncology Pharmacotherapy

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Third-year standing in the College of Pharmacy or consent of the instructor.

This course details the pharmacology and therapeutics of antineoplastic agents, mechanisms of action, toxicities, mechanisms of resistance and general principles that guide the monitoring of patients receiving chemotherapy.
