PHAR 5371 - Ambulatory Clinical Practice Management

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Third year standing in the curriculum.

The goal of this course is to provide an overview of Class A pharmacy operations, provide exposure to management principles common to all areas of pharmacy practice and describe acute and ambulatory direct patient care nuances.
