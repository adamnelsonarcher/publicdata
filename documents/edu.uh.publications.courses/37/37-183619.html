BIOL 6223 - Animal Models of Human Disease

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Consent of instructor.

Principles and application of animal models to study pathogenesis of human disease and to develop therapeutics with emphasis on cancer.
