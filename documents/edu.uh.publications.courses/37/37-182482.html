PHAR 5155 - Pharmacy Skills Program IV

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 4
Prerequisite: PHAR 5254 IPPE I and Professional Development

Exercises in specialized dosage form compounding, dispensing functions to optimize patient care, patient counseling and drug information retrieval/literature evaluation; problem solving integrating clinical physical assessment with clinical and basic pharmaceutical sciences.
