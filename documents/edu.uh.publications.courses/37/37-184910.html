CIVE 6373 - Experimental Methods in Environmental Engineering

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: Students are expected to have prior experience with Excel spreadsheets and Word processors.

General chemistry knowledge is also expected. This course will provide practical training on experimental methods in environmental engineering. Methods covered will include experiment planning, sample preparation, measurement using analytical instruments, and data analysis and reporting. Significant focus will be placed throughout the course on quality assurance, namely following best practices to acquire the best quality data possible and navigating the various approaches to make measurements on complex environmental samples.
