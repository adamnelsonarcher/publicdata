ELCS 7371 - Higher Educ Law

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ELCS 7330 or consent of instructor.

Analysis of legal issues in postsecondary education, including governance, faculty, students, and regulations by state and federal governments.
