PETR 7304 - Advanced Numerical Analysis

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 7302 , PETR PhD Graduate standing and/or consent of both the instructor and the Director of Petroleum Engineering Graduate Admissions

Techniques in mathematical modeling, including partial differential equations, finite-difference and finite element methods for boundary-value problems, initial-value problems, and direct and iterative methods for solving a linear algebraic systems.
