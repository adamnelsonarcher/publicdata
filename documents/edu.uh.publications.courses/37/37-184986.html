LAW 6376 - Intellectual Property Survey

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

This course covers domestic intellectual property laws - patent, copyright, trademark, and trade secret - through statues and cases. The course will provide roughly equal treatment of patent, copyright and trademark law, approximately four weeks for each, with the remainder applied to the law of trade secrets, introduction, and/or review.
