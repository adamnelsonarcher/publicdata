PHAR 5213 - Pharmacy Base Immunizatn Deliv

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Third year standing in the College of Pharmacy or consent of instructor.

Pharmacy based immunization delivery is an innovative and interactive training program that teaches pharmacy students the skills necessary to become a primary source for vaccine information and administration. Students taking the elective learn the basics of immunology and vaccine administration and focus on practice implementation and legal/regulatory issues.
