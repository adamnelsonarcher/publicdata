OPTO 6153 - Vision Therapy Elective

Credit Hours: 1.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 0.0 Lab Contact Hours: 4.0
Prerequisite: Permission of instructor.

Managing patients referred for vision therapy, learning new binocular vision tests, training strategies, prognosis for success, and performing in-office vision therapy.
