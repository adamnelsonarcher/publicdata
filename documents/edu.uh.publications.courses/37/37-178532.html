CUIN 6375 - Classroom Mgt

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CUIN 6372 or consent of instructor.

Analysis of the sociopsychological dynamics of classroom groups with emphasis on implications of behavior modification, socioemotional, and group process theories of classroom management.
