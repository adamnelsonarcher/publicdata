CUIN 7331 - Diagnosis and Correction of Reading Problems

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: none.

Procedures for diagnosing, assessing, monitoring and correcting reading problems; methods and materials useful in addressing reading problems. This course will include a practicum experience.
