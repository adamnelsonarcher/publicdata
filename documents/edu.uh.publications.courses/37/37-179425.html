LAW 5111 - Advocates-Blakely Moot Court

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Students seeking credit for this course must participate in the Blakely Moot Court Competition. The Blakely Competition is an intra-scholastic moot court competition.
