ELCS 6304 - Law & Policy for School Leaders

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

Sources of educational policy development, legal authority, liability, and legal principles involved in operating school systems will be studied. Course cases, current policy issues, and legal controversies will be referenced.
