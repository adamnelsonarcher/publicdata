LAW 6243 - Privacy and Data Protection

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

Deals with areas where privacy law conflicts with the media, & with law enforcement, & with the privacy accorded government records. Encompasses privacy of financial data, and the relationship between privacy & place.
