CUIN 8369 - Leadership in a Complex World

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Doctoral status.

Comprehensive account of the seminal works, contemporary theories and models, and emerging perspectives of leadership. Cross-disciplinary and integrative views of the leadership phenomenon are adopted to highlight how different disciplines inform leadership study and to illustrate various methodologies that are used for understanding and assessing the concept of leadership.
