CUIN 8386 - Advanced Issues in Qualitative Research

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
This research course focuses on issues qualitative researchers encounter in the literature and in the field. Fine-grained differences between different qualitative research approaches and different research paradigms will be discussed.
