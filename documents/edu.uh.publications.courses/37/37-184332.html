PHAR 5228 - Integrated Men’s and Women’s Health Module

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Second year standing in the professional program.

Corequisite: PHAR 5259 .

This course will help integrate the pathophysiology, medicinal chemistry, pharmacology, toxicology, and therapeutics in the clinical management of common diseases related to men and women’s health.
