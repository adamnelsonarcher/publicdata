PHAR 4160 - Fundamentals of Community Pharmacy Practice

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: First year standing in College of Pharmacy.

Corequisite: PHAR 4251

To prepare students with the foundational knowledge needed to provide typical patient care activities in a Class A pharmacy while complying with applicable state and federal rules and laws.
