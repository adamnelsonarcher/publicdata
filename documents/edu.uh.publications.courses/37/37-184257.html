ECE 6011 - Colloquium

Credit Hours: 0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing.

The ECE 6011 Colloquium is a research seminar event that is offered by the various research groups within the Department of ECE. Meetings are normally held once a week for a one-hour period. Each research group organizes the format of the Colloquium to best suit the needs of their group. During the Colloquium students typically make presentations about their research work and engage in discussions about their research with other students and faculty that are present. Guest speakers may also give presentations at the Colloquium.
