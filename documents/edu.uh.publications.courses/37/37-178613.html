CUIN 7389 - Digital Media

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Basic computer literacy.

Educational applications of digital graphics, animation, multimedia and video, including delivery online; to support projects that merge multiple media into teaching and learning.
