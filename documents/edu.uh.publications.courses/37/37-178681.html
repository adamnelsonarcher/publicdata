ELCS 6370 - Research for Educational Leaders

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SPEC 6327 or corequisite, and admission to the graduate program in Educational Leadership or approval of chair.
