PETR 6310 - Petroleum Production Economics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 6351 , 6362, 6364 or BS in Petroleum Engineering and PETR Graduate Standing or Consent of Program.

Exploring techniques used to find, produce, and process energy discoveries. Ownership, operational decision making, finding and lifting costs, risk assessment and mitigation, reserve accounting, financing, and unconventional hydrocarbons.
