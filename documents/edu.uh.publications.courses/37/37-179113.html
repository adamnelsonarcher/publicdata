ECE 6331 - Advanced Telecommunications

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ECE 4371 and ECE 6337 or consent of instructor.

Random signals and wide, baseband data transmission, error analysis of additive white Gaussian noise channels, intersymbol interference, multipath interference, fading, signal-space concepts, and optimal receiver design.
