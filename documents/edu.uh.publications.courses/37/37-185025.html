SCM 7325 - Process Analysis and Design

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and SCM 6A01 or equivalent.

Concepts, issues and techniques used to plan, analyze, design and control processes in production, service and logistics systems. Sample topics include process types, process analysis techniques, quality management, lean processes, service lines and sustainability.
