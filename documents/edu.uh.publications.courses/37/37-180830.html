CIS 6358 - Secure Software Design

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CIS 6321 .

Examines the management of software engineering process with an emphasis on security, including common bugs and tools to prevent them in a secure development process.
