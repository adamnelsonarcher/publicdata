PHCA 8303 - Multivariate Analysis in Pharmaceutical Health Outcomes and Policy

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHCA 7308 and 7301 or consent of instructor.

Advanced statistical techniques for handling multiple dependent and independent measures in pharmaceutical health outcomes research such as factor analysis, multiple discriminant analysis, MANOVA, cluster analysis, canonical correlation, and structural equation modeling.
