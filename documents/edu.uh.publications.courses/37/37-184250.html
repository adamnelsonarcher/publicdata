ELCS 8312 - Laboratory of Practice - Research Methods Development

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: Successful completion of first four semesters of coursework.

This course develops applied knowledge and application of educational research terminology, perspectives that support the development of unique dissertation chapter containing the methodology/methods sections.
