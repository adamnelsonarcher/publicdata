SPAN 1507 - Intensive Elementary Spanish for Heritage Learners

Credit Hours: 5.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: Placement by exam or by Director of Basic Language Program.
Description
Designed for students from a Spanish-speaking background. Literacy development in Spanish with emphasis on vocabulary. Intensive reading, writing, and speaking.
