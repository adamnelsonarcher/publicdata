KIN 4190 - Sport Administration Seminar

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Sport Admin Major, KIN3360, and completion of 75 credit-hours.
Description
To be taken the semester before internship. Analysis of management, organizational structure, and strategic planning in sport organizations; current issues in sport management; career planning; internship placement. .
