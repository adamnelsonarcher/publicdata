MUSI 4366 - Music in the United States

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUSI 3300 or MUSI 3364 or consent of instructor.
Description
A social, historical, and stylistic survey of American music from the Pilgrims to the present, including popular music in all periods.
