JWST 3386 - Jewish Mysticism

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1303
Description
This course surveys Jewish mystical ideas, beliefs and experiences, emphasizing five different types of Jewish mysticism as well as a chronological view of its development.
