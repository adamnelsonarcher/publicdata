MUED 3108 - Piano for Choral Directors

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Formerly MUSI 3108.
Prerequisite: Pass piano proficiency exam or consent of instructor.
Description
May not be used as an advanced music elective. Develop piano skills for the choral ensemble including accompanying, transposition, and score reading and reduction.
Additional Fee Y
