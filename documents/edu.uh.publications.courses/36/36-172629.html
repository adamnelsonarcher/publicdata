MUSI 4122 - Electronic Composition

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: composition majors studying at the 3000-level or by consent of Composition Area Coordinator.
Description
Basic composition techniques in electronic music; an historic overview and study of significant works of electronic music.
