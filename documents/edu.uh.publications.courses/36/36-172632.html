MUSI 4220 - Choral Conducting I

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUSI 2214 and MUSI 2171 .
Description
Fundamental aspects of gesture in choral conducting, particularly physiology, preparation, the proper execution of beat patterns, cuing, and releases. Introduces rudimentary score marking and score study.
