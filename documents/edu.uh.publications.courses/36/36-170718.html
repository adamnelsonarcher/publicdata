COMM 4371 - American Cinema: The Directors

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: COMM 1301 .
Description
Examination of the role of the director in the conceptualization and creation of a film. Students will look at different directing styles.
The director studied will vary from semester to semester. Therefore, students are permitted multiple enrollments for credit. A maximum of three total enrollments of the course is permitted.
