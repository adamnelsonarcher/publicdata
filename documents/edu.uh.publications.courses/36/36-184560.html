CEA 4335 - Capstone Design I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: CEA 3302, ECON 2304, INDE 2333.
Description
Propose and begin team projects involving open-ended design problems supplied by industry and faculty. Professionalism, research methodologies, design tools, and technical communication. The primary deliverable at the end of the semester is a prototype, the nature of which will be dependent on the project type.
