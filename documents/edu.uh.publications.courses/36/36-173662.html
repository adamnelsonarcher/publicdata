DAN 3312 - STOTT PILATES: Intensive Mat Certification

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Anatomy, Pedagogy II (or equivalent) or consent of instructor.
Description
Preparation for certification in STOTT PILATES Level I Matwork repertoire. Topics include postural analysis, self-practice, teaching, and observation of the STOTT PILATES method.
Typically Offered: Spring
