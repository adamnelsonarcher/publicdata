NURS 4216 - Perioperative Nursing Clinical Elective

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: NURS 3631 .
Description
Focuses on the role of the nurse in the Perioperative setting. Emphasizes aspects of communication, asepsis, and patient safety within the Perioperative setting. Designed to be an elective course for and limited to undergraduate baccalaureate nursing students.
