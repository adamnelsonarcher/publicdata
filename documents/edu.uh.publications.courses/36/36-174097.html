BIOE 4302 - Numerical Analysis for Biomedical Engineering

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BIOE 2100 and MATH 2433 . Credit for or concurrent enrollment in BIOE 3340 and BIOE 3140
Description
Numerical algorithms and skills to implement algorithms for solving mathematical problems in biomedical engineering.
