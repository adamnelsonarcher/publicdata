SYSE 4334 - Systems Engineering Capstone Design

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: INDE 3333, SYSE 3340, SYSE 3355.
Description
This course requires students to apply the knowledge and techniques gained from the systems engineering curriculum to address and solve open-ended problem(s) supplied by project sponsors. The capstone design project represents a substantive application and evaluation of coursework covered in the systems engineering program.
