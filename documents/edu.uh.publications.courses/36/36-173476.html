BUSI 3302 - Connecting Bauer to Business

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Completion of ENGL 1303 or alternative; good academic standing; and a pre-business major or declared business major.
Description
Overview of the business programs in Bauer College of Business and the connection to career paths. Development of business protocol and communication skills.
