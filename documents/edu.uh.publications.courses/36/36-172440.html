MECE 2336 - Mechanics I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHYS 1321 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in MATH 2433 .
Description
Fundamentals of vector mechanics applied to systems of forces, resultants, free body diagrams, equilibrium and analysis of frames, machines and other structures. Centroids of areas, center of mass and moments of inertia.
TCCNS Equivalent: ENGR 2301
