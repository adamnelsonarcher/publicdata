MATH 3335 - Vector Analysis

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 2433 .
Description
Algebra and calculus of vectors, vector differential operators, Green’s and Stokes’ theorems, curvilinear coordinates, tensors.
