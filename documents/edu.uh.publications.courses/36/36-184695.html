SOC 3314 - Juvenille Delinquency

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOC 1300.
Description
A look into the extent and causes of unlawful behavior among minors and the system of social control developed to manage it.
