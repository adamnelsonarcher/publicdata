MANA 4341 - Leading Organizational Change

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MANA 3335 ; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Processes of planned organizational change at the individual level with emphasis on design, intervention methods, and evaluation of change programs.
