MATH 1310 - College Algebra

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Description
Quadratic equations, inequalities, logarithmic and exponential functions, graphs, elements of theory of equations, systems of equations.
Note: This course is designed to prepare students for MATH 1330 Pre-calculus and MATH 1431 Calculus I. Students with prior credit in MATH 1330 or MATH 1431 will not be allowed to enroll or receive credit in MATH 1310.
Core Category: [20] Mathematics
TCCNS Equivalent: MATH 1314
