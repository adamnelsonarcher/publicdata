BIOL 4340 - Research Methods

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CUIN 3351 and one of CHEM 1111 , CHEM 1117 ; BIOL 1161 , BIOL 1162 ; PHYS 1101 , PHYS 1121 or equivalent, or consent of instructor.
Description
Students perform four independent inquiries, combing skills from mathematics and science to solve research problems. Credit may not be received for BIOL 4340 and CHEM 4340 or PHYS 4340 .
