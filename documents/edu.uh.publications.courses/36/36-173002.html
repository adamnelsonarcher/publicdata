PSYC 4360 - Child Abuse:Interdisc Perspect

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 1300 , PSYC 2301 , PSYC 3301 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Dynamics of child maltreatment, consequences of abuse for victims and society, and the impact and limitations of current interventions and public policy.
Additional Fee Y
