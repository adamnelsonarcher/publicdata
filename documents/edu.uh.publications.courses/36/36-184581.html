CNE 3333 - Building Information Modeling

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: CNE 3310.
Description
Principles, theory, and techniques of building information modeling applications in the construction industry; variants of Building Information Modeling; standards.
