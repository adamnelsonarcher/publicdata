PHYS 4370 - Nanophysics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHYS 1322 Univ Phys II.
Description
Theory and principles of a broad range of characterization methods ranging from proximal probes to optical techniques used to study the world at the nanoscale level.
