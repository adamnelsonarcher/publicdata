INDS 4500 - Industrial Design Studio VII

Credit Hours: 5.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 9
Prerequisite: INDS 3501 .
Description
Systematic process of generating and solving a design problem. Emphasizes interdisciplinary learning through complex design problems in selected industries.
