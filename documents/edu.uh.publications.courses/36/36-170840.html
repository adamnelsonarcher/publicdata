COSC 3371 - Cybersecurity

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly COSC 4371.
Prerequisite: COSC 3360 .
Description
Introduction to cybersecurity: theoretical foundations of security, cryptographic algorithms and protocols, widely used security protocols (such as SSL), and practical attack and defense techniques, including software vulnerability exploits and firewalls
Additional Fee Y
