HIST 1379 - The United States Since 1877

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Description
Satisfies half of the state requirement in American history. The social, economic, and political history of the United States since 1877.
Note: Taught in large lecture and small discussion group format.
