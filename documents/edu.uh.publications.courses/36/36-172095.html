INDE 2333 - Engineering Statistics I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 1432 ;
Description
ENGR majors must also have credit for CHEE 1331 , CIVE 1331   , ECE 1331   , INDE 1331 , MECE 1331   or equivalent and permission of chair. Probability and statistical inference for engineering applications; probability distributions, estimation, statistical tests, and reliability theory.
