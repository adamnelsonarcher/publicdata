DIGM 4376 - Integrated Media

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: DIGM 2351 , 2353, 3354 and 3357 ( DIGM 3354 waived for Computer Graphics Minor).
Description
Developing personal and corporate brands that integrate print- and web-design, computer graphics, photographs, videos, social- and mobile-media.
Additional Fee Y
