COMM 4337 - Television and the Familiy

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Junior standing, COMM 1301 and COMM 1333 , or consent of instructor.
Description
Television portrayals of the family and the processes and relational consequences associated with family use of television.
