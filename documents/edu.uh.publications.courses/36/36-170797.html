COSC 1300 - Introduction to Computing

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Description
May not be used to satisfy any computer science degree requirement. An introduction to computing with emphasis on the practical usage of personal computers; concepts of recorded programs, computer organization, operating systems, mainframes, minicomputers, and personal computer; selected applications of personal computers; word processing, databases, and spreadsheets; societal impact of computers.
