SPAN 1501 - Elementary Spanish I

Credit Hours: 5.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: Placement Exam within a semester prior to enrollment.
Description
Heritage Learners of Spanish are not eligible to enroll. Basic oral, listening, reading and writing skills in Spanish.
TCCNS Equivalent: SPAN 1511
