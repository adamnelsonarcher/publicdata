CEA 3323 - Data Structures and Algorithms

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CEA 3201.
Description
Data structures, arrays, trees, graphs, stacks and heaps, sorting, efficient hashing and indexing, computational complexity, recursion, sequences, sets and maps, efficient search algorithms, applications.
