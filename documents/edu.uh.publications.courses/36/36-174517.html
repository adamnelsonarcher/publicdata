BIOE 4311 - Advances in Vision Research

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BCHS 3304
Corequisite: BIOE 3340 , BIOE 3140 .
Description
Eye anatomy and development, cellular composition, phototransduction, and diseases that affect the visual system and therapeutic approaches.
