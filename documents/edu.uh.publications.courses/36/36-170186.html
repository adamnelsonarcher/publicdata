ART 3364 - Plastics Fabrication

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: ART 1360 and nine semester hours in foundation program studio courses or consent of instructor.
Description
Exploration of plastics as a medium for sculpture, including mold making, vacuum forming, and casting.
May be repeated for credit with consent of instructor.
