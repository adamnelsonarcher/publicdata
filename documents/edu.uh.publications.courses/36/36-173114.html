SOC 3370 - Popular Music and Society

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOC 1300 or consent of instructor.
Description
Application of sociological theory and methods to the analysis of post- World War II popular music as a social institution with emphasis on its cultural, technological, organizational, political, and mass media contexts.
