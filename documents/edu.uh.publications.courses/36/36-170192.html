ART 3372 - Intermediate Video Art

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: twelve semester hours in fundamental studio courses to include ART 1371 and ART 1372 .
Description
Technical skills in video recording and editing, and development of creative means of expression in this time-based media art form.
May be repeated for credit with major faculty consent.
