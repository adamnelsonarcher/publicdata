MATH 4151 - Mathematics Internship

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH major with junior standing or higher and prior written permission of the Department Chair or designee.
Description
Enhancement of concepts and techniques learned in the classroom through work experience directly related to the mathematics profession.
Note: Employer evaluation, written report, and presentation are required to obtain credit.
