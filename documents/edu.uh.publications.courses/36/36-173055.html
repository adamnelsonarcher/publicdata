RUSS 1501 - Beginning Russian I

Credit Hours: 5.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 5 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Description
Credit for both RUSS 1301 and 1501 may not be applied toward a degree. Instruction in understanding, speaking, reading, and writing Russian, including practice sessions and laboratory drills.
