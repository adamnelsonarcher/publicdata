CHNS 3344 - Modern and Contemporary Global Chinese Literature

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304.
Description
This course introduces students to the canon of Chinese literature, and traces the social-politial and cultural transformation of Chinese societies and the global Chinese diaspora through literature. Taught in English.
