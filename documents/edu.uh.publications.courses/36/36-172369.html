MATH 1314 - Calculus for Business and the Life Sciences

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 1310 or MATH 1311 or MATH 1313 or MATH 2311 or a satisfactory score on a placement examination.
Description
Curve sketching and graphical analysis, differentiation and integration of elementary functions, topics in functions of several variables, applications in business and the natural and social sciences.
Note: Students with prior credit for MATH 1431 will not be permitted to enroll in or receive credit for MATH 1314. May not apply to course or GPA requirements for a major or minor in natural sciences and mathematics.
Core Category: [20] Mathematics and [90] Math/Reasoning
TCCNS Equivalent: MATH 1325
