PSYC 4322 - Behavior Modification

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 1300 , PSYC 2301 , PSYC 3301 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Applied operant behavior theory; operant and respondent paradigms, case studies, and current issues in behavior therapy.
