SPAN 3374 - Spanish American Culture and Civilization

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly SPAN 4374
Prerequisite: SPAN 3301 or SPAN 3307 and SPAN 3302 or SPAN 3308 ; ENGL 1304 .
Description
Survey of the cultural history of Spanish America. Analysis of major cultural, social, literary, and political topics.
Note: Taught in Spanish.
Core Category: [40] Language, Philosophy, & Culture
