IDNS 1147 - Freshman Interest Group

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Description
Designed to create a central-support system for freshmen through connections with peers, staff/faculty and university programs that benefit academic and personal development. May not be used to satisfy degree requirements.
