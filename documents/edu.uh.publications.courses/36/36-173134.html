SPAN 1502 - Elementary Spanish II

Credit Hours: 5.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: SPAN 1501 or equivalent with a minimum grade of C- in twelve months prior or by placement exam.
Description
Students with two years or more of high school Spanish within the past five years or heritage learners of Spanish are not eligible to enroll. Credit for SPAN 1502 may not be applied toward a degree by students who have credit for SPAN 1505 . Continued basic oral, listening, reading, and writing skills.
TCCNS Equivalent: SPAN 1512
