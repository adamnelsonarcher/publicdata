ENGI 2304 - Technical Communications for Engineers

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGI 1100 , ENGL 1304 , engineering undergraduate standing, and completion of one of the following courses: ECE 2201 , CHEE 2331 , CIVE 2330 , INDE 2333 , MECE 2336 , PETR 2311 or permission of the Director of Undergraduate Programs.
Description
Engineering communication skills: written proposals, specifications, progress reports, and technical reports; individual and group oral presentations; essays on engineering ethics, contemporary engineering issues and the impact of engineering decisions.
Core Category: [81] Writing in the Disciplines
