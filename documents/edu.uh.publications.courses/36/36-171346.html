ENGL 4351 - Poetry Writing

Credit Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: admission to the Creative Writing Concentration and 3 hours in 3000-/or 4000-level literature courses in poetry. ENGL 4354 recommended prior to this course.
Description
Analysis and writing of lyric poetry, including crafting of complete poems; traditional and contemporary lyrics; and lyric as a genre.
May be repeated once for credit.
