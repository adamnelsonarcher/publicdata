WCL 3346 - Terrorism: Past and Present

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304 .
Description
An exploration of the nature of terrorism, from its ancient origins through its postmodern evolution, through history, literature, and film.
