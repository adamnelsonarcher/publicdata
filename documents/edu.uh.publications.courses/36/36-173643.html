ART 4381 - Pursuing Utopia

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: twelve hours in foundation program studio courses, or consent of instructor.
Description
Examination of artistic and creative strategies having a direct, conscious and often catalytic relationship to place and community in which they exist, with focus on sustainability.
