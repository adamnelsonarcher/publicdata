MATH 1300 - Fundamentals of Mathematics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Description
A survey of precollege algebra. Topics include signed numbers, operations with algebraic expressions, first-degree equations and inequalities in one variable, factoring, the Cartesian coordinate system, systems of first-degree equations in two variables solved by graphical and algebraic means, exponents and radicals, an introduction to quadratic equations, and stated problems. This course may not be used to satisfy any degree requirements.
Note: This course is intended for students who have not attained a satisfactory college readiness score in mathematics.
