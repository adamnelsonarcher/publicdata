ARCH 3341 - Contemporary Japanese Architecture

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ARCH 2350 and ARCH 2351 .
Description
Investigation of the roots of new Japanese architecture, looking beyond the nostalgic argument that Japans success is based on its respect towards tradition.
