COSC 4394 - Physical Computing for Image Analytics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COSC 3337 or COSC 4351 or COSC 4393 .
Description
The design and construction of physical systems using a mix of software and hardware to sense and respond to the surrounding world.
