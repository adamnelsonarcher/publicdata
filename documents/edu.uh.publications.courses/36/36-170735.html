COMD 3385 - Speech Science

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Approved COMD major; COMD Core complete.
Description
Theoretical and physiological aspects of speech production and perception. Contributions of respiratory, laryngeal, velopharyngeal and articulatory systems to speech production; physical and speech acoustics; speech across the lifespan
Additional Fee Y
