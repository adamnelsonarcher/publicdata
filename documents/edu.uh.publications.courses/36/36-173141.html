SPAN 2308 - Spanish for Hispanic Heritage Learners II

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: completion of SPAN 2307 with a minimum of C- or placement exam.
Description
Credit for both SPAN 2308 and SPAN 2302 may not apply to degree. Continued development of reading and writing skills, and control of formal Spanish.
