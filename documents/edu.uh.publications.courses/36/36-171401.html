FINA 4350 - Derivatives I: Options

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ECON 2304 , FINA 3332 , and STAT 3331 ; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Provides a thorough analysis of the theory of options and its applications in practice. Emphasis is on risk management by investors and firms of all sizes.
