ENGR 2405 - Circuit Analysis

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: ENGI 1100 , ENGI 1331 , MATH 1432 PHYS 1321 , PHYS 1121
Corequisite: MATH 2433 , MATH 3321 , PHYS 1322 , PHYS 1122
Description
Basic concepts of electric circuit analysis techniques. Inductors, capacitors, first older circuits. Sinusoidal analysis.
Typically Offered: Spring
