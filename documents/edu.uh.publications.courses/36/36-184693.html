RELS 3342 - Religion, Politics, and History

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304.
Description
Examines intersections of religion and politics to study the United States and its history. Possible topics to include censorship, civil religion, demographic changes, economics, gender, laws, politicians, schools, sexuality, race, popular culture representations, and/or rituals.
