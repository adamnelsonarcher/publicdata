DIGM 3351 - Individualized Communication Processes

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: Grade of C or higher in DIGM 2325 and DIGM 2353.
Description
Strategically designing and producing personalized and engaging print media campaigns through the use of database and variable data software. Image capture, manipulation, color management, transfer, and finishing.
