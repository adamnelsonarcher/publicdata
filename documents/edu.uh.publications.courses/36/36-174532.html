COMM 2371 - Media, Sports and Culture

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COMM 1301 and COMM 1303 .
Description
Examination of the impact of culture on sports and the role that sports media play in shaping social values. Students will analyze and produce sports media using a cultural lense.
