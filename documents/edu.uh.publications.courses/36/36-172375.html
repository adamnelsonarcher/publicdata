MATH 1451 - Accelerated Calculus

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: MATH 1450 . An accelerated calculus sequence.
Description
MATH 1451 will include topics normally covered in MATH 1432 and MATH 2433 .
Note: Course Notes: Students with credit in MATH 1451 may not enroll in or receive credit for either MATH 1432 or MATH 2433.
Core Category: [90] Math/Reasoning
