MECE 2334 - Thermodynamics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEM 1112 , CHEM 1332 , MATH 2433 and PHYS 1322 .
Description
Fundamental concepts of heat and work, simple substances, energy analysis, first and second laws, thermodynamics of state and power cycles.
