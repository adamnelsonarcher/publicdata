TLIM 3371 - Budgeting and Financial Principles

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly TELS 2371.
Prerequisite: C or better MATH 1313.
Description
Concepts, procedures, and processes of recording and presenting financial data for industry. Budgeting, accounting systems, record keeping methodology, financial planning, and fiscal leadership.
