COMM 3385 - Communication Practicum Abroad

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COMM 1301 and instructor consent.
Description
Applies concepts and skills related to the field of communication in an international context. May be taken for credit twice for different programs.
Note: Instructor consent required; extra fee for travel.
