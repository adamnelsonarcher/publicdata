RUSS 2304 - A Survey of Russian Literature in English

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1303 or equivalent.
Description
No knowledge of Russian is required. First semester: beginnings to 1881; second semester: 1881 to the present.
Note: Course cannot be applied toward foreign language requirement.
