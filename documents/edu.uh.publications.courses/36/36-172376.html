MATH 2303 - Concepts in Algebra

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 1312 or MATH 1313 .
Description
Mathematical systems: classical and abstract algebra, systems of numeration, and basic number theory.
Note: May not apply to course or GPA requirements for a major or minor in natural sciences and mathematics.
