CUIN 2320 - Mathematics for Elementary and Middle School Teachers

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 1310 or MATH 1311; MATH 1312.
Description
Prepares students to teach mathematics at the elementary and middle school level. Emphasis is placed on developing specialized knowledge for teaching mathematics, which includes deep understanding of our base 10 number system, arithmetic, and problem solving. Topics include introduction to problem solving, counting, the four basic operations whole numbers decimals, and fractions, number theory, and the integration of algebra into arithmetic.
Core Category: [90] Math/Reasoning
