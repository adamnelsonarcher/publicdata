COMM 3322 - Latino Journalism

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COMM 2310 or consent of instructor.
Description
Discussion of theorectical approaches to Latino news focusing on news creation, production, circulation, distribution and representations. Student will practice newswriting skills, producing a course-long news package or digital writing portfolio.
Additional Fee Y
