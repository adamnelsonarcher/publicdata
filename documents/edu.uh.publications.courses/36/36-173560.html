BIOE 5319 - Introduction to Global Healthcare

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BIOE 3440 or consent of instructor.
Description
Overview of the global healthcare systems, advances and applications in information technologies in biomedicine, development of new drugs, technology regulation, and ethical issues using novel technologies.
