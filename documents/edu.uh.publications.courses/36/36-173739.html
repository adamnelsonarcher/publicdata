GEOL 4367 - Geochemical Reaction Modeling

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Declared major in the Department of Earth and Atmospheric Sciences; GEOL 3370 or GEOL 3325 , MATH 1431 and MATH 1432 , and both CHEM 1112 and CHEM 1332 or a grade of B- or better in GEOL 4365 , or consent of instructor.
Description
Theory and application of geochemical reaction modeling. Approaches to modeling aqueous processes and their applications in diverse geological and environmental systems.
