MUED 4221 - Choral Conducting II

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly MUSI 4221.
Prerequisite: MUSI 4220 .
Description
Builds on skills acquired in MUSI 4220 and examines the execution of dynamics and character in the conducting gesture. Continues the discussion of comprehensive score study.
Additional Fee Y
