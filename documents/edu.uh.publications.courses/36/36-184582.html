CNE 4312 - Construction Engineering Design Project

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: CIVE 4311, CNE 3322, CNE 3333, CNE 4321 and INDE 3333.
Description
Design of capstone construction project, including estimating, scheduling, document preparation, constructability site analysis, safety, equipment and material plans.
