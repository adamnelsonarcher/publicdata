MANA 4331 - Current Issues Human Res Mgmt

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MANA 3335 , or equivalent, and MANA 4330 or equivalent; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Topics of current interest and emerging issues in human resource management, including implications for research and applications.
