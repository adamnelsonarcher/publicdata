MUSI 2188 - Keyboard Skills and Collaborative Techniques

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: Admission as music major by audition.
Description
Techniques for developing skills in sight reading, keyboard harmony, rhythmic training, and accompanying.
Note: May be repeated for a maximum of four semester hours.
Additional Fee Y
