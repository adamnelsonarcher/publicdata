GEOL 3396 - Senior Research Project

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: A minimum GPA of 3.0 in both the major and cumulative, and approval of the department chair.
Description
Directed research project culminating in a departmentally approved report. GEOL 3396 and GEOL 4396 must be satisfied in order for any to apply to a degree.
