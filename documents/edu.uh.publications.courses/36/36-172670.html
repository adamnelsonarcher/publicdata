NAVY 3301 - Navigation

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Corequisite: NAVY 3311 .
Description
Coastal piloting, celestial and electronic ship navigation, involving nautical astronomy, navigational aids, satellite, and inertial systems.
