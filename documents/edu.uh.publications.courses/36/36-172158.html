INAR 3500 - Interior Architecture Design Studio V

Credit Hours: 5.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 9
Prerequisite: INAR 2501 and permission of the Dean.
Description
Interior building design with an emphasis on spatial organization, context within an architectural concept, and constructional systems.
