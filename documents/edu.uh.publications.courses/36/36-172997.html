PSYC 4345 - Emotion & Motivation

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 1300 , PSYC 2301 , PSYC 3301 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Basic concepts of emotions and motivation in both their normal and abnormal mode, with emphasis on their psychological bases.
Additional Fee Y
