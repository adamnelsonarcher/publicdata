SOC 3311 - Sociology of Law

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOC 1300.
Description
An examination of the institution of law as it actually operates in society: how legal institutions are structured, how people use law, how it affects us, and how it can be changed.
