COMM 3349 - Nonverbal Communication

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COMM 1301 or consent of instructor.
Description
Introduction to major codes of nonverbal communication within personal, interpersonal and professional contexts. The course will help students develop heightened awareness of the role of nonverbal features in human interaction and foster a deeper understanding of nonverbal messages across social contexts.
