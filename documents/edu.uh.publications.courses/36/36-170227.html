ART 4375 - Senior Photography/Digital Media Major II

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: ART 4370 , ART 4371 , and ART 4372 in preceding semester, portfolio review/approval by major faculty, and concurrent enrollment in ART 4373 and ART 4374 required.
Description
Individual work on senior thesis project resulting in a coherent body of artwork demonstrating creative and technical command of various photography and digital media. ART 4373 , ART 4374 , and 4375 must be satisfied in order for any to apply to a degree.
