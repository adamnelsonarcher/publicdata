MATH 3306 - Problem-Solving in Mathematics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Three hours of 1000-2000 level Mathematics, excluding MATH 1300.
Description
Strategies for solving problems in mathematics: reduction to smaller problems; analogy in mathematics; conjecture and proof; the processes of abstraction, generalization, and specialization.
Note: May not apply to course or GPA requirements for a major or minor in Natural Sciences and Mathematics.
