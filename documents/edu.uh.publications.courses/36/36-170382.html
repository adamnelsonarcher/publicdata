BIOE 2100 - Introduction To Biomedical Engineering

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGI 1100 , 1331.
Description
Key topics in biomedical engineering, including lectures from professors, engineers, and physicians active in the field.
Note: On- and off-campus visits to biomedical engineering laboratories.
