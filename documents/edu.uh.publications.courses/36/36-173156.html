SPAN 3339 - Spanish for the Global Professions

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SPAN 3302 & SPAN 3308 .
Description
All-skills course in the language of various professions, formal varieties of Spanish, the lexicon, language structures, and discourse devices required for communicating effectively in the professional world.
Additional Fee Y
