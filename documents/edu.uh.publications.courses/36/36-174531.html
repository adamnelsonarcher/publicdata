COMM 2356 - Business & Professional Communication

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COMM 1301 , COMM 1303 , ENGL 1304 , or consent of instructor.
Description
Communication activities for business and professional settings with emphasis on emails, texts, posts, memos, letters and reports, how to make presentations and how to interact with peers and supervisors appropriately.
