MECT 4367 - Industrial Maintenance and Reliability

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECT 3367.
Description
Industrial maintenance, replacement, and reliability. Inspection methods, data collection and analysis, and decision making in the maintenance and replacement planning.
