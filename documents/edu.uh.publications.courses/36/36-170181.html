ART 3358 - Clay Processes

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: ART 1350 and nine semester hours in foundation program studio courses or consent of instructor.
Description
Examination of sculptural form through the use of a variety of glazed and unglazed elements.
May be repeated for credit with consent of instructor.
