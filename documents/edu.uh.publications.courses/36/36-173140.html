SPAN 2307 - Spanish for Hispanic Heritage Learners I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: placement by exam or by Director of Basic Language Program. Credit for both SPAN 2307 and SPAN 2301 may not apply to a degree.
Description
Designed for Hispanic students and other students from a Spanish-speaking background. Basic skills in reading, spelling, and composition.
