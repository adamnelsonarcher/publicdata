DAN 3314 - Technology in Dance

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Junior standing or consent of instructor.
Corequisite: Concurrent enrollment required in a two or three credit hour dance technique class.
Description
This course will investigate the fusion of dance and technology with respect to the creation of intermedial dance works.
