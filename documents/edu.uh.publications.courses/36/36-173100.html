SOC 3340 - Sociology of Film

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Description
Socio-cultural study of themes, production, and distribution of cinema. How film reflects the values and attitudes of society: moral codes, minority groups, family and marital arrangements, sexuality and deviance.
