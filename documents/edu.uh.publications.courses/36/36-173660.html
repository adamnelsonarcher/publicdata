DAN 2304 - Dance Kinesiology

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: consent of instructor.
Description
Study of human body in motion as it relates to dance with a focus on anatomy, physiology, and biomechanics. Other course topics include: somatics, nutrition, conditioning, and injury prevention.
Typically Offered: Fall
