PSYC 4311 - Close Relationships

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 1300, PSYC 2301, and PSYC 3301, or consent of instructor.
Description
Basic theory and research in the psychological study of close relationships with an emphasis on adult romantic relationships. Topics include attraction, relationship cognitions, love and intimacy, sexuality, conflict, divorce, and relationship maintenance strategies.
