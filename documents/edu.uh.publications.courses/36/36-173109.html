SOC 3352 - Population Studies

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOC 1300.
Description
Theory and methods in the study of human populations. Fertility, mortality, migration, trends of world population change, population and the environment and population and economic development.
