ENGL 2304 - English Literature Since 1798

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304 or equivalent.
Description
Critical examination of works from the beginnings of Romanticism to present. Emphasis on composition.
