PHIL 1361 - Philosophy and the Arts

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1303
Description
Introduction to philosophical topics in the visual and performing arts, including criticism, interpretation, moral issues, and cultural contexts.
Core Category: [50] Creative Arts
