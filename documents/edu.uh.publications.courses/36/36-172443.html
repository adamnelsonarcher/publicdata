MECE 4343 - Thermal Design

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly MECE 3334 Thermodynamics II
Prerequisite: MECE 2334 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in MECE 4364 .
Description
Power and refrigeration cycles, mixture of ideal gases, basic combustion processes, equilibrium, thermodynamics of compressible flow, thermal system design.
