COSC 4346 - Security Analytics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Math 2331, Math 3339, COSC 2430 .
Description
Review of security problems including malware, phishing and intrusions. Unique challenges of security domain. Adaptations and Applications of Statistical, Data Mining, Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning techniques to security.
