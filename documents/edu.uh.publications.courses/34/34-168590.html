KIN 3303 - Sports Communication

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: junior standing and KIN 3360
Description
The role of sports communication and how it highlights the importance of sports, the cultural identities of those engaged in sports communication, and industry practices.
