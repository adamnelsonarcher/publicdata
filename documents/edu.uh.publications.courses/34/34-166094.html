DAN 4307 - Teaching Dance in Education

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: DAN 3310 , DAN 3311 , DAN 2305 DAN 3305 or instructor approval.
Corequisite: DAN 3302 , or DAN 4302 or instructor approval.
Description
Introduction to educational dance for K-12 inclusive of lesson and curriculum planning. Development of skills for teaching Creative Dance with field experiences in an elementary school.
