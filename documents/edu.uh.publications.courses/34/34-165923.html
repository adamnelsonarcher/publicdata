COSC 4315 - Programming Languages and Paradigms

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: COSC 2320 or COSC 2430 .
Description
Discussion and comparison of basic programming styles; their underlying paradigms and languages based on these paradigms; such as imperative and functional programming styles.
