PHIL 1301 - Introduction to Philosophy

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly PHIL 1340
Prerequisite: ENGL 1303 .
Description
Introduction to philosophy: issues such as the nature and extent of knowledge, the objectivity of scientific theories, the rationality of theistic belief, and human freedom.
Core Category: [40] Language, Philosophy, & Culture
TCCNS Equivalent: PHIL 1301
