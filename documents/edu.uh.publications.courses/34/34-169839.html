INDS 3370 - Advanced Design Systems

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: INDS 2341 or consent of advisor.
Description
Advanced CAD/CAM/CAE and product life cycle management systems in the context of product development. Topics include product data quality, parametric modeling, synchronous technology, and digital mock-ups.
