PHIL 3376 - Philosophy and the Scientific Revolution

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: three semester hours in philosophy and junior standing, or consent of instructor.
Description
Philosophical issues at the heart of the scientific revolution of the sixteenth and seventeenth centuries.
