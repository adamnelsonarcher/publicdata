PSYC 4372 - Interviewing

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 1300 , PSYC 2301 , PSYC 3301 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Skill development in personnel interviewing; includes tape-recorded practice interviews and discussion of theoretical issues and research findings.
Additional Fee Y
