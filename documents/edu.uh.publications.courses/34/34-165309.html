ART 4302 - Senior Painting Major I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: Concurrent enrollment in ART 4300 , ART 4301 , and ART 4302 required. ART 3309 - ART 3310 - ART 3311 and review of portfolio by major faculty. ART 4300 - ART 4301 - ART 4302 must be completed prior to ART 4303 - ART 4304 - ART 4305 .
Description
Development of analytical, conceptual, and critical skills through intensive studio work, critical studies, faculty mentoring, critiques, and visiting artists. ART 4300 , ART 4301 , and ART 4302 must be satisfied in order for any to apply to a degree.
