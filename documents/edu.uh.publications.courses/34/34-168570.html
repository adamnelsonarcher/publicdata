ENRG 4320 - Case Studies in Energy and Sustainability

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENRG 3310 and declared Energy and Sustainability minor, or permission of the program manager.
Description
Case studies on existing, transitional, and alternative energy resources, and sustainability from the perspective of economics and business; technology; architecture and design; public policy; and education.
