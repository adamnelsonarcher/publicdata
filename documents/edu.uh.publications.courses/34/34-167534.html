MATH 4390 - Applied Mathematics Seminar

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 3331 , or equivalent, and three additional hours of 3000-4000 level Mathematics.
Description
A team approach to a case study of a real world problem; emphasis on mathematical modeling, analysis on written and oral communication; completion of a technical report.
