HIST 3302 - Sex and Society in American History

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: junior standing or consent of instructor.
Description
Changes in sexual mores, the family, and the roles of men and women from the eighteenth through the twentieth centuries.
