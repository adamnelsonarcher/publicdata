HIST 4341 - The Roaring Twenties: Europe, 1919-1933

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304 ; HIST 2353 or HIST 3380 or consent of instructor.
Description
Lecture and discussion class, which utilizes wide-ranging primary sources to examine in comparative context the profound upheavals, events, and developments that shaped the decade after the First World War.
