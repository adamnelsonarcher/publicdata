DAN 1103 - Freshman Seminar in Somatics

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
Concurrent enrollment required in a two or three credit hour modern and/or ballet technique class. Each semester, students will learn through practical experience, a new somatic technique. Topics will include but are not limited to: Pilates, Alexander Technique, Feldenkrais, Yoga, Yamuna Body Rolling, and Gyrotonics.
