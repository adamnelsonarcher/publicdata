BTEC 3345 - Programming Tools for Bioinformatics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ELET 2300 or COSC 1320 .
Description
Fundamental PERL and R programming skills for bioinformatics. Emphasis on DNA and amino acid sequences and microarray profiles.
