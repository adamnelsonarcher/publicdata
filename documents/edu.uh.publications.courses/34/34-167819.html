PETR 5328 - Petroleum Fluid Properties and Phase Equilibria for non PETR major

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 5361 , 5362, 5364, and admitted as either a minor in PETR or PETR graduate program or consent of program..
Description
Volumetric behavior and equations of state representation of petroleum fluids; thermodynamics phase equilibrium of binary and multicomponent systems, experimental techniques for phase equilibrium measurements, equation of state tuning; advanced topics.
