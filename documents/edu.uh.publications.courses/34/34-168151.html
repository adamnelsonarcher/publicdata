RELS 4320 - Religion and Personality

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304 or equivalent.
Description
Tracking the evolution of human consciousness using symbols and myths, found particularly in Hebrew and Christian scared stories. Using the viewpoint of Jungian depth psychology, this course reveals the structure and dynamic of the Psyche.
