COSC 4368 - Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: COSC 2320 or COSC 2430 .
Description
An overview of main topics in the field of artificial intelligence. Topics include search techniques, reasoning with logic, planning, decision making, machine learning, and robotics.
