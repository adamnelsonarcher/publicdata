GEOL 1370 - Natural Disasters

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: Course MATH 1310 or MATH 1311 .
Description
The scientific study of the geology, meteorology, oceanography, and climatology of common natural disasters.
Typically Offered: FALL

Core Category: (30) Life & Physical Sciences
