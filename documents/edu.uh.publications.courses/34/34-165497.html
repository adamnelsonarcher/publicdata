BIOE 3350 - Biosensors II

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BIOE 2150 , CHEE 2331 , ECE 2100 , ECE 2201 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in BIOE 3341 .
Description
Biosensor data generation methods, data manipulation techniques, and health information synthesis processes.
