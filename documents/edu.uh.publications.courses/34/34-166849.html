HIST 3347 - Comparative European Revolutions - English, French, and Russian

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: HIST 2353 or consent of instructor.
Description
A comparative analysis of the seventeenth century English Revolution, the French Revolution of 1789, and the Russian Revolution of 1905-1917.
