PHIL 3349 - Philosophy of the Social Sciences

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: 3 hours in Philosophy or consent of instructor.
Description
Philosophical issues arising in such social sciences as psychology, archaeology, anthropology, and economics.
May be repeated for credit with approval of department chair when topics vary.
