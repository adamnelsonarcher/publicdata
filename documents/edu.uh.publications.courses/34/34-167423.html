MIS 3370 - Information Systems Development Tools

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Completion of or concurrent enrollment in MIS 3360 ; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Survey of development options in commercial application systems, including structured methods and programming tools.
