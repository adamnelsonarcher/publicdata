ART 3330 - Intermediate Graphic Design

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: twelve semester hours in foundation program studio courses (1000-level), including ART 1330 , or consent of instructor.
Description
Message and meaning in imagery, through media exploration, image stylization, visualizing skills, and basic typography.
May be repeated for credit with consent of instructor.
