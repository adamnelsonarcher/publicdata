ENGL 2350 - Intermediate Composition

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304 .
Description
This course serves as a bridge between the first year composition sequence and/or Advanced Writing in the Discipline courses for both English majors and non-majors.
