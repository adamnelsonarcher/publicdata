BIOL 1362 - Introduction to Biological Science 2

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 1310 or equivalent.
Description
BIOL 1362 is the second semester of a two-part Introduction to Biological Science course and focuses on genetics, biotechnology, evolution and ecology.
Core Category: [30] Life & Physical Sciences
TCCNS Equivalent: BIOL 1307
