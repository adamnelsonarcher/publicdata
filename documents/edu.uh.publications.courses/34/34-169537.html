BIOL 2315 - Biology of Food

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
An exploration of the biological diversity, physiology, cell biology, genetics, and evolution of the organisms (plants, animals, fungi, and bacteria) we eat and their relation to human biology. Modification of food organisms through domestication and genetic engineering and the role of modern agriculture in food production and sustainability will be explored.
Core Category: [30] Life & Physical Sciences
