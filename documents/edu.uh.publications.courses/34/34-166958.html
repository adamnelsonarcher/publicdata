HON 4310 - City Dionysia

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304 or equivalent.
Description
A project-based, course wherein students develop, research, curate, and perform a creative project located within or inspired by the City of Houston or the City Dionysia project. A capstone course for the Creative Work minor.
