PSYC 4399 - Senior Honors Thesis

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 1300 , PSYC 2301 , PSYC 3301 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Qualified student selects faculty member to supervise the topic choice and writing of the thesis. PSYC 3399 and 4399 must be satisfied in order for any to apply to a degree.
Additional Fee Y
