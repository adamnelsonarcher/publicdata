ENGI 4100 - Engineering Internship

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: Undergraduate Engineering Major.
Description
Student work on a project in industry which is also supervised by a program faculty.
Course may be repeated for credit for a maximum of 3.0 credit hours.

Typically Offered: Fall, Spring & Summer
