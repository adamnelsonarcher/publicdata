COSC 4151 - Computer Science Internship

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COSC major with junior standing or higher and prior written approval of department chair or designated.
Description
Enhancement of concepts and techniques learned in the classroom through work experience directly related to the computer science profession.
Note: Employer evaluation and written report required.
