COMD 2338 - Phonetics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Description
Analysis of spoken English in articulatory, perceptual, and acoustic terms, with emphasis on general American dialect and its phonetic transcription.
