ILAS 3390 - Museum Internship at Blaffer Art Museum

Credit Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: junior standing and consent of instructor.
Description
Internship at Blaffer undertaken in one or more of the following: curatorial, education, external affairs, public relations and membership, registrar.
May be repeated once for credit with consent of instructor.
