PETR 5361 - Intro To Petroleum Engineering for non PETR majors

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: admitted as either a minor in PETR or PETR graduate program or consent of program.
Description
Petroleum origin and migration, major oil and gas fields, drilling and production methods, petroleum composition and phase behavior, and reservoir engineering methods of oil resource estimation and optimization.
