MECE 4334 - Mechanical Design - Capstone Course

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: senior standing in mechanical engineering, MECE 3338 , MECE 3369 , and MECE 4364 .
Description
Credit may not be received for both INDE 4334 and MECE 4334 . Applications of analytical, experimental and computational techniques in open-ended problems supplied by industry and faculty.
