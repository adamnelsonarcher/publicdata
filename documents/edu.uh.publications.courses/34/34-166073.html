DAN 2217 - Introduction to Music Video Dance

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: none
Description
Concepts and skills involved in jazz dance with an emphasis on styles and methods in the commercial industries of dance, including current trends in television and video.
