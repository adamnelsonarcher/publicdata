PHYS 4350 - Computational Physics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHYS 3315 , MATH 3331 , and COSC 1306 .
Description
Simulation of classical and quantum mechanical problems on digital computers using numerical and modern programming techniques.
