HLT 3303 - Health of Elementary School Child

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Junior standing or consent of instructor.
Description
Health status, determinants of health, needs, and interests of elementary age children with implications for public health and health education.
