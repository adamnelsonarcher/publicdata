INDS 4180 - Design Internship

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: INDS 3500 and prior written approval from the director.
Description
Application of design knowledge and techniques through work experience at design consultancies or related companies. Requires employer evaluation.
