PHYS 1122 - University Physics Laboratory II

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0.0 Lab Contact Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in PHYS 1322 .
Description
Experiments in thermodynamics, electricity, magnetism, and optics. Credit may not be applied toward a degree for PHYS 1122 and PHYS 1102 .
