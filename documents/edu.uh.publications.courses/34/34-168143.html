RELS 3370 - The Bible and Modern Science

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304 or ENGL 1310 .
Description
Detailed study of the reciprocal influence of modern science and the interpretation of the Bible. Critical examination of contemporary theories of their relationship.
Note: The same assignment will be used to assess all noted competency areas.
Core Category: [81] Writing in the Disciplines
