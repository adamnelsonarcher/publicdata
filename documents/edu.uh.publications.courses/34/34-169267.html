BTEC 3302 - Molecular Genetics and Biotechnology

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BIOL 1361 / BIOL 1161 , BIOL 1362 / BIOL 1162 , CHEM 1332 , and BTEC 1322 .
Description
Overview of molecular genetics and biology as applicable to biotechnology. Discussion includes industrially relevant organisms, synthetic biology, and the impact of bioengineering solutions in economic, environmental, and societal contexts.
