FINA 4384 - Residential Real Estate

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: FINA 4330 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in FINA 4380 ; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Overview of residential real estate: multi-family, single family, community development and homebuilding, including professional aspects, financial and market analysis, financing, development, sale or leasing and operations.
