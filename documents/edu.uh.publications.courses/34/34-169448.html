ARCH 4394 - Environmental Design Internship

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: ARCH 2501 .
Description
Enhancement of concepts and techniques learned in the classroom through work experience directly related to the environmental design profession.
Note: Requires employer evaluation.
