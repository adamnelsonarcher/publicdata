ENVS 4301 - Environmental Science and Public Policy

Credit Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: BIOL 1161 / BIOL 1361 , BIOL 1162 / BIOL 1362 , MATH 1432 and CHEM 1332 or CHEM 1372 .
Description
A critical look at environmental policy and decision making in the 21st century. Environmental and energy policy; environmental policy as an international and strategic security issue; environment and fisheries and agriculture; environmental policy and extreme weather events on the Gulf Coast; environmental policy and the “quality of life.”
