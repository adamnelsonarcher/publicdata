MIS 4371 - Interactive Systems

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MIS 3360 ; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Techniques for implementing human-machine interfaces for data entry, inquiry, modeling, and data processing systems.
