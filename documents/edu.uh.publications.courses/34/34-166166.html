ECON 4390 - Economics Internship

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ECON 3332 and ECON 3334 and consent of instructor.
Description
Work with practicing economists in selected private industry, federal, state and local government offices. Correlated readings.
May be repeated once for credit. Only three hours may apply toward the ECON major requirements.
