ART 4304 - Senior Painting Major II

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: Concurrent enrollment in ART 4303 , 4304, ART 4305 required. ART 4300 - ART 4301 - ART 4302 and review of portfolio by major faculty.
Description
Continued concentrated studio immersion emphasizing conceptual and critical skills to develop a unique, cohesive, pertinent, and timely body of work. Ongoing critical studies, faculty mentoring, critiques, and visiting artists. ART 4303 , 4304, and ART 4305 must be satisfied in order for any to apply to a degree.
