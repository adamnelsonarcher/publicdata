AAMS 3310 - Asian American in Popular Culture

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly CUST 3310.
Prerequisite: CUST 2300 or ANTH 2302 or consent of instructor.
Description
An interdisciplinary approach analyzing the social and political implications in the representation of Asian Americans through film, newspapers, and other media. Emphasis on concrete applications of theoretical models from the social and human services.
Additional Fee Y
