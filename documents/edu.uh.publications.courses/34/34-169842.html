MATH 3349 - Inferential Statistics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 3338 .
Description
Basic principles for statistical inference with practical applications of data. Includes point estimation, confidence intervals, hypothesis testing, ANOVA and simple linear regression. Includes use of statistical software.
