THEA 3369 - Acting Shakespeare I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: THEA 1338 , THEA 1339 , and theatre major or consent of instructor.
Description
Basic principles and training in the use of Shakespearean verse forms and their application to text and character.
