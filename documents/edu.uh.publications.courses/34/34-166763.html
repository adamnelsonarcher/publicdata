HLT 2320 - Introduction to Public Health

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: HLT 1353 and 3 credit hours of Life & Physical Sciences.
Description
This course will provide an overview of critical issues in the field of public health and health promotion as well as concepts of health and health education and contributions of health care professionals in a variety of settings.
Additional Fee Y
