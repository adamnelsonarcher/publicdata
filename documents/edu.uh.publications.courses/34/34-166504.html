FINA 4351 - Derivatives II: Forwards, Futures and Swaps

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ECON 2304 , FINA 3332 , and STAT 3331 ; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Valuation and use of futures and swaps on commodities and financial instruments.
