ITAL 2101 - Intermediate Italian Conversation I

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ITAL 1302 , ITAL 1502 , or ITAL 2301 , or consent of instructor.
Description
Can not be used to satisfy 2000-level language requirement. May be taken concurrently with ITAL 2301 or ITAL 2302 . Intensive practice in spoken Italian.
May be repeated for credit with consent of instructor.
