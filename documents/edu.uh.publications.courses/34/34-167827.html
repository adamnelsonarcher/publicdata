PETR 4392 - Petroleum Project Management

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 3315 , 3318, 3362 and 3372.
Description
Effective management of petroleum products based on best practices established by the Project Management Institute Standards Committee.
