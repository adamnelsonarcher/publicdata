HIST 4335 - Greek and Roman Historians

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304 and junior standing, or consent of instructor.
Description
Reading the works of the Greek and Roman founders of the historical discipline to explore the writing of history as a philosophical and literary enterprise.
