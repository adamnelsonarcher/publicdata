MECE 3369 - Solid Mechanics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECE 3336 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in MATH 3363 .
Description
Intermediate studies of the principles of stress analysis, buckling, energy methods, and the theories of failure; includes practical applications.
