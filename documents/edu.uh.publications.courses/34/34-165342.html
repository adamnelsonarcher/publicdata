ARTH 1300 - Ways of Seeing: Art and Our Visual World

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1303 .
Description
Examination of our visual world and the various ways in which that world has been analyzed and valued.
Note: May not be applied to a major or minor in art history or art.
Core Category: [40] Language, Philosophy, & Culture
