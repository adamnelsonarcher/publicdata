ART 4300 - Senior Painting Major I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: ART 3309 - ART 3310 - ART 3311 and review of portfolio by major faculty.
Corequisite: Concurrent enrollment in ART 4300 , ART 4301 , and ART 4302 required.
Description
Development of analytical, conceptual, and critical skills through intensive studio work, critical studies, faculty mentoring, critiques, and visiting artists.
Note: AART 4300, ART 4301, and ART 4302 must be satisfied in order for any to apply to a degree; ART 4300-ART 4301-ART 4302 must be completed prior to ART 4303-ART 4304-ART 4305.
