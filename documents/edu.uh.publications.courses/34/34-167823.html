PETR 5364 - Origin & Development of Oil and Gas Reservoirs for PETR majors

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 5361 and admitted as either a minor in PETR or PETR graduate program or consent of program.
Description
Major oil provinces of the world reviewed from the standpoints of geologic and depositional environment, and of diagenetic changes affecting petroleum entrapment.
