ART 3376 - Junior Photography/Digital Media Major

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: concurrent enrollment in ART 3375 , 3376, and ART 3377 completion of pre-major courses and portfolio review, and approval by major faculty.
Description
Chemical and digital photography, single and multiple imagery, Web art and video art.
May be repeated for credit with faculty permission. ART 3375, 3376, and 3377 must be satisfied in order for any to apply to a degree.
