ELET 4421 - Computer Networks

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: credit for or concurrent enrollment in ELET 3402 .
Description
Introduction to computer networking: local area networking, access technologies, and internet protocol. Laboratory instruction in network implementation and development and operating system management.
