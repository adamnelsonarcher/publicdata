THEA 3367 - Acting in Comedy and Farce

Credit Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: THEA 1338 , THEA 1339 , and theatre major or consent of instructor.
Description
Application of advanced acting techniques to comedy/farce through monologues and scene work drawn from contemporary plays.\
