MUSI 4251 - Advanced Performance Pedagogy

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Junior standing in applied music. Successful completion of MUSI 4250 .
Description
An exploration of methods, materials, teaching techniques, and strategies for individual and group instruction for intermediate to early advanced piano students. Includes an introduction to teaching musical style and performance practice of the standard teaching repertoire.
