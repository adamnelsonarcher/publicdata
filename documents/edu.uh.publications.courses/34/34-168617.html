IRW 1300 - Integrated Reading and Writing

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Consent of instructor.
Description
Permits the enrolled student to complete the requirements for both developmental reading and developmental English in a single semester course. Through weekly meetings with a consultant, the student will read, discuss and demonstrate the critical thinking, planning, and drafting which constitute the academic reading and writing processes.
Note: May not be used to satisfy any degree requirements at the university.
Additional Fee Y
