ITAL 3345 - Theater in Italy: Text, Audience, and Performance

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304 and ITAL 2302 or equivalent.
Description
The social and historical context in which Italian theatre developed. Taught in English; papers must be written in Italian.
Note: May apply to major in Italian Studies, minor in Italian. Credit for ITAL 3345 may not be applied toward a degree by students who have credit for ITAL 3346.
