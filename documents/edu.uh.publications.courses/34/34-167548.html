MECE 3345 - Materials Science

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECE 2334 , CHEM 1332 and CHEM 1112 .
Description
Properties of materials described by and related to materials structure. Emphasis on mechanical properties of materials.
