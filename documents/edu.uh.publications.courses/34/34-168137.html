RELS 2380 - Religion and Film

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304 or ENGL 1310 .
Description
Introduction to the study of religions and their portrayal in film; exploration of two-way pervasion of religion and culture and identity.
