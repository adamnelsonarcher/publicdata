PETR 5300 - Petroleum Data Mining & Database Management

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 3321 and PETR 2311 .
Description
Petroleum data mining goals, data quality, data preprocessing, OLAP, exploratory data analysis, classification, regression, clustering, dimensionality reduction, association rules, post processing, and data mining case studies.
