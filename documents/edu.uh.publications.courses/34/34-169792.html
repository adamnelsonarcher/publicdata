COMM 4389 - Media for Social Justice: Digital Media & Activism in Latin America & Houston

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
An experiential learning course that introduces students to both sides of the media/movement relationship, preparing students to fairly, ethically and accurately cover movements as journalists. They will also learn how activists can best deal with media and how to take advantage of new technologies to take charge of message and media content.
