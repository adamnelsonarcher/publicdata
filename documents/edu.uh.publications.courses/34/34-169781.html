COMM 2326 - Introduction to Sports Broadcasting

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COMM 1301 and COMM 1303 or consent of instructor.
Description
Interactive study of 21st century sports broadcasting, including local high school sports live coverage.
Note: Equipment needed: A digital audio tape recorder or other device for audio recording, microphone optional but recommended (cell phones will work).Students must be able to travel to games.
