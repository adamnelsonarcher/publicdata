HIST 4348 - Social History of Early Modern England

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: HIST 2351 .
Description
Society, economy, and culture in England during the age of More, Shakespeare, and Milton. Topics include the nature of a pre-modern economy, high culture vs. popular culture, and the lives of common women and men in this period.
