ELED 4320 - Social Studies in the Elementary School

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: admission to QUEST 2.
Description
Field-based course that introduces the contemporary structure of social studies programs; social science backgrounds: inquiry processes; cognitive, affective; teaching strategies.
