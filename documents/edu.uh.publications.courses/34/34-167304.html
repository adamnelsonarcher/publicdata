ITAL 3304 - Culture of Italy

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ITAL 2302 or equivalent.
Description
Overview of the culture of Italy through readings about its history, art, society, and politics. Taught in Italian.
