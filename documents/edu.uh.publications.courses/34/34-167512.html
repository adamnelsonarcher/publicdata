MATH 4331 - Introduction To Real Analysis I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 3333 .
Description
Study of metric spaces and real-valued functions. Topics include: convergence of sequences, continuity and uniform continuity of functions, sequences and series of functions, differentiation, and Riemann-Stiljies integration.
