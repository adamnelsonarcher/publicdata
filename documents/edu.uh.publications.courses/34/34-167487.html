MATH 3310 - History of Mathematics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 1431 .
Description
A survey of the history of mathematics from Euclid to Dedekind focusing on the development of the real number system and its relation to the Euclidean line.
