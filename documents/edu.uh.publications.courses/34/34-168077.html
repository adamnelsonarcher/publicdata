PSYC 3337 - Psychology of Human Sexuality

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 1300 , PSYC 2301 , PSYC 3301 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Empirical studies regarding adult sexual behaviors, the physiology of sexual behaviors and sexual disorders.
