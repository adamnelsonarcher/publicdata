MANA 4330 - Intro To Human Resource Mgmt

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MANA 3335 ; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
A survey of personnel management and the utilization of human resources, including research and applications.
