BIOE 4335 - Capstone Design I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: ENGI 2304 , INDE 2333 , MECE 3400 , all required BIOE 3000 level courses in the degree plan, and credit for or concurrent enrollment in BIOE 4115 and BIOE 4315 .
Description
Propose and begin team projects involving open-ended design problems supplied by industry and faculty. Professionalism, research methodologies, design tools, and technical communication.
