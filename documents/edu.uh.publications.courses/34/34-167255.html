INAR 3300 - History of Interior Architecture

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ARCH 2350 and 2351.
Description
History and theory of interior architecture, including cultural, political, economic, and environmental influences. Introduction to the important role of “the existing” in interior architecture through a global survey of designers, works, history, theory, and contemporary influences including philosophy, fine and applied art and broader material culture.
Additional Fee Y
