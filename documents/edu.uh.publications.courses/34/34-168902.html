NURS 4521 - Community Health Nursing

Credit Hours: 5.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: Credit for or Concurrent Enrollment in NURS 3310 , NURS 3311 , NURS 3315 .
Description
This course introduces the concept of community as client. The focus is on providing culturally appropriate care to populations within the public health frame work. Allows opportunities for knowledge synthesis in the provision of nursing care in community-based and community health settings. Clinical instruction focuses on application of the nursing process to families, groups, communities and populations.
Additional Fee Y
