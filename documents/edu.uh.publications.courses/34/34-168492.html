CORE 1101 - College Success

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Description
First year student academic success course providing in-class and online self-assessment activities covering learning/critical thinking/study skills, major selection, motivation, accountability, and strategic planning.
Note: This course is being redeveloped to be offered again in Fall 2015.
