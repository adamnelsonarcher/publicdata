ENGL 1100 - Basic Writing

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Corequisite: Must be taken concurrently with ENGL 1303 .
Description
A non-course competency-based option comprising 15-30 hours of mandatory individual tutoring to address developmental writing outcomes that students have not mastered. The tutoring supports the development of college-level writing through practice in the writing process.
