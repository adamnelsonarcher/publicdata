HDFS 4319 - Advanced Early Childhood Development

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2.0 Lab Contact Hours: 2.0
Prerequisite: HDFS 2317 , HDFS 3350 and prior or concurrent enrollment in HDFS 4317 .
Description
Application of theories and evidence-based practices of early childhood education with a practicum in early childhood setting or research.
Additional Fee $2.00 Fee Type Lab Fee
