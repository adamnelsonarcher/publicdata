DAN 4332 - Business & Financial Management in the Arts

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: consent of instructor.
Description
Financial management skills related to budget development and management. Additional topics include financial planning, bookkeeping, and accounting systems appropriate for independent artists and small nonprofit organizations.
