NURS 4215 - Maternal, Newborn, and Women

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 30 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: NURS 3310 , NURS 3311 , NURS 3315 , NURS 3312 , NURS 3313 , NURS 3314 .
Corequisite: NURS 4217 Maternal Child Clinical, and Credit for or Concurrent Enrollment in: NURS 4316 Med/Surg 1 Concepts of Care, NURS 4417 Med/Surg 1 Clinical.
Description
This course focuses on collaborative care of the women, newborn, and child bearing families.
