ITAL 3309 - Women Writers and Filmmakers of Modern Italy

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304 .
Description
Seminal works of women writers and filmmakers of modern Italy are read and screened in conjunction with current scholarship on Italian modernity and gender studies.
Note: Taught in English.
Core Category: [81] Writing in the Disciplines
