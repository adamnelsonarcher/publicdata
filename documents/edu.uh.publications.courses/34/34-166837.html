HIST 3335 - Barbarians and the Birth of Europe to 1050 A.D.

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: junior standing or consent of instructor.
Description
An assessment of the successive waves of “barbarians” - early Christians, Germans, Saracens, Vikings - who overthrew the Roman Empire and created the First Europe - the world of castles, knights, and monasteries.
