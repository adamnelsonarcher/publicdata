THEA 4348 - Improvisation for Actors

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: theater major, THEA 1338 , THEA 1339 , and consent of instructor.
Description
Acting exercises to unlock and unblock self to allow truth in character portrayal.
May be repeated for credit.
