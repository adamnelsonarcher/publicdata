MIS 3100 - Excel Lab

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: Credit for MIS 1FOS [TCCN BCIS 1305, or 1405, or alternative].
Description
Fundamentals of information literacy skills and computer tools relevant for business processes and decision making.
Note: A passing score on the Excel Competency Exam is required for a grade of Satisfactory in the course. Credit may not be received for both MIS 3100 and MIS 3300.
