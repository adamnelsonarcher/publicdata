CUIN 3201 - Teaching Young Children

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: admission to Teacher Education Program and credit for or concurrent enrollment in CUIN 3111 .
Description
Introduction to the teaching profession. Theories and principles of learning in young children with an emphasis on the importance of family.
