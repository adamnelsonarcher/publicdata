PSYC 3338 - Psychology of Older Adults

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 1300 , PSYC 2301 , PSYC 3301 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Lifespan, research-based perspective of the biopsychosocial stages of older adulthood, age 55 and over.
Additional Fee Y
