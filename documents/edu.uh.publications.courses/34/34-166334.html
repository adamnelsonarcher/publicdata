ENGI 3301 - Technology and Western Culture

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly MECE 3301
Prerequisite: junior standing.
Description
Open to all students who meet the stated prerequisite. Focuses on technological foundations of northern European culture, eighth century through the industrialization of Europe and America. Reference to technological influences from earlier Mediterranean and oriental civilizations.
