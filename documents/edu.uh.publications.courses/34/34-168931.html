SPAN 2610 - Intensive Intermediate Spanish

Credit Hours: 6.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 6 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Completion of SPAN 1502 or SPAN 1505 with a minimum grade of C- or placement by exam.
Description
Designed for Second Language learners. Heritage speakers not eligible to enroll. Continued development of oral expression and listening comprehension with increased emphasis on reading and writing skills.
Note: Credit for both SPAN 2610 and SPAN2301/2302 may not apply to a degree.
