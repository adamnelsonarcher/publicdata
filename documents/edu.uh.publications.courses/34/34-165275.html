ART 3332 - Illustration

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: twelve semester hours in foundation program studio courses (1000-level) or consent of instructor.
Description
Investigation of the Technical l and compositional practices common to the field of contemporary illustration.
May be repeated for credit with consent of instructor.
