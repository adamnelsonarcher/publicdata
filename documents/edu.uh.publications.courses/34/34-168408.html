THEA 3335 - History of the Theatre I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: THEA 2344 and consent of instructor.
Description
Survey of theatrical theory and practice from Ancient Greece through the Early Modern Era with special attention paid to cultural, philosophical, and religious contexts.
