SOC 3351 - Social Class and Mobility in America

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOC 1300 .
Description
Class, race, and sex inequalities in power, prestige, and life chances viewed in the context of American occupational structure and social mobility processes.
Core Category: [81] Writing in the Disciplines
