HIST 4339 - Modern European Intellectual History

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304 and either of HIST 2353 or HIST 3380 , or consent of instructor.
Description
Fundamental texts in the history of European ideas, from the Enlightenment into the 20th century. Explores major currents of modern thought in their historical contexts.
Core Category: [81] Writing in the Disciplines
