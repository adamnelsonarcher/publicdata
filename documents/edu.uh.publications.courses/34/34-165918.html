COSC 3380 - Design of File and Database Systems

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: MATH 3336 , and COSC 2320 or 2430. Declared Computer Science majors and minors, and Computer Engineering majors.
Description
Techniques for file organizations on secondary storage, performance, design, and management of large integrated databases, data models, query languages.
Typically Offered: Fall
