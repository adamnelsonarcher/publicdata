MATH 1313 - Finite Math with Applications

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Description
Systems of linear equations, introduction to linear programming, mathematics of finance, topics in probability and statistics.
Note: Students with prior credit for MATH 2331 will not receive credit for MATH 1313. May not apply to course or GPA requirements for a major or minor in natural sciences and mathematics.
Core Category: [20] Mathematics and [90] Math/Reasoning
TCCNS Equivalent: MATH 1324
