BIOE 2350 - Continuum Biomechanics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BIOE 2100 or ENGI 1100 , BIOL 1362 , BIOL 1162 , MATH 2433 , and PHYS 1322 and credit for or registration in MATH 3321 .
Description
Application of solid mechanics and fluid mechanics to biological systems.
