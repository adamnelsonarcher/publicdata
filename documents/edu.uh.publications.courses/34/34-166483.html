ENVS 3301 - Data Analysis in Environmental Sciences

Credit Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: MATH 2433 and either MATH 2311 or consent of instructor.
Description
Basic techniques of data analysis relevant to environmental sciences: data acquisition and recording, data processing and presentation, statistical methods and error handling, spatial analyses of data fields, and time-series analysis method. Examples drawn from data collection and analysis in environmental sciences.
