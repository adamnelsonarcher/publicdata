ART 3367 - Junior Sculpture Major

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: Concurrent enrollment in ART 3365 , ART 3366 , and 3367 required. completion of premajor courses and approval of major faculty. Investigation of sculptural problems related to various media.
Description
Sculpture studio emphasizes individual problem solution and expression. ART 3365 , ART 3366 , and 3367 must be satisfied in order for any to apply to a degree.
