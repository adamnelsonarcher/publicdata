SOC 1301 - Honors Introduction to Sociology

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: enrollment in Honors College. credit for or concurrent enrollment in ENGL 1303 or equivalent.
Description
Scientific study of human societies; characteristics of social units, their interrelationships, and processes of change. Major theories, methods, and research findings in sociology.
Core Category: [80] Social & Behavioral Sciences
