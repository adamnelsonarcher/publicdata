MUED 4340 - Elementary and Middle School Instrumental Administration

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly MUSI 4340.
Prerequisite: MUED 3320 .
Description
Recruiting methods, materials and organization applicable to the beginning band/orchestra program; wind instrument instruction and student accountability. May not be used as an advanced music elective.
Additional Fee Y
