LATN 3321 - Medieval Latin Prose Readings

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: LATN 2302 or consent of instructor.
Description
May apply toward 3 hours of the foreign language requirement and/or 3 hours in Latin required for major in Classical Studies or minor in Latin. Grammatical analysis and translation of selected Latin prose texts from late antique and medieval writers. Style and development of the Latin language in the Middle Ages.
