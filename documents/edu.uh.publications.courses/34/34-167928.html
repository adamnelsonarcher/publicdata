PHYS 3112 - Modern Optics Laboratory

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0.0 Lab Contact Hours: 3.0
Prerequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in PHYS 3110 and PHYS 3312 .
Description
Experiments in geometric optics, interference, diffraction, polarization, holography, Fourier transform optics, graded index optics, and nonlinear optics
