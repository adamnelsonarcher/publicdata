SPAN 2302 - Intermediate Spanish II

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: successful completion of SPAN 2301 with a minimum grade of C- in twelve months immediately prior to enrollment or by placement exam immediately prior to enrollment.
Description
Heritage speakers of Spanish not eligible to enroll. Cultural and literary readings, composition, and grammar review.
TCCNS Equivalent: SPAN 2312
