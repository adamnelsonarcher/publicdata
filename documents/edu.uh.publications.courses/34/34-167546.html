MECE 3336 - Mechanics II (Dynamics)

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGI 1331 , CIVE 2330 or MECE 2336 , MATH 2433 .
Corequisite: Corequisite(s): Credit for or concurrent enrollment in MATH 3321 .
Description
Kinematics and dynamics of particles and rigid bodies, and introduction to mechanical vibrations.
