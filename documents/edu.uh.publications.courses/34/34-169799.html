DIGM 4350 - Venue Transmedia

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: Grade of C or better in DIGM 3374 .
Description
The tools, technologies, and practices of producing and directing a multiplatform live event in a venue with an audience in attendance. Capturing the event, video switching, text and motion graphics generation, sound design and mixing, video projection, lighting, and content streaming for remote delivery and viewing.
