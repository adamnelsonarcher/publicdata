MATH 1100 - Developmental Math

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Corequisite: MATH 1310 or MATH 1311 or MATH 1312 or MATH 1313 or MATH 2311 .
Description
Individualized instructional support for students who are not college ready in mathematics. Topics will vary depending on student skill level and pathway.
Note: This course is intended for students who have not attained a satisfactory college readiness score in mathematics. Students must co-enroll in MATH 1310 or MATH 1311 or MATH 1312 or MATH 1313 or MATH 2311.
