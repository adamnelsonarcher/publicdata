RELS 3334 - Us and Them: Ethical Decision-Making in Complex Cultures

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304
Description
Examination of how diverse religions and other cultural belief systems create structures of exclusion and inclusion, tolerance and intolerance, obligation and responsibility in ethical decision-making.
