MECE 3400 - Introduction to Mechanics

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 4 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHYS 1321 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in MATH 3321 .
Description
Not for mechanical engineering majors. Statics of bodies, analysis of deformation and stress in elastic solids subject to bending and torsion, dynamics of particles, and mechanical vibrations.
