ITAL 3346 - Theatre in Italy: Text, Audience, and Performance

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304 .
Description
A comprehensive vision of the social and historical context in which Italian theatre developed. Taught in English; papers must be written in English.
Note: May apply to major in Italian Studies. Credit for ITAL 3346 may not be applied toward a degree by students who have credit for ITAL 3345.
