FINA 3332 - Principles of Financial Management

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ACCT 2331 , MATH 1313 and 1314; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Time value of money, principles of corporate financial management and investments.
