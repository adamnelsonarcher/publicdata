ECE 3318 - Applied Electricity and Magnetism

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: ENGI 1100 , ENGI 1331 , MATH 2433 , MATH 3321 , PHYS 1322 , PHYS 1122 .
Description
Fundamentals of electricity and magnetism, vector calculus, Maxwell’s equations, Kirchoff’s laws, static electric and magentic fields, resistance, capacitance, inductance, magentic circuits, and transformers.
