HIST 3380 - World Civilization Since 1500

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: junior standing or consent of instructor and ENGL 1304 .
Description
An overview of the interactions among seven major cultural traditions (Judeo-Christian, Graeco-Roman, Indian, Chinese, African, Islamic, American Indian) from c.e.1500.
Core Category: [40] Language, Philosophy, & Culture
