ILAS 4350 - Liberal Studies Senior Seminar

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304 and junior standing.
Description
Classic works in world literature from across the disciplines. Students will integrate content and methodology from their three minors that constitute the Liberal Studies major, aimed at a broad Liberal Studies perspective.
