INDE 4374 - Industrial Supervision

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGI 2304 .
Description
Tools and scientific methods necessary to better understand the role of human behavior in complex sociotechnical systems such as cognitive processes of organizational behavior, dynamics of organizational behavior, leadership, and innovation.
