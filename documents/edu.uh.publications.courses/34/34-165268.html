ART 3317 - Photo-Based Printmaking

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: twelve semester hours in foundation program studio courses or equivalent.
Description
Photo-mechanical printing techniques including photo-polymer intaglio and relief, photo-lithography, and polyester plate lithography.
May be repeated for credit with consent of instructor.
