SPAN 3343 - Spanish for the Health Professions

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SPAN 3302 or SPAN 3308 .
Description
Acquisition of vocabulary, grammar and cultural knowledge necessary for the health professional, by providing skills to communicate effectively with the Spanish-speaking clientele. Writing, listening, reading and speaking practice.
Additional Fee Y
