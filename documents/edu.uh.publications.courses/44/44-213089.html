SPAN 3374 - Span American Culture & Civ

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly SPAN 4374

Description
Survey of the cultural history of Spanish America. Analysis of major cultural, social, literary, and political topics.
