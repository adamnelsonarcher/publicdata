ARCH 2351 - History of the Built Environment II

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: ARCH 2350.
Corequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in ENGL 1302.
Description
Second part of a two-semester chronological history of the global human-impacted environment, with attention to social, cultural, philosophical, technical, and environmental issues.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
