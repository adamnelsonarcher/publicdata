GEOL 4332 - Applications of GPS & LIDAR

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: PHYS 2326 and GEOL 4330.
Description
Principles of Global Positioning System (GPS) and Light Detection And Ranging (LIDAR); hands-on experience on field instrumentation, data collection, and data processing.
Repeatability: No

Note: Some previous GIS experience useful. Credit may not be applied for both GEOL 4332 & GEOL 6323.
Additional Fee: Y
