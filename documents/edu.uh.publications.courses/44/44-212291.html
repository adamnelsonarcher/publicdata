MATH 1314 - College Algebra

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
Quadratic equations, inequalities, logarithmic and exponential functions, graphs, elements of theory of equations, systems of equations.
Repeatability: Yes

Note: This course is designed to prepare students for MATH 2312 Pre-calculus and MATH 2413 Calculus I. Students with prior credit in MATH 2312 or MATH 2413 will not be allowed to enroll or receive credit in MATH 1314.
Core Category: (20) Core-Mathematics
TCCNS Equivalent: MATH 1314
Additional Fee: Y
