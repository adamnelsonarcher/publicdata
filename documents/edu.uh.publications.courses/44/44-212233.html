MANA 4353 - Mgmt Training and Career Devel

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MANA 3335 , or equivalent and MANA 4330 or equivalent; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Practices and trends in training and development in organizations, including skills training, coaching and mentoring, leadership training, and motivational techniques.
