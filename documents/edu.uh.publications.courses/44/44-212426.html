MECT 2354 - Introduction to Mechanics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: Grade of C- or better in PHYS 1301 and PHYS 1101 and MATH 2413 or equivalents.
Description
Static equilibrium conditions of forces, moments, friction, centroids, trusses, and moments of inertia.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
