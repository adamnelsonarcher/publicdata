SOC 3324 - Hispanic Identity

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOCI 1301.
Description
Historical, sociocultural, and structural aspects of the construction of identity among Hispanics in contemporary U.S. society.
