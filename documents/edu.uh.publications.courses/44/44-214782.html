GHL 3383 - Career Fair Management

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Must be a HRMA major and consent of instructor. In this course students gain the knowledge, skills and abilities to plan, execute, and evaluate an industry event.
Description
Responsibilities include marketing, employee/vendor relations, human resources, and operations. This includes the creation of professional development for their peers.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
