CHEE 2331 - Chemical Processes

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEM 1312, ENGI 1100, ENGI 1331, MATH 2414 or 1451, and PHYS 2325.
Description
Introduction to chemical engineering, chemical engineering calculations, unit equations, process stoichiometry, material and energy balances, states of matter, and case studies.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y Fee Type Engineering Equipment Access Fee
