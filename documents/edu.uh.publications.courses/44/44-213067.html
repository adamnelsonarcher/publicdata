SPAN 2308 - Spanish for Heritage Learners II

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Completion of SPAN 2307 with a minimum of C- or placement exam.
Description
Continued development of reading and writing skills, and control of formal Spanish.
Repeatability: No

Note: Credit for both SPAN 2308 and SPAN 2312 will not apply to a degree. Designed for students from a Spanish-speaking background.
Additional Fee: Y
