MAS 2342 - Introduction to Research and Policy in Latino/a Studies

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 1314 or equivalent.
Description
Introduction to research techniques for the design, implementation, and monitoring of public policies emphasizing the methodological, cultural, and logistical issues in Latino/a studies.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
