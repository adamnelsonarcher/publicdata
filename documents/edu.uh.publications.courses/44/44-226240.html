CHEM 1322 - Honors Fundamentals of Chemistry 2

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 2312 (formerly MATH 1330) or equivalent and CHEM 1321. Credit may not be applied toward a degree for both CHEM 1322 and CHEM 1311 or CHEM 1312.
Description
Intensive systematic study of the general principles, fundamental laws, equilibrium, kinetics, electrochemistry, and elementary inorganic, nuclear, and organic chemistry.
Repeatability: No

Note: Course is strongly recommended for science and engineering majors. CHEM 1311 does not satisfy the prerequisite for CHEM 1322.
Core Category: (30) Core-Life & Physical Sciences
Additional Fee: No
