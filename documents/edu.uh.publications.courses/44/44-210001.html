ARCH 2500 - Architecture and Interior Architecture Design Studio III

Credit Hours: 5.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 9
Prerequisite: ARCH 1501 and 1358 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in ARCH 2327 .
Description
Introduction to architectural design strategies through a series of small scale design problems. Students must pass a portfolio review to continue the design studio sequence.
Additional Fee: Y Fee Type Lab Fee
