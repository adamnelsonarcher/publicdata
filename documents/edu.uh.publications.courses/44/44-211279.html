ENGL 4364 - Minorities in Lit

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 or equivalent.
Description
The minority as subject and symbol in selected literature, concentrating on one or more of the following groups: the Black, the Chicano, the Jew, the woman, and the American Indian.
Repeatability: May be repeated once with the approval of the chair if the particular topic changes.

Additional Fee: Y
