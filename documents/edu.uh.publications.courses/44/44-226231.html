BIOE 4308 - Neural Engineering Methods and Applications

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Corequisite: BIOE 3340 / BIOE 3140.
Description
Survey of methodologies currently used to interrogate and modulate the nervous system; neural ensemble recording and neural stimulation; advanced techniques including modulation by optogenomics and the development of new voltage fluorescent probes will be explained. The use of neural prosthesis for the restoration of sensory and motor function.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
