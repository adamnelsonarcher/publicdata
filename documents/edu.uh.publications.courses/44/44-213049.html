SOC 3390 - Sociology of Gender

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOCI 1301.
Description
Contemporary gender role stereotypes and their effects on childhood socialization, major social institutions, interpersonal relationships; social movements and changing gender roles.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
