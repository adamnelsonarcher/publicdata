PHYS 4360 - Suborbital Methods in Space and Atmospheric Physics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: MATH 2413 Calculus I.
Description
Inquiry-based learning research projects in contemporary space and atmospheric physics for design and testing of payloads flown on balloons of suborbital and CubeSat research experiments.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
