BIOL 4350 - The Biology of Animal Signaling

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BIOL 1306, BIOL 1307, and BIOL 3301.
Description
How animal signaling is shaped by sensory modality, habitat structure, and the degree of shared interest between signaler and receiver.
Repeatability: No
