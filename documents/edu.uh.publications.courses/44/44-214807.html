WCL 4382 - Post-Conflict Literatures: Europe, Africa and the Americas

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: WCL 2351 or permission from instructor.
Description
This course studies literature produced out of political conflict in Europe, Africa and the Americas.
