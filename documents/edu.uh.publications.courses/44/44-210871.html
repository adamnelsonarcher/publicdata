SPEC 4364 - Education of Students with Gifts and Talents

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly CUIN 4363 Gifted and Talented Instruction
Prerequisite: Admission to a Teacher Education/Certification program.
Description
Issues, principles, instruction, and assessment related to differentiating curriculum for students with gifts and talents.
Repeatability: No
