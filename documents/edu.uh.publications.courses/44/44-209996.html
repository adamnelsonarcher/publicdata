ARCH 2350 - History of the Built Environment I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in ENGL 1302.
Description
First part of a two-semester chronological history of the global human-impacted environment, with attention to social, cultural, philosophical, technical, and environmental issues.
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (50) Core-Creative Arts
Additional Fee: Yes
