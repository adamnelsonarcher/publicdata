MECT 3341 - Computer-Aided Drafting

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: C- or better in ELET 2300 or ENGI 1331 or equivalents and MATH 2312.
Description
Computer-aided drafting and design (CAD) systems and computer graphics hardware and software. Selection and evaluation of CAD systems.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
