GERM 2111 - Intrmediate Convrs Ger

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GERM 1304 or GERM 2502, or equivalent (placement test score).
Description
Intermediate level with intensive practice in spoken German through guided conversation and discussion of contemporary topics, using visual materials.
Repeatability: No

Note: May not be used to satisfy second year language requirement. May be taken concurrently with GERM 2311; GERM 2312.
Additional Fee: Y
