PSYC 3334 - Psychology & Law

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301, PSYC 2305, PSYC 2317 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Presentation and critique of research n human behavior in, or regulated by, legal institutions. Includes a survey of criminal procedure and an analysis of the legal techniques for predicting dangerousness and inferring intent.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
