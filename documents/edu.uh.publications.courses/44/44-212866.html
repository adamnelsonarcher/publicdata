POLS 3387 - Politics of the Qur’an

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2305 and GOVT 2306.
Description
In this course, participants will be asked to examine the political theory implicit in the Qur¿an, and how the text motivates certain understandings of the nature and aims of the political world.
Repeatability: No
