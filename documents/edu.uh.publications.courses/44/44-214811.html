BTIS 3320 - US Border Management and Policy

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BTIS 3300.
Description
Foundational understanding of the history of borders and boundaries, sovereignty of nations, and the geopolitical and socioeconomic implications under different case scenarios.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
