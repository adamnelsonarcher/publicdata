MUED 3320 - Introduction to Education in Music

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: Admission as music major by audition and admission to teacher education program.
Description
Principles of teaching and learning music in education settings; focus on teaching/learning processes; structure, organization, and management of schools. School observations will be made in accordance with state-mandated, site-based requirements.
Additional Fee: Y
