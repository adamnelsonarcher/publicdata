SCLT 4385 - Sustainable Supply Chain and Logistics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: C or better in both SCLT 3384 and SCLT 3385.
Description
Environmental impact of logistics and supply chain operations, future trends and strategies and the management of change. Performance measures and monitoring.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
