ARCH 1358 - Introduction to Design Culture

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.
Description
Introduction to concepts, events, and figures across the design disciplines, including architecture, interior architecture, and industrial design. Lectures and readings address the history and culture of design, its major texts and arguments, and how these have shaped design discourse and design thinking.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
