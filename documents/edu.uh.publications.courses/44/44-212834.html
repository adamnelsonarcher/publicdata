POLS 3353 - Policy & Administration

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly POLS 3389
Prerequisite: GOVT 2306 and GOVT 2305 or equivalent, or consent of instructor.
Description
The role of administrative agencies in policy formation and implementation. Topics include agency organization, procedures, political influences, interpersonal behavior, and compliance. (Public Administration category.)
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
