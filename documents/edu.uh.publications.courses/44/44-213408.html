BIOE 1331 - Computing for Biomedical Engineering

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly BIOE 1397 Computing for Biomedical Engineering
Prerequisite: MATH 2413 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in BIOE 1100.
Description
Introduction to computing, data types and operations; Matlab-based programming constructs, algorithms, and biomedical applications; computing tools for biomedical engineering problem-solving.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
