THEA 3371 - History of Architecture, Interiors & Decorative Arts

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: DRAM 1330, DRAM 2331, THEA 2373, and consent of instructor.
Description
Survey of the styles of architecture, decor, and furniture from antiquity to the present, with emphasis on applications in theatrical design.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
