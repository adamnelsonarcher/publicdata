SPEC 4362 - Behavior: Evidence-Based Decisions

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in EDUC 2301.
Description
Behavioral observation, assessment, data collection tools, and evidenced-based decisions are presented and practiced. Behavioral analyses are applied in a variety of school, home, and professional settings.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
