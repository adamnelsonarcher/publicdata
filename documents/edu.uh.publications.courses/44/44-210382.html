CHEE 4321 - Chemical Engineering Design I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CHEE 3300, CHEE 3333, CHEE 3369, CHEE 3462, ECON 2302, ENGI 2304, and credit for or concurrent enrollment in CHEE 4367.
Description
Design of chemical processes with emphasis on health and safety, and environment aspects; mass and energy balances; equipment design; process economics; profitability analysis; and optimum operating conditions.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y Fee Type Engineering Equipment Access Fee
