CHIN 2311 - Intermediate Chinese I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Completion of CHIN 1502 completed with minimum grade of C- in 12 months immediately prior to enrollment or placement by examination immediately prior to enrollment.
Description
Introduction to modern spoken and written Mandarin Chinese.
Repeatability: No

Note: Restricted to nonnative speakers of Chinese.
TCCNS Equivalent: CHIN 2311
Additional Fee: Y
