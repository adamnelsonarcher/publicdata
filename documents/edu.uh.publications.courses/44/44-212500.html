MUSI 1121 - Concert Chorus

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Audition required.
Description
Choruses for singers with music-reading skills. Section 1: This chorus consists of soprano and alto voices only. Section 2: This chorus consists of tenor and bass voices only.
Repeatability: May be repeated for credit.

Note: May be repeated for credit.
