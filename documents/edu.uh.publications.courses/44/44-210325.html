BIOE 3341 - Biothermodynamics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in BIOE 3340 .
Description
Biological thermodynamic systems; heat and work; properties of pure substances; and first, second, and third thermodynamic laws.
