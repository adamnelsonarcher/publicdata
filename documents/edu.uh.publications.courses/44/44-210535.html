CLAS 3375 - Roman, Jew, and Christian

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Political and social interactions of religions in the Roman Empire ca. 31 BC to 150 A.D. Special focus on emperor worship, Second Temple Judaism and the Jewish War of 66-70, and the early Jesus movement.
Repeatability: No

Note: Taught in English.
Additional Fee: Y
