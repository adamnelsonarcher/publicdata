TLIM 3350 - Emergency Management Principles

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: C or better in TLIM 3340 .
Description
Emergency management, preparedness, mitigation, response, and recovery; integration of local, state, federal and public sector resources; roles and responsibilities of law enforcement, fire service, emergency medical service, public works, public health, and others.
Additional Fee: N
