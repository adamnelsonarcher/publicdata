COSC 4372 - Fundamentals of Medical Imaging

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: A grade of C- or better in COSC 2320 or COSC 2436.
Description
Medical imaging modalities; principles, data collection, and image reconstruction. X-rays, CT, ultrasound, and MRI. Simulations of image generation.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
