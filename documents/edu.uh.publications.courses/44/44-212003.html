INDS 3501 - Industrial Design Studio VI

Credit Hours: 5
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 9
Prerequisite: INDS 2501, INDS 3500, PHYS 1301, PHYS 1302 and successful portfolio review at the conclusion of INDS 2501.
Description
Integration of diverse design methods and techniques to develop innovative products. Analysis of product content and context, including social, cultural, and environmental elements.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
