MECE 4340 - Mechanical Engineering Capstone I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: MECE 3245 , MECE 3338 , MECE 3345 , MECE 3360 , MECE 3381 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in MECE 4331 and MECE 4364 .
Description
Students propose and begin team projects offered by industry and faculty. Professionalism, research methodologies, and technical communications.
