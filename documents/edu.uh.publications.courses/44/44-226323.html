MAS 2341 - Introduction to Multicultural Business Principles

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 1314 or equivalent.
Description
Introduction to Multicultural Business Principles is designed to provide students with the fundamental principles and practical skill sets to understand the diverse cultures that comprise the workplace of the 21st century. Participants discover “cultural proficiency”, the cutting-edge approach to successfully navigating the intense diversity challenges of today’s interdependent business community.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
