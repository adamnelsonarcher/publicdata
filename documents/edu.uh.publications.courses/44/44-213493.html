MECE 3381 - Introduction to Finite Element Methods for Mechanical Engineers

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECE 3369 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in MATH 3363 .
Description
Introduction to theory and practice of the finite element method. One-dimensional, two-dimensional, and three-dimensional elements in selected applications are studied. Extensive work with commercial FEM software.
