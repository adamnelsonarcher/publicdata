BUSI 4305 - Business Ethics for Accountants

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Good academic standing; and at least one of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Ethical principles in business and accounting, focusing on the origins of ethical thinking (history/philosophy), modern day ethical issues (real business cases), and the professional ethical standards applicable to CPAs today.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
