TLIM 3340 - Organizational Leadership and Supervision

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly TELS 3340.
Prerequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in TLIM 3363 (or WID for non majors).
Description
The supervisory process. Established practices and supporting knowledge of supervisory planning, leading, organizing, and controlling.
