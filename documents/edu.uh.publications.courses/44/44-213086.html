SPAN 3345 - Hispanic Folklore of the Southwest

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SPAN 3301 or SPAN 3307 and SPAN 3302 or SPAN 3308 .
Description
An introduction to the folklore of the people of Hispanic descent in the Southwest United States.
