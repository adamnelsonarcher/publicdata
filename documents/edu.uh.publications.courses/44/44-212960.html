RELS 2360 - Introduction to Buddhism

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 or ENGL 1310.
Description
Introduction to Buddhist thought, practice, culture(s), and history. Traces the historical development of Buddhism and examines a variety of Buddhist traditions. Assessments for Social Responsibility and Personal Responsibility.
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (81) Core-Writing in Discipline WID
Additional Fee: Y
