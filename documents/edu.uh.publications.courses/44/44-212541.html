MUSI 3303 - Pop Music of Americas Sn 1840

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Historical survey of popular music styles from the U.S., Mexico, the Caribbean, and Latin America from 1840 to the present.
Repeatability: No
