POLS 3318 - Intro To Public Policy

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2306 and GOVT 2305 or equivalent, or consent of instructor.
Description
The nature and functions of public policy, policy institutions and processes, and policy evaluations with a primary focus on the United States. (Introductory category.)
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
