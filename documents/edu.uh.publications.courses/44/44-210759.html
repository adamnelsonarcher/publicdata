COSC 4355 - Introduction to Ubiquitous Computing

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: A grade of C- or better in COSC 4353.
Description
For Computer Science majors and minors only. Introduction to software development for ubiquitous devices such as PDAs and cell phones.
Repeatability: No

Typically Offered: Fall

Additional Fee: Y
