CHIN 1501 - Elementary Chinese I

Credit Hours: 5
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Description
Introduction to modern spoken and written Mandarin Chinese. Emphasis on oral skills with initial presentation of the written language.
Repeatability: No

Note: Restricted to nonnative speakers of Chinese.
TCCNS Equivalent: CHIN 1511
Additional Fee: Y
