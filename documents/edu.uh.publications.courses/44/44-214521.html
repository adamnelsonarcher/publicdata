NURS 4211 - Mental Health Concepts of Care

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 30 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: NURS 3310 , NURS 3311 , NURS 3315 , NURS 4521 .
Corequisite: NURS 4213 Credit for or Concurrent Enrollment in: NURS 3312 , NURS 3313 .
Description
This course focuses on utilization of the nursing process as a systematic approach to symptom management and evidence based care of patients with alterations in mental health.
Additional Fee: N
