MATH 3334 - Advanced Multivariable and Vector Calculus

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 2318 and MATH 3333 (or concurrent enrollment in the latter by permission of instructor).
Description
Basic theory underlying multivariable and vector calculus, with applications. Topics include topology of n-space, derivative of a multivariable function as a linear transformation and its many applications, multivariable Taylor theorem, the inverse and implicit function theorems; calculus of vector fields and vector differential operators, Stokes and Gauss integral theorems, physical applications.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
