CHIN 3346 - Sociolinguistic Perspectives on Contemporary China

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
This course explores various aspects of the contemporary Chinese society from sociolinguistic perspectives.
