SOC 3371 - Sociology of the Family

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: three semester hours in sociology or anthropology, or consent of instructor.
Description
The family as an institution; general structures and functions; variation and types of American society.
