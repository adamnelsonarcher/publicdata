SOC 3392 - Sociology of Latino in Houston

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly SOC 3397 Sociology of Latinos in Houston; Soc 3397 Hispanics in Houston
Prerequisite: SOCI 1301 or consent of instructor.
Description
A sociological examination of Mexican Americans and other Latinos in Houston focusing upon history, demographics, education, politics, culture, and relations with other groups in the city of Houston.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
