ARCH 2331 - Digital Vocabularies

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Major in the College of Architecture and Design, or permission from advisor.
Description
Introduction to basic and intermediate concepts of digital drafting and modeling using parametric software, as well as exploring methods of digital fabrication and production.
Additional Fee: N
