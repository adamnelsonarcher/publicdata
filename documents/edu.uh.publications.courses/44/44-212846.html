POLS 3360 - Politics & Mass Media

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2306 and GOVT 2305 or equivalent, or consent of instructor.
Description
Impact of the mass media coverage on American political institutions, the election process, and public opinion in general and the appropriate role of media and news in a society. (American Politics category.)
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
