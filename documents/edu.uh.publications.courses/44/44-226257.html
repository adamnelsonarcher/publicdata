GHL 3155 - Event Planning

Credit Hours: 1
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Consent of instructor.
Description
This course offers an in-depth experience to the researching, planning, coordinating, marketing, management, execution, and evaluation of special events.
