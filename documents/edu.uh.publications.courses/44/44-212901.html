PSYC 2306 - Psychology of Human Sexuality

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly PSYC 3337
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301, PSYC 2305, PSYC 2317 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Empirical studies regarding adult sexual behaviors, the physiology of sexual behaviors and sexual disorders.
Repeatability: No

TCCNS Equivalent: PSYC 2306
Additional Fee: Yes
