INDE 3330 - Financial and Cost Management

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGI 1331.
Description
Methods for collecting, organizing, presenting, interpreting, managing, and controlling of financial and cost management related information for decision making in enterprises.
