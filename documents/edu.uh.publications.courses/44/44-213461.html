INAR 2501 - Interior Architecture Design Studio IV

Credit Hours: 5.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 9
Prerequisite: ARCH 2500 and credit for or concurrent enrollment in ARCH 2328 and passing portfolio review.
Description
Development of design process and communication media; qualifying and quantifying aspects of interior building design.
