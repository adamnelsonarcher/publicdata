MANA 3335 - Introduction to Organizational Behavior and Management

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
General management functions and the role of individual, group, and organizational behavior in the management process.
Note: Credit for both MANA 3335 and MANA 3333: 3334 cannot be applied toward a degree.
