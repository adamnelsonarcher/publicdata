COSC 3360 - Fundamentals of Operating Systems

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COSC majors and minors: A grade of C- or better in COSC 2436 and COSC 2425; CpE majors: A grade of C- or better in COSC 2320 AND ECE 5367.
Description
Operating system; sequential processes, concurrent processes, deadlock, mutual exclusion, semaphores; memory management, processor management, peripheral device management.
Repeatability: No

Note: COSC majors and minors and CpE majors only.
Additional Fee: Y
