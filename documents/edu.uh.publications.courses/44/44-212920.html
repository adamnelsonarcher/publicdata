PSYC 2320 - Abnormal Psychology

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly PSYC 4321
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301 and six additional semester hours in psychology.
Description
Behaviors considered deviant or pathological and the various approaches to understanding and treating them.
