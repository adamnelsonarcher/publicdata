MECE 4372 - Mechanics-Controls-Vibration Laboratory

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.5 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: MECE 3360, MECE 3338.
Description
Experimental methods to study dynamic response of structures and process systems. Design of control systems and modification of laboratory hardware.
