INDE 4331 - Analysis of Industrial Activities

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGI 2304 , INDE 3364 , and INDE 3432 .
Description
Analysis of production systems to determine systems capacity and capacities in situations involving complex mechanical-human interactions. Establishment of production standards. Problems in instrumentation and recording.
