ENGI 2304 - Technical Communications for Engineers

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGI 1100, ENGL 1302, engineering undergraduate standing, and completion of one of the following courses: ECE 2201, CHEE 2331, ENGR 2301, INDE 2333, MECE 2336, PETR 2311 or permission of the Director of Undergraduate Programs.
Description
Engineering communication skills: written proposals, specifications, progress reports, and technical reports; individual and group oral presentations; essays on engineering ethics, contemporary engineering issues and the impact of engineering decisions.
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (81) Core-Writing in Discipline WID
Additional Fee: Y Fee Type Engineering Equipment Access Fee
