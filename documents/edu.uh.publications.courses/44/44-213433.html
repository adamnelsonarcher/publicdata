MATH 3325 - Transition to Advanced Mathematics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly MATH 3397 Transitions to Advanced Math
Prerequisite: Credit for MATH 1451 or MATH 2415 or MATH 2318 or MATH 3321 with a grade of C- or higher.
Description
An introduction to proof in mathematics: logic, sets, relations, functions and cardinality, a first look at epsilon-delta methods of proof. Writing and communication of mathematical ideas will be emphasized.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
