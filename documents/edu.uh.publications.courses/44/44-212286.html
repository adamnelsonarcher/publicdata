MARK 4390 - Marketing for Non-Profit Orgs

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MARK 3336 ; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Marketing for nonprofit organizations that are both mission and market driven.
Note: Taught as a practicum involving projects for nonprofit organizations.
