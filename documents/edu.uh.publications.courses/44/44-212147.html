JAPN 1502 - Elementary Japanese II

Credit Hours: 5
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: Completion of JAPN 1501 in the twelve months immediately prior to enrollment or by placement exam.
Description
Expansion of the listening and oral skills previously acquired. Listening comprehension, oral communication, reading and writing.
Repeatability: No

TCCNS Equivalent: JAPN 1512
Additional Fee: Y
