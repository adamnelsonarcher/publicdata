NURS 4322 - Policy and Ethics in Professional Nursing Practice

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: NURS 3310 or NURS 3230 .
Description
This course focuses on policy, legal, ethical and advocacy issues affecting health care and professional nursing practice.
