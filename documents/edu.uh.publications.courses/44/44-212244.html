BCIS 1305 - Business Computer Applications

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly DISC 3300
Prerequisite: None.
Description
Fundamental concepts of information systems used in business including the use of computer tools to aid in business decision making.
Note: BCIS 1305 is included in the Business Administration and Management Field of Study. Students under the AY2020 or later undergraduate catalog are not allowed credit for both BCIS 1305 and MIS 3300.
TCCNS Equivalent: BCIS 1305
