POLS 2346 - Politics of Greek Theater

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2306 and GOVT 2305 or equivalent, or consent of instructor.
Description
A study of the influence of Greek comedy and tragedy on the politics of democratic Athens. (Introductory category.)
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (50) Core-Creative Arts
