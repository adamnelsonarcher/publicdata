MANA 4355 - Selection and Staffing

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MANA 3335 , or equivalent, and MANA 4330 or equivalent; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Selection and staffing methods and practices in organizations, including evaluation of the impact of selection on individual and organizational performance.
