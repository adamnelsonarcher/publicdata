ENGL 2308 - Intro-Non-Fiction Prose

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 or equivalent.
Description
Study of literary works of nonfiction prose, centering on important aspects of the Western tradition. Emphasis on composition.
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (40) Core-Language, Philosophy & Culture
Additional Fee: Y
