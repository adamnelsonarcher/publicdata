MATH 2451 - Accelerated Calculus II

Credit Hours: 4
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 2
Prerequisite: MATH 2450.
Description
The second course in an accelerated calculus sequence. Includes topics normally covered in MATH 2414 and MATH 2415.
Repeatability: No

Note: Students with credit for MATH 2451 may not enroll in or receive credit for either MATH 2414 or MATH 2415.
Core Category: (90) Core-Math/Reasoning, Component Area Option
Additional Fee: Yes
