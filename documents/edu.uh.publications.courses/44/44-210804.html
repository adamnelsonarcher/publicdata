CNST 4331 - Construction Management II

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: C or better in CNST 3205, CNST 3301, CNST 3331 and CNST 3351.
Description
Capstone course to develop a formal construction proposal for a construction project. The American Institute of Constructors (AIC Level 1 Associate Constructors (AC exam is required. Students are responsible for the AIC exam fees.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
