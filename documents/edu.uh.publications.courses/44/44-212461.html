MECT 4384 - Lean Manufacturing Systems

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: Grade of C- or better in MECT 3360 or equivalent.
Description
Manufacturing systems including layout, maintenance, material handling, and controls. Lean manufacturing, cost analysis, and manufacturing economics.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
