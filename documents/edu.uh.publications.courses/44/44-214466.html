COSC 4346 - Security Analytics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: A grade of C- or better in MATH 2318, MATH 3339, and COSC 2436. Review of security problems including malware, phishing and intrusions.
Description
Unique challenges of security domain. Adaptations and Applications of Statistical, Data Mining, Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning techniques to security.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: N
