TLIM 3345 - Human Resources in Technology

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly TELS 3345.
Prerequisite: Credit for or concurrent enrollment in TLIM 3340 or equivalent.
Description
Functions of personnel administration in human resource development, employee-supervisor relationships, interviewing, recruiting and selection techniques, and training programs.
Additional Fee: Y
