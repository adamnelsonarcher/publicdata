INDS 4361 - E-Portfolio

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: INDS 3500 or consent of instructor.
Description
Establish a portfolio strategy, storyline, artist statement, skill evaluation and action plan to gain employment in the industrial design field. Creating a consistent visual language and identity for content across interactive digital and print media.
Additional Fee: Y Fee Type Lab Fee
