WCL 4352 - Frames of Modernity II - Postmodernity and Globalization

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Major cultural trends in World Cultures from WWII t the present time: Existentialism, Structuralism, Postmodernism, Deconstruction, Feminism, Cultural Studies, Postcolonial Studies, Globalization Studies, and Cultural Perspectives from Non-Western Areas.
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (81) Core-Writing in Discipline WID
Additional Fee: Y
