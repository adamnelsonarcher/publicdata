POLS 3324 - Gov & Pol of Contemp Germany

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2306 and GOVT 2305 or equivalent or consent of instructor.
Description
Historical and geographical perspectives on recurrent issues in contemporary German politics, such as territorial boundaries, strength of the central state, and appropriate institutions for ensuring democratic stability. (Comparative Politics category.)
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
