ARCH 3374 - World Cities

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Critical exploration of cities of the world from different geographical, chronological and disciplinary perspectives. Development of analytical tools for understanding complex urban phenomena.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
