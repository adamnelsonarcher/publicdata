BUSI 3301 - Connecting College to Business

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly GENB 3301 Effectiveness Skills
Prerequisite: Junior standing, ENGL 1302 or equivalent and classified as a non-business major.
Description
Effective communication and professional skills and exploration of business careers for non-business majors.
Repeatability: No

Note: Students who change their major to business with credit for BUSI 3301 (formerly GENB 3301) may substitute it for BUSI 3302 (formerly GENB 3302) and must meet the business writing evaluation requirement. Credit not given for both BUSI 3302 (formerly GENB 3302) and BUSI 3301 (formerly GENB 3301).
Additional Fee: Y
