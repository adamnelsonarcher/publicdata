HDCS 3303 - Retailing and Consumer Science

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1301 or equivalent and MATH 1314, or consent of instructor.
Description
Interdisciplinary analysis and comparison of retailing and consumer science. Psychology of consumer choice and the structure of the retailing industry.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
