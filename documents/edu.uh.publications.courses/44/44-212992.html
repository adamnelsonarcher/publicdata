RUSS 3304 - Post-Soviet Russia

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: RUSS 2312 or equivalent.
Description
Study of contemporary Russian culture and society through film, television, and the popular press. Taught in Russian.
Repeatability: No
