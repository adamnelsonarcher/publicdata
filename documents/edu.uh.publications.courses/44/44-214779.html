HIST 4373 - History of the US-Mexico Borderlands

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1304, and junior standing or higher.
Description
This seminar takes a multidisciplinary approach to understanding the history and development of the U.S. Â¿ Mexico Borderlands. The course starts with the proposition that borders themselves are social/historical creations and can be analyzed as such.
