MUED 2342 - Music for Children

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly MUSI 2342.
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Study covers the elements of music, and musical materials and teaching concepts appropriate for children in grades K-4.
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (50) Core-Creative Arts
Additional Fee: Y
