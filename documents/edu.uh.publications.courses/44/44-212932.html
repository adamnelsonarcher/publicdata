PSYC 4372 - Interviewing

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301, PSYC 2305, PSYC 2317 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Skill development in personnel interviewing; includes tape-recorded practice interviews and discussion of theoretical issues and research findings.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
