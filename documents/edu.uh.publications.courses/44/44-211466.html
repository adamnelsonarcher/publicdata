GEOL 3383 - Remote Sensing

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: GEOL 1303, MATH 2414, and PHYS 2326, or consent of instructor.
Description
Principles of remote sensing, data collection, digital image processing, and applications in geologic, environmental, and land use studies.
Repeatability: No

Note: Counts as advanced geophysics elective. Credit may not be received for both GEOL 3383 and GEOL 6325.
Additional Fee: Y
