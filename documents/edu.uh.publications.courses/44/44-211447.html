GEOL 3338 - Environmental Hydrogeology

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Declared major in the Department of Earth and Atmospheric Sciences; GEOL 3370 or GEOL 3325, PHYS 2325, and MATH 2413, or consent of instructor.
Description
Principles of groundwater and surface water flow and interaction with the environment: water quality and pollution.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Y
