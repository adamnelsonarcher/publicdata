PSYC 4399 - Senior Honors Thesis

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301, PSYC 2305, PSYC 2317 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Qualified student selects faculty member to supervise the topic choice and writing of the thesis.
Repeatability: No

Note: PSYC 3399 and 4399 must be satisfied in order for any to apply to a degree.
Additional Fee: Yes
