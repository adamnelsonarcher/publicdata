GHL 4372 - Global Hospitality Leadership: Asian Community

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Description
The course offers an in-depth examination of factors that contribute to successful leadership practice in the hospitality industry, especially in Asia. Topics include what leadership is, the challenges leaders face, the impact leadership has and how leaders build organizations and key relationships. Emphasis is placed on leadership knowledge, approach, and application.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
