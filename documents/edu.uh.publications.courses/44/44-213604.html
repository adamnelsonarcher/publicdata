RELS 3382 - Politics and Religion in South Asia

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Focus on ethno-religious conflicts in South Asia from a historical and contemporary perspective. Effect of religion on electoral and party politics of nations in South Asia.
Repeatability: No

Typically Offered: Fall

Additional Fee: N
