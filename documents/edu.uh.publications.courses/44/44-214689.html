SOC 3301 - Introduction to Sociological Research

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOC 3400 or equivalent. Preference given to declared major or minor in SOC.
Description
The logic of social research and an overview of foundational methods in the social sciences.
