ENGL 4374 - Literature and Alienation: Alienation Then and Now

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302 or equivalent.
Description
Selection of novels, stories, poems, plays, music, and films that explore the ethics of alienation, resistance, and critique.
