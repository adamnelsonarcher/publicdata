PSYC 4301 - Psychology and the Arts

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PSYC 2301, PSYC 2305, PSYC 2317 (or equivalent), or consent of instructor.
Description
Explores the arts as source material for understanding those parts of the psyche which are unknown, and how they become known.
Repeatability: No

Note: This course is an open Honors course.
Additional Fee: Yes
