MUSI 3218 - Advanced Jazz Theory

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUSI 3117 - Jazz Improvisation III and MUSI 3215 - Introduction to Large Forms.
Description
Presents elements of advanced Jazz theory: rhythm, harmony, melody, forms, and chord/scale relationships. This includes techniques of analysis, ear training and keyboard harmonizations.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
