MUTX 4600 - Internship in Music Therapy

Credit Hours: 6
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 18
Prerequisite: Completion of all MUTX coursework and approval of the Program Director.
Description
Under the supervision of a Board-Certified Music Therapist, students will hone and establish skills to meet the entry level competencies of the music therapy profession, through placement in one or more clinical settings approved by the AMTA. This is a 6 month, minimum 990 hour internship.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: No
