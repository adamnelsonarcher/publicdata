ARTH 1300 - Ways of Seeing: Art and Our Visual World

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1301.
Description
Examination of our visual world and the various ways in which that world has been analyzed and valued. May not be applied to a major or minor in art history or art.
Repeatability: No

Core Category: (40) Core-Language, Philosophy & Culture
Additional Fee: Y
