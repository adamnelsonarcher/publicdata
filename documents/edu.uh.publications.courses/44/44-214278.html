BIOL 1305 - Human Biology for the Informed Citizen

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 1314 or MATH 1332 or equivalent.
Description
The goal of this course is to introduce students to basic concepts in human biology so that they can apply their knowledge in their everyday lives as informed consumers and users of scientific information. The format of the course is inquiry based within the context of important cultural and social issues. This course will give students a greater appreciation for the sciences and enhance the studentâ€™s ability to make informed and ethical decisions about human biological issues that affect the global community.

Note: This course will not count toward Biology major requirements.
