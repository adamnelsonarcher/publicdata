MIS 4477 - Network and Security Infrastructure

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: MIS 3360 ; good academic standing; and one or more of the following: pre-business major with advisor approval, declared business major, or if required for a declared minor or special program.
Description
Coordination of the hardware and software components of data communications systems including laboratory experience.
