RELS 3339 - Secularisms and Atheisms

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 1302.
Description
Examines the non-religious history of the United States, the various forms of non-belief or alternative belief, and the everyday life of religious nones.
Repeatability: Yes

Additional Fee: N
