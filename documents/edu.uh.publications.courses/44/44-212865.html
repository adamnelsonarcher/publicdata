POLS 3384 - Morality and Politics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: GOVT 2305 and GOVT 2306.
Description
Approaches morality from a psychological perspective. Analyzes the origins, structure, and purpose of morality, as well as how our moral values and political views influence each other.
Repeatability: No

Additional Fee: Yes
