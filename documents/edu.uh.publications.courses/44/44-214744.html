COMM 4390 - Film Genres

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COMM 1307 or consent of instructor.
Description
Study of a specific genre of film (musicals, comedies, dramas, war, etc., or film eras) with emphasis on features and documentaries that have reflected or fueled public attitudes.
Repeatability: Genre will vary and the course may be taken twice for credit with different topics.

Additional Fee: N
