PHAR 5270 - Pharmacoeconomics and Hospital Pharmacy Management

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHAR 4260 and PHAR 4280 .

This course will introduces concepts of health outcomes and pharmacoeconomics to medication decision making and strategic hospital pharmacy management within the health care and medication use systems.
