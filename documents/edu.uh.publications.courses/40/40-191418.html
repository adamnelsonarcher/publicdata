FINA 7A40 - Fixed Income I

Credit Hours: 1.5
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.5 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and FINA 6A35 or FINA 6335.

This course focuses on (1) the basic concepts of fixed income securities such as yield, duration, and convexity; (2) the empirical techniques to describe the term structure of interest rates; (3) general understanding of the market for defaultable securities; (4) the valuation of defaultable securities.
