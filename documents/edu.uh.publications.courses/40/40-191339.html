SPEC 7395 - Educational Diagnostician Internship II

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: SPEC 7394 and consent of instructor.

This course is required for Educational Diagnostician certification and addresses TAC 239.83. Standards Required for the Educational Diagnostician Certificate. A minimum of 100 hours may be obtained. Primary activities for Internship II should include administration, scoring, and interpretation of norm-referenced tests.
