CUIN 7314 - Sociocultural Context and Policies in Bilingual Education

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: N/A.

Examines social, cultural, and political aspects of bilingual education in the United States and other countries. Cultural identity and differences, the politics of recognition and social justice, and the relationship to educational policy and practice will be discussed.
