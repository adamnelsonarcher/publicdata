HRMA 6372 - Global Hospitality Leadership: Asian Community

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

The course offers an in-depth examination of factors that contribute to successful leadership practice in the hospitality industry, especially in Asia. Topics include what leadership is, the challenges leaders face with the Asian culture, and how leaders build organizations and key relationships. Emphasis is placed on leadership knowledge, approach, and application in the Asian culture based hospitality industry.
