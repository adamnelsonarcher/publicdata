SOCW 8425 - Statistics and Data Analysis II

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Prerequisite: SOWC 8424 or permission from instructor.

Explores the application of multivariate statistics in social and behavioral sciences research via a weekly 3-hour lecture and 1-hour computer lab.
