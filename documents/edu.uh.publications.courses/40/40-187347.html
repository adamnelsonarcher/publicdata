PHAR 5211 - Advance Research & Drug Design

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PHAR 5210 , third year standing in the College of Pharmacy or consent of the instructor.

The student will take the information offered in Research and Drug Design and prepare a preclinical project for grant submission determining the hypothesis, rationale, budget, laboratory experiments and submit the proposal for mock IRB approval.
