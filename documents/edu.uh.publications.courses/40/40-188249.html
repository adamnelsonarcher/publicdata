SOCW 7373 - Human Sexuality & Sw

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Successful completion of foundation curriculum or consent of instructor.

Examines human sexuality from an integrated practice perspective and includes content relevant to children and families, aging, health, mental health, and political social work.
