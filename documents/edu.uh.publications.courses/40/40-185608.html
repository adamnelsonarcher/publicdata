CUIN 8352 - Technology Applications for Educational Leaders

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Admission in the CUIN Ph.D. program.

This course prepares educational leaders to direct and manage technological innovations in learning environments. Students in this course will explore, assess, and utilize a range of educational technologies that allow individuals and organizations to create, share, and deliver information in classrooms and in online environments.
