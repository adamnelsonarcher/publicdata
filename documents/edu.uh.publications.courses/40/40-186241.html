MECE 6339 - Introduction to Engineering Alloys

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECE 3345 or equivalent and graduate standing.

Metallurgy of alloy systems are covered at an introductory graduate level. Topics include structure of metals, defects, phase equilibria, phase transformations, heat treatment for microstructure property control.
