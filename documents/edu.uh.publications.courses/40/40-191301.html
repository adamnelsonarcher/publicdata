PHAR 5111 - Leadership and Interprofessional Competence

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

This course will foster professional self-awareness, capabilities, responsibilities, and leadership. It will also analyze contemporary practice roles, innovative opportunities, and inculcation of professional attitudes, and behaviors in developing interprofessional competence.
