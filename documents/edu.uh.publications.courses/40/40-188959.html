MATH 6385 - Mathematical Finance

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Probability.

This course will cover main topics in mathematical finance including Option pricing, binomial trees, risk-free valuation, Brownian motion and Black-Scholes model, volatility estimation.
