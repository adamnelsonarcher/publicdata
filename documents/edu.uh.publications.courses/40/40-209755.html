ENGL 8387 - Postmodern Fiction

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing in English or approval of chair of graduate studies in English.

This course explores the works of poststructural (and poststructural feminist) and postmodern theoreticians such as Michel Foucault, Jacques Derrida, Jean Baudrillard, Felex Guattari, Jacques Lacan, Helene Cixous, Luce Irigaray, and Jean Francois Lyotard in attempt to sketch out a culture beyond modernity and modernism.
