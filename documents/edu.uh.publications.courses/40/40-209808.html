NURS 8301 - Healthcare Research Design, Methods & Analysis

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3
Critically examines the science of healthcare research and models and methods for translation of research into practice. Students will explore the research continuum from basic scientific discovery to adoption of relevant research into clinical practice.
Note: Online Course Delivery
