INDE 6364 - Experimental Design and Regression

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: INDE 6364 - Advanced Engineering Statistics
This is a one-semester course in the design and analysis of experiments and concludes with the study of regression analysis. The material relies heavily on the analysis of variance (ANOVA) and the statistical estimation criterion of least squares.
