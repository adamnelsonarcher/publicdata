SOCW 8343 - Data Software and Analysis

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOCW 8425 .

This course will focus on how to utilize SPSS and other computer software programs for research in social work and social sciences.
