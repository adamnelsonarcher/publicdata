PETR 6314 - Pressure Transient Testing

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 6328 , 6351, 6362 or BS in Petroleum Engineering, PETR 6372 and PETR Graduate standing or Consent of Program.

Theory and application of pressure transient testing of oil and gas wells for determination of reservoir properties and near-well damage or stimulation.
