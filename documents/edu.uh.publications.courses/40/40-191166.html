CUIN 8367 - Core Concepts of Learning and Teaching

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Doctoral status.

Introduction to many of the core concepts of learning and instruction that are currently in use in health science education. These include learning theories, learning styles, feedback, cognitive load theory, small/large group teaching, deep/strategic/surface learning and basic principles of the neuroscience of learning and memory. Topics will be considered in detail, alongside their application to the context of health science education.
