OPTO 5315 - Optics II

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: OPTO 5111 and OPTO 5314 .

The nature of light, apertures and stops, optical instruments, photometry, dispersion, polarization, interference, diffraction, lasers, and modern optics.
