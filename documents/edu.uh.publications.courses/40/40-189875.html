MECE 6359 - Tribology I

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

Introduction to Tribology; Contact Surface Interactions; Thermal, Mechanical and Physical Properties of Materials Affecting Surface Interactions; Contact Mechanics; Friction; Wear; Lubrication; Adhesion; Nano Tribology; Methods of Improving Tribological Behavior of Sliding Surfaces.
