ENGL 6318 - Queer Theory

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing in English or approval of chair of graduate studies in English.

This course will examine the work of gay and lesbian studies that informs much of contemporary queer theory, selections from foundational essays on sexual orientation that provide the historical intellectual foundations of lesbian and gay studies allow for the exposure of the ongoing debates and assumptions between those who would argue for a lesbian/gay studies field and those who would argue for queer studies.
