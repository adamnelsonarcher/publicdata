CUIN 7302 - Community Education

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: none.

Community education offers community and place-based education experiences linked to curriculum and instruction. Problem and project-based activities and service learning are also integrated.
