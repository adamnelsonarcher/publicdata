OPTO 5335 - Ocular Anatomy and Physiology

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: OPTO 5133 , OPTO 5134 , OPTO 5233 , and OPTO 5334 .

Corequisite: Concurrent enrollment in OPTO 5135 .

Gross and microscopic anatomy, physiology and biochemistry of the eye and visual system with its associated circulation and neurology.
