PETR 6328 - Petroleum Fluid Property & Phase Equilibrium

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 6351 , 6362, 6364 and PETR Graduate standing or Consent of Program.

Volumetric behavior and equation of state representation of petroleum fluids; thermodynamic functions; conditions of phase equilibrium; phase behavior calculations; techniques for phase equilibrium measurements; equation of state tuning; advanced topics.
