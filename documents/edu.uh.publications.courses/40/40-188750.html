COSC 6377 - Computer Networks

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COSC 4377 or consent of the instructor.

Designing and specification of network protocols; advanced network protocols: broadcast, multicast, security, compression, congestion control, quality of service guarantees.
