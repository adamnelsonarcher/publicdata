FORE 6359 - Design Futures

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

Design futures combines futures thinking with design thinking in order to create tangible futures scenarios by learning through intellectual and practical “in use” approaches.
