CUIN 6314 - Spanish/English Bilingual Curriculum

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: CUIN 6313 or consent of instructor. Students must be fluent in Spanish.

Exploration of theories and methods of curriculum development in bilingual education as well as techniques for teaching content areas in Spanish.
Note: Readings will be in English and Spanish. All instruction will be in Spanish.
