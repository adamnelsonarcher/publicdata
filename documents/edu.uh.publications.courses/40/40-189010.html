MECT 6323 - Applied Heat Transfer

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECT 3331 and MECT 3318.

Heat Transfer Concepts, Heat Exchange Systems, Advanced vapor and gas power cycles, Refrigeration and Heat Pumps, HVAC systems, Turbomachinery Principles.
