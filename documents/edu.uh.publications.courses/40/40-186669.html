LAW 6348 - Texas Criminal Procedure

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
An in-depth analysis of the Texas Code of Criminal Procedure and case law interpreting the same. Where appropriate, the Texas Rules of Criminal Evidence and Texas Rules of Appellate Procedure will be addressed and discussed. There will be a comparative analysis of the mentioned rules and statutes with their federal counterparts.
