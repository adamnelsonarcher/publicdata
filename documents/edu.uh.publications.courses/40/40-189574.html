SOCW 7372 - Global Social Justice

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Successful completion of foundation curriculum.

Analysis of international political economy and human rights issues; rights and responsibilities of global citizenship; and understanding of activism strategies and skills for achieving social justice goals. Includes study of power and oppression as fundamental to understanding global social justice issues in a social work context.
