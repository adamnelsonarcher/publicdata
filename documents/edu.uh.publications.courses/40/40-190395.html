MECE 6386 - Computational Modeling of Materials

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Consent of instructor.

Computational methods for the modeling, analysis, and numerical simulation of mechanical response of materials from the atomistic scale to the continuum scale. Topic include molecular dynamics, finite element method, and multi-scale modeling techniques.
