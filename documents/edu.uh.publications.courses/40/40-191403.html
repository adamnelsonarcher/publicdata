ELET 6354 - Biomedical Image Analysis

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Linear Algebra, Programming, Matlab. Digital image processing from an operational, application oriented perspective.

Aspects of image acquisition, digitization, enhancement, restoration, segmentation and object measurement in practical applications will be covered.
