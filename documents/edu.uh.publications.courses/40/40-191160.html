CUIN 8326 - Math Education Leadership & Coaching

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: none.

To prepare doctoral students to coach classroom teachers in mathematics instruction and lead a mathematics program. Students will explore current issues in mathematics education related to leadership and coaching including models for coaching teachers, curriculum leadership, professional development and strategies to improve and support mathematics instruction and assessment. Course Notes: Admission to the PhD or EdD program in Curriculum and Instruction or approval of the instructor
