ECE 6345 - Microstrip Antennas

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ECE 6340 .

Introduction to microstrip antennas, overview of basic properties, CAD formulas, transmission-line model, cavity model, equivalent-circuit model, spectral-domain analysis, mutual coupling, segmentation methods.
