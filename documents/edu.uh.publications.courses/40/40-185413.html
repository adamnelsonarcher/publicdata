MARK 7374 - New Product Development

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and MARK 6361 or MARK 6A61 .

New product development and testing, business analysis for new products and applications of marketing planning models for successful introduction of new products.
