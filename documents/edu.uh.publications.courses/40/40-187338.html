PHAR 4421 - Organ Systems Life Sciences II

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 4 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: First year standing in the College of Pharmacy.

Corequisite: PHAR 4251 .

Principles of human anatomy, physiology and pathophysiology of gastrointestinal, cardiovascular, respiratory and renal systems with emphasis on those systems and disease states commonly encountered in the provision of pharmaceutical care.
