ACCT 7384 - Enterprise Risk Management

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing.

This course covers a range of internal audit topics and risk issues that are encountered in a business setting. Topics include risk management for financial, external and operational risks.
