PHLA 6240 - Regulatory Compliance

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing.

The goals of this course are to familiarize students with various regulatory bodies, resources, and processes in regulatory compliance. This course will specifically address practice, policy, and research issues in pharmacy performance improvement and regulatory compliance.
