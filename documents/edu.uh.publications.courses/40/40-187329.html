PHAR 4260 - Pharmacy Management I

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: First year standing in the College of Pharmacy.

Corequisite: PHAR 4250

Examines the laws, roles of medical personnel, and opportunities of pharmacists within the UH health care system.
