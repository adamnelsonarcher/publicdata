MECT 6317 - Applications in Stress Analysis

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and consent of the graduate faculty advisor.

This course addresses advanced applications of combined stress systems and non-symmetrical loadings; deformation analysis of thin and thick walled pressure vessels, beams, and columns of composite materials; failure theories, energy methods, fatigue, fracture, and impact applications; and an introduction to elasticity theory.
