LAW 5395 - Entrepreneurship and Community Development Clinic II

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: NONE.

Students represent entrepreneurs and small businesses in connection with entity formation, drafting and negotiating commercial contracts, raising capital, buying and selling equity and assets, working with employees and independent contractors, and other transactional business law matters.
