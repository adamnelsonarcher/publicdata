SOCW 7356 - Groups in Clinical Settings

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Successful completion of the MSW foundation.

Theories and concepts of therapeutic interventions with small groups emphasizing roles and skills of social workers in various mental health settings.
