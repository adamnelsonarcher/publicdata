ECE 6321 - Principles of Internetworking

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: graduate standing and consent of instructor.

Local area networks, Internet Protocol addressing, routing protocols, Transport Control Protocol flow, congestion and error control, Domain Name System, Dynamic Host Configuration Protocol, and Network Address Translation. Selected applications.
