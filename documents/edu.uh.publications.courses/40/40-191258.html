PHAR 5223 - Patient Safety

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Second year standing at UH College of Pharmacy or consent of instructor.

Introduction to Patient Safety and preparation for safe professional pharmacy practice through increasing student knowledge, application and awareness of patient safety principles as it relates to pharmacy practice.
