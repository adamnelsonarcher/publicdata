BIOL 6231 - Advanced Cell Biology II

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Undergraduate genetics, biochemistry, and cell biology.

Core course for Molecular and Cell Biology program. Topics include the cytoskeleton, cell motility, extracellular matrix, establishment and maintenance of epithelial cell polarity, and protein trafficking.
