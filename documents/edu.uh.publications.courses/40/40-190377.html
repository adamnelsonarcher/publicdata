LAW 6218 - Client Interviewing & Counseling

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

This course emphasizes a practice approach to learning with instructions on client-centered interviewing and counseling techniques and students will engage in mock interviews and counseling sessions.
