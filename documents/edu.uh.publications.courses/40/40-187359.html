PHAR 5280 - Therapeutics I

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Second year standing in the College of Pharmacy.

Corequisite: Co-requisites: PHAR 5203 , and PHAR 5403 .

Requires integration and application of pharmacological and biopharmaceutical principals to the treatment of specific disease states through clinical pharmacy practice and therapeutics.
