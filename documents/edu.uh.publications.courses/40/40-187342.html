PHAR 5206 - Pharmacy and Geriatrics

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Third year standing or consent of instructor.

Social, psychological and therapeutic factors involved in geriatric pharmacy practice; the role of the pharmacist in counseling and monitoring the geriatric patient.
