ELCS 8336 - Commun/Jr Coll

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ELCS 7330 or consent of instructor.

Study of community college education - the students, institution, and concepts underlying the programs of instruction. Emphasis on the planning, design, development, implementation, and evaluation of community/junior college programs.
