ETIM 6312 - Legal Issues in Technology

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing or consent of instructor.

Provides an overview of warranty law, deceptive trade practices law, product liability, and class action concepts. Class discussions will focus on legal considerations for managers in technology driven enterprises, risk assessment, and the expense and adverse impact of litigation.
