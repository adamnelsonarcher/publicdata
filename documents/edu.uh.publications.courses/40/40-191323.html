PHLS 6324 - Addictions Counseling

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

This course will provide students with an understanding of theories and techniques of drug and alcohol counseling including sources of help and information, assessment, and program planning.
