HRD 6359 - Trends in Organization Development

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: None.

Graduate standing or consent of instructor. Explores cutting-edge practices and findings in the field of organization learning. Attention will be given to organization theory, organizational learning, knowledge management, diversity and talent management, and development.
