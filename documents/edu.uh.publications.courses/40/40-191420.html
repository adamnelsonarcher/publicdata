FINA 7A51 - Derivatives II

Credit Hours: 1.5
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.5 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and FINA 7A50.

Mechanics and institutional features of futures markets. Hedging strategies with futures. Swaps markets for interest rates, FORX, commodities and bases. Financial engineering.
