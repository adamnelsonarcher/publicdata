PHLS 8329 - Moderation and Mediation Analysis in Psychological and Educational Research

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate Status; PHLS 8319 Inferential Statistics; PHLS 8322 Intermediate Statistics; or consent of instructor.

This course will cover the questions that moderation and mediation analysis can answer, and the underlying principles and the practical applications of these methods. Students will learn how to implement various moderation and mediation analysis procedures using a variety of software packages, including SPSS, SAS, and Mplus.
