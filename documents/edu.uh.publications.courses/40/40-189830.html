PHLS 7325 - Cross-Cultural Aspects of Health

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

An examination of cross-cultural influences on the health problems, medical practices, and use of health services within ethnic minority communities.
