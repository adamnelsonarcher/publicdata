MATH 5387 - Introduction to Data Science

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Linear Algebra.

This is an introductory course in the area of data science. Course will cover a combination of concepts from statistics and machine learning. Examples of topics covered in this course include - regression, support vector machines, clustering, neural networks.
