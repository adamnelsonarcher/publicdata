MATH 6386 - Big Data Analytics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Linear algebra, probability, statistics, or consent of instructor.

Concepts and techniques in managing and analyzing large data sets for data discovery and modeling: big data storage systems, parallel processing platforms, and scalable machine learning algorithms.
