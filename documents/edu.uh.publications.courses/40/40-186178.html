INDE 6333 - Probability Stat For Engineers

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: INDE 6333 - Industrial Ecology.
Prerequisite: Graduate standing or consent of instructor.
