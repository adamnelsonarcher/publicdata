ELCS 6390 - Introduction to Disrupting the School to Prison Pipeline

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: N/A.

This course is designed to expose diverse leaders to the policy and the practice of school discipline programs grounded in the theory of law and order and the criminalization of young people. Connections between theory and practice in the K-12 system will give students an opportunity to learn how to design equitable, and sustainable social, racial, economic and political justice in school culture.
No

Note: This course is designed to prepare school leaders, community activists, and practitioners to understand the complex challenges of K-12 systems with low-income, minority students and their families, teachers, and schools that make up the school-to-prison pipeline.
