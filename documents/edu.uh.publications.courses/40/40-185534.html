CUIN 7311 - Teaching Language and Literacy to Bilingual Learners

Credit Hours: 3.00
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: N/A.

Advanced topics in the teaching of language and literacy to bilingual or ESL learners such as culturally responsive education, literacy development, effective ways to teach vocabulary, and other language components in content classes.
