ELET 6350 - Overview of Computational Health Informatics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

Overview of key areas in health informatics, including, information retrieval, electronic medical records, health information exchange, clinical decision making, telemedicine, consumer and public health informatics, HIPPA and evidence based medicine.
