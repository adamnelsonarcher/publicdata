NURS 8207 - Professional Writing Development

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2
Provides opportunities to identify a population of interest, review the literature, and develop a proposal for a clinical problem that has potential for national impact. Human Subjects Protection/IRB will be explored. Students will be required to seek an appropriate funding agency and submit a grant to support development of an evidence-based project. An overview of the concepts of scholarly and professional writing as well as the processes involved in developing professional publications will be explored.
Note: Online Course Delivery
