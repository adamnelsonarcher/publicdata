PETR 6302 - Reservoir Engineering II

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 6328 , 6351, 6362 or BS in Petroleum Engineering and PETR Graduate Standing or Consent of Program.

Capillary pressure; vertical distribution of gas, oil, and water; relative permeability and fractional flow relationships; Buckley-Leverett equation and linear displacement efficiency of gas and water drives: areal and vertical sweep.
