CUIN 8322 - Mixed Methods Research in Education

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Individuals participating in this course need to have had an introduction to both quantitative and qualitative methods or approval of the instructor.

This course provides an overview of mixed methods research to graduate students who are already familiar with quantitative and qualitative research. Students will gain knowledge of the foundations of mixed methods research, mixed methods quality criteria, major mixed methods research designs, the value added of mixed methods research, and legitimation and validation concepts. Throughout the course, students will develop a scale project to demonstrate skills in designing a mixed methods research study.
