PHOP 6967 - Research Practicum A

Credit Hours: 9.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 9

Prerequisite: None

Practical training in vision research methods and procedures. Provides current in-depth training on methods and procedures for assessment of visual optics, and psychophysical research in vision. Includes training in stimulus design and control, psychophysical methods and procedures, and psychophysical and optical image analysis.


May be repeated.
