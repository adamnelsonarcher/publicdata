CUIN 8384 - Experience-Based Research I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
The course focuses on the substance and techniques of experience-based research. The course involves the analysis and synthesis of key readings and covers a broad range of qualitative research tools.
