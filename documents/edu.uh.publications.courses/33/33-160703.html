MATH 6384 - Discrete Time Model in Finance

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 6382 or consent of instructor.

Single-period securities markets, arbitrage, risk-neutral probabilities, complete and incomplete markets, consumption investment problems, mean-variance portfolio analysis, equilibrium models, valuation of options, futures and other derivatives on equities, currencies, commodities and fixed-income securities.
