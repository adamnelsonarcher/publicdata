CUIN 7321 - Advanced Technology Applications in Education

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: CUIN 7321 - Advanced Telecomputing Applications in Education.
Prerequisite: CUIN 7316 or equivalent HTML skills.

Effective use of information design in education through the conceptualization, design and development of dynamic websites and relational databases.
