MECE 6369 - Mechanical Design Proj

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECE 5367.

Nonlinear state-space models; Phase-plane techniques; describing function methods; stability analysis; Lyapunov function techniques; exact linearization methods; sliding mode control; adaptive control; examples from robotics and spacecraft dynamics.
