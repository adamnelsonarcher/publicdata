SPEC 7316 - Autism: Assessment and Intervention

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: PHLS 7316 Autism: Assessment and Intervention
Prerequisite: Admission to graduate/certification program in special populations.

Investigates developmental and cognitive characteristics of children with autism. Emphasis on diagnostic/prescriptive processes effective with this unique population.
