PHAR 5212 - Literature Evaluation

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Third year standing in the College of Pharmacy or consent of instructor.

To provide students with a better understanding of the medical literature by instilling the essential skills to critically evaluate article findings. The fundamental principles that will be covered include: characteristics of different study designs and interpretation of articles (measurements, variability, statistical significance, risk).
