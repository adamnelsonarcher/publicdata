CUIN 7363 - Soc-Clt Psy Bas Rdg Ins

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ELED 6320 and three hours of approved graduate credit in reading or concurrent enrollment.

Examines instructional implications of sociocultural, psychological, orthographic, and language factors underlying the development of reading ability in preschoolers through young adults.
