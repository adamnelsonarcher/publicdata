INDE 7344 - Network Optimization

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and/or consent of instructor

Concepts and terminologies of networks and graphs; graph theory; complexity theory; network search; shortest path; maximum flow; minimum flow; matching and assignment problems; minimum cost flow; minimum spanning tree.
