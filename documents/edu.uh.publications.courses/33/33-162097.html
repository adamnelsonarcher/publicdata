FINA 7A50 - Introduction to Derivatives

Credit Hours: 1.5
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.5 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and FINA 6A35 .

Mechanics and institutional features of option markets, and trading strategies involving option. Properties of options prices and valuation using the Black-Scholes-Merton model.
