PHAR 4265 - Patient Assessment

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: First year standing in the College of Pharmacy.

Corequisite: PHAR 4340 , PHAR 4251

The student pharmacist will learn how a pharmacist evaluates patient function and dysfunction through the performance of tests and assessments leading to objective and subjective data.
