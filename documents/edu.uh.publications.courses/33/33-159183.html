PHCA 7305 - Social and Behavioral Aspects in Pharmaceutical Health Outcomes and Policy

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: PHCA 6305 Pharmacy Administration and Management I
Prerequisite: Graduate standing.

Introduces social and behavioral concepts in pharmaceutical health outcomes and policy. A case-based discussion approach with pre-assigned reading material.
