NURS 6355 - Management of Health Disorders Across the Lifespan in Diverse Settings

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: NURS 6330 , NURS 6331 , NURS 6338 , NURS 6335 , NURS 6336 , NURS 6345 , NURS 6346 .

Corequisite: NURS 6356

Management of Health Disorders Across the Lifespan in Diverse Settings ClinicalLast of three-course sequence to prepare Family Nurse Practitioners in the care of older adults, and individuals and families across the lifespan in diverse healthcare settings. Systems approach with emphasis on differential diagnosis, complex symptom management and disease treatment, and evaluation of plan.
