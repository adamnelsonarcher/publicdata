MANA 7A32 - Effective Negotiations

Credit Hours: 1.5
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.5 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing; cannot be taken for credit by students who have completed MANA 7332 .

Fundamentals of distributive (i.e., winlose) and integrative (i.e., win-win) negotiating, cross-cultural negotiating, and negotiating in virtual contexts.
