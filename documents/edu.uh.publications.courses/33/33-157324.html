CUIN 7373 - Instr Strat Tchng Adult

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Doctoral degree objective or consent of instructor.

The focus is on how to teach adults so that change in instructional behavior occurs. Three classroom observation systems are taught so that changes in instructional behavior can be evaluated.
