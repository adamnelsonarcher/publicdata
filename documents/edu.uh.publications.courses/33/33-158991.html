PHOP 8385 - Visuomotor Processes

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Monocular and binocular motor mechanisms including pupillomotion, accommodation, vergence, pursuit and saccadic tracking, reflex eye movements, and fixational tremor. Interactions of these mechanisms and abnormalities (e.g., nystagmus and strabismus).
