SPEC 8353 - Seminar in Evidence-Based Practice - G&T

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: CUIN 8353 Theory/Inquiry in C&T in GATE
Prerequisite: SPEC 6349 .

Designed to make beginning doctoral students aware of important problems and issues in curriculum and teaching, to introduce students to methods of formulating questions and to modes of inquiry appropriate to doctoral-level research, and to build a cohesive student cohort.
