SOCW 8200 - Research Internship III

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Doctoral standing or permission from the instructor.

Prepares doctoral students for research experiences by applying an internship experience with an established faculty member.
Course can be repeated for credit.
