FINA 7A33 - Mergers, Acquisitions and Joint Ventures

Credit Hours: 1.5
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.5 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and FINA 7A10 .

In-depth study of major aspects of mergers, acquisitions, and joint ventures and their impact of firm value. Emphasis is on effective techniques for valuation, tax optimization, and post-project implementation.
