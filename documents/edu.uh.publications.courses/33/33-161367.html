CUIN 8385 - Experience-Based Research II

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CUIN 8484.

This qualitative research course focuses on field-based research and involves the interpretation of school observations, interviews, focus groups, archival data, and the creation of participant observation notes.
