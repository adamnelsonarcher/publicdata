CNST 6375 - Building Information Modeling Applications for Construction Management

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1.5 Lab Contact Hours: 1.5
Prerequisite: Graduate standing or consent of instructor.

Introduction to concept of model-based construction management workflows using BIM. Theory and hands-on practices on BIM technologies for important construction management tasks including cost estimating, scheduling, and coordination.
