PETR 7322 - Advanced Formation Evaluation

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: PETR 7320 , PETR PhD Graduate standing and/or consent of both the instructor and the Director of Petroleum Engineering Graduate Admissions.

Physics of logging tools and their interaction with subsurface formations. Quantitative interpretation of well logs with correction for tool response and formation properties. Design of tools to optimize formation evaluation.
