CUIN 8361 - The State of the Curriculum Field in Education

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Consent of instructor and/or enrollment in the CUIN PhD program.

Course examines the state of the curriculum field through critical analysis of handbook chapters, yearbook entries, and other scholarly works.
