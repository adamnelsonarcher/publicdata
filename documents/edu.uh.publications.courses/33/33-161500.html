ELCS 8301 - Leadership Theory for School Administrators

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

Examines advanced leadership theory and application by analysis of leadership, culture, organizational dynamics, change and function. Research, theory, and method are addressed for school administrators.
