COSC 6366 - Knowledge Based Systems

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COSC 4350 or equivalent.

Computerization of human expertise. Topics include: rule based programming; control strategies, inference techniques, knowledge representation techniques, knowledge acquisition strategies of knowledge based systems; models for approximate reasoning; knowledge base design.
