COSC 6356 - Computer Animation and Simulation

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COSC 4370 or COSC 6372 .

Fundamental computer animation techniques, keyframing animation, physics-based simulation, fluid animation and its underlying algorithms, and optimization algorithms.
