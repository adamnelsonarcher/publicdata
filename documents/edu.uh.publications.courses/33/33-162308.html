CUIN 8394 - Scholarly Research & Dissemination I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Admission to Doctoral Program in Curriculum & Instruction.

Focuses on developing, critiquing and disseminating different genres of research papers in Web of Science and other high quality journals.
