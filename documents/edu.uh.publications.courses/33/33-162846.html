BIOE 6308 - Cellular & Molecular Bioengineering

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BIOE 6307 or consent of instructor.

Understanding how molecular cellular components work together as functioning systems to enable cells to execute cellular tasks for the ultimate goal of designing therapeutic strategies to stop diseases and promote regeneration.
