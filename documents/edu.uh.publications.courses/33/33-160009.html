SOCW 8115 - Integrative Colloquium

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Doctoral standing.

Varied topics such as writing for publication, integrating specialization into social work practice, writing dissertation proposals, topics in social work education, and pedagogical methods. Peer review of research ideas with critiques and process reports.
May be repeated.
