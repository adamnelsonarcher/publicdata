COSC 6389 - Computer and Network Security

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 3336 and COSC 4330, graduate standing or consent of instructor.

Security attacks, mechanisms, and services. Overview of secret-key and public-key cryptography, authentication protocols and key management. Email, IP, and web security, intrusion detection and prevention systems. Firewalls, malware, protocol verification.
