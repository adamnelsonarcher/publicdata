OPTO 5271 - Optometry I

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: concurrent enrollment in OPTO 5171 . Overview of the optometric examination.

Discussion of the diagnostic examination process with emphasis on the patient history interview. Introduction to problem-oriented record-keeping as applied to optometric practice.
