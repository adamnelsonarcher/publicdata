MECE 6388 - Optimal Control Theory

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MECE 5367 or equivalent.

Variational calculus maximum principal and Hamilton- Jacoby theory. Linear quadratic regulator/servo problems and minimum time control. Computational methods in optimum systems control.
