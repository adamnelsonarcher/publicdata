BIOE 6301 - Statistical Methods in Biomedical Engineering

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing or approval of instructor.

Foundation of statistical literacy with regard to basic methods of descriptive statistics, parameter estimation, and hypothesis testing. Additional emphasis will be placed on the use of computers for statistical analysis and modeling.
