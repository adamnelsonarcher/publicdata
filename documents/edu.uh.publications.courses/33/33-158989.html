PHOP 7165 - Advanced Seminar in Physiological Optics and Visual Sciences

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 1 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

Review scientific papers in a topical area of physiological optics and visual sciences such as spatial and temporal aspects of vision, visual optics, biology and structure and function of ocular surface or retina, eye movements. Students make oral presentations; critique papers, reviews, and presentations.
May be repeated for credit.
