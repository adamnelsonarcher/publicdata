MECE 6357 - Computational Fluid Dynamics II

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: I. MECE 3363, BIOE 3440 or equivalent fluid mechanics course; II. MATH 3321 or equivalent; or permission of instructor

Solution methods for the Navier-Stokes equations. Complex geometries and grid generation. Turbulent flows.
