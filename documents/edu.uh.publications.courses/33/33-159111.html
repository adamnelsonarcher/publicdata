PHAR 5332 - Pharmacokinetics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3.0 Lab Contact Hours: 0.0
Prerequisite: PHAR 4330 and PHAR 4331 .

The goal of the course is to equip students with the basic understanding of pharmacokinetic principles and their applications in rational use of medications for optimal therapeutic outcomes, in terms of regimen recommendation, therapeutic drug monitoring and regimen modification.
