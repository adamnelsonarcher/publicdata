PETR 6364 - Origin and Development of Oil and Gas Reservoirs

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: PETR 6362 - Methods of Applied Mathematics.
Prerequisite: PETR 6351 and PETR Graduate Standing or Consent of Program.

Major oil provinces of the world reviewed from the standpoints of geologic and depositional environment and of diagenetic changes affecting petroleum entrapment.
