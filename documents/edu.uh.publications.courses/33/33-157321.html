CUIN 7364 - Literacy Dev Yng Child

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ELED 6320 or equivalent or consent of instructor.

Examination of theories concerning developmental stages and environmental factors involved in early childhood acquisition of literacy during the preschool years through early primary grades.
