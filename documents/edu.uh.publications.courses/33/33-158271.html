LAW 5411 - Health Law Externship I

Credit Hours: 4.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 4 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Completion of or concurrent enrollment in LAW 6322 (Health Law Survey: Bioethics & Quality of Care), LAW 6331 - Health Law Financing, Organization, and Qualityor permission of instructor required. In addition, requires a minimum GPA of 2.7.

Students have the opportunity to work with non-profit organizations or government agencies dealing with health issues.
