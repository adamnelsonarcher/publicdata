CUIN 7306 - Design & Development of Web Graphics for Education

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: CUIN 6320 or permission of instructor.

Conceptual framework for and experiences in developing visually-rich and appropriate materials for online environments that support and promote active teaching and learning.
