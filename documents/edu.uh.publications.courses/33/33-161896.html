IDNS 6391 - Ethics in Science

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing and consent of instructor

The course links historical cases of ethics to present science standards and practices, while offering experiential training.
