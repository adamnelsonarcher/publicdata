IDNS 6393 - Advanced Science Ethics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: IDNS 6391 or consent of instructor

Includes a semester long practicum observing ongoing animal or human subject experiments. In-depth discussions of problem in scientific ethics, including issues encountered by students during practicum.
