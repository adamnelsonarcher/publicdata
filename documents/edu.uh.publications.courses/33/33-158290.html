LAW 5353 - Business Planning

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: LAW 5359 , LAW 5459 - Federal Income Tax; and either LAW 5421 - Business Organizationsor LAW 5350 (Corporations).

An advanced course in the legal problems encountered in the organization of a corporation. Problems of taxation are a major topic of this course.
