LAW 6331 - Health Law Financing, Organization, and Quality

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
An introductory health law course addressing the regulation, structure, and financing of the American health care system. Regulation and structural issues include how health care institutions are legally organized, accreditation, medical staff disputes, managed care, fraud and abuse, health care transactions, and antitrust. Access and financing issues include private health insurance, ERISA, COB RA, HIPAA, Medicare, and Medicaid.
