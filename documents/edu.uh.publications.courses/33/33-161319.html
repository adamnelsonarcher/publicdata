SOCW 7372 - SW and Global Justice Issues

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Successful completion of foundation curriculum

Analysis of international political economy and human rights issues; rights and responsibilities of global citizenship; and development of advocacy strategies and skills for achieving social justice goals. Includes study of power and oppression as fundamental to understanding global justice issues in social work context.
