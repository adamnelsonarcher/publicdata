CUIN 8334 - Issues in Multicultural / Diversity Education

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

This course is an opportunity to rethink multicultural and diversity education for the 21st century. You will engage in critical constructivist collaborations to examine current issues in rethinking multicultural / diversity education and will construct meaning as a community of learners as the course progresses. The course will consist of discussions in rethinking multicultural and diversity education. Course Notes: Admission to the EdD or PhD in Curriculum and Instruction or the consent of the instructor.
