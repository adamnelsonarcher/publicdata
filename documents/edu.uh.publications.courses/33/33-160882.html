SOCW 7363 - Hc Systems & Issues

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOCW 6323 and 31 hours in social work or the consent of the instructor.

Analysis of existing health care systems, emphasizing consumer access, distribution of resources and quality of care. Attention is given to the impact of health/illness in relation to implications for social work practice.
