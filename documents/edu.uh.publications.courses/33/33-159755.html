POLS 6384 - Survey Research Methods

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: POLS 6480 .

Practical issues of sampling, questionnaire design, interviewing techniques, and supervision; analysis of survey results and the presentation of data to academic, governmental, and commercial audiences.
