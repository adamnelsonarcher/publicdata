PUBL 6348 - Seminar in Urban Service Delivery

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None

Review of political, economic, and administrative factors affecting the delivery of urban services. Particular attention paid to questions of efficiency, effectiveness, and equity in services provided by local governments.
