BIOE 6370 - The Spectrum of Human Diseases

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Enrollment in MIPH program, or consent of instructor.

Overview of the following diseases, including current treatment & prevention approaches: Skin Diseases; Musculoskeletal Diseases; Diseases of Heart and Blood Vessels; Blood Diseases; Diseases of the Respiratory System; Gastrointestinal Diseases; Diseases of the Liver, Gall Bladder & Pancreas; Endocrine Diseases; Neurological Diseases; Diseases of the Urinary System; Diseases of Reproductive Organs; Immune-mediated Diseases.
