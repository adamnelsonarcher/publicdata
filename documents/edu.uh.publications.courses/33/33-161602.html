INDE 6360 - Engineering Analytics

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate Standing.

Method and application of analytics techniques in statistical learning, machine learning and predictive modeling . The course will cover a variety of analytic techniques including regression, classification, tree-based methods, model assessment and selection, prediction enhancement, system modeling and identification, and high-dimensional statistical modeling.
