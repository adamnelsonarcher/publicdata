LAW 6258 - Medical Malpractice Litigation

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: LAW 6322 or permission of instructor required.

A broad-based study of malpractice law & policy, including the effect of malpractice on health care access, quality, & cost; malpractice legal doctrine; & legislative reforms.
