BIOE 6307 - Cell Biology for BME

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing or consent of instructor.

Cell biological concepts such as cell structure, DNA replication, RNA transcription, translation of proteins, cell membrane and methods to study each.
