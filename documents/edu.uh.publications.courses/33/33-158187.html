LAW 5215 - Environmental Law in Oil & Gas Production & Refining

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None

Protection of the environment is not a “money making” proposition for the oil and gas industry. A good environment record may gain a company better public relations versus its “messier” peers and be a selling point to foreign governments in attempts to gain exploration concessions or locate refineries and chemical plants on foreign shores.
