BIOE 6372 - Exercise for Health

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Enrollment in MIPH program, or consent of instructor.

Introduction to Exercise and Health; Strength Exercises, Endurance Exercises, Balance Exercises and Flexibility Exercises; Fitness Training Methods; Exercise for Special Populations; Exercise Nutrition & Exercise Physiology; Exercise in Wellness and Health Promotion; Sport and Exercise Promotion; Fitness assessment; Clinical Fitness Evaluation Techniques; Lifestyle Diseases and Exercise Prescription. Impact of Exercise on disease outcomes; evidence based studies; Impact of Physical activity on cardiovascular diseases, metabolic diseases, including diabetes, and other chronic diseases; Clinical Applications of Exercise; Exercise Prescription, Referral & Rehabilitation; Health and Fitness Technology; Hands-on Training sessions.
