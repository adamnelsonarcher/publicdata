PEP 7317 - Exercise Immunology

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: none.

Lectures and discussions examining how exercise and stress can alter immunity in both human and animal models. Course emphasis will focus primarily on immunology.
