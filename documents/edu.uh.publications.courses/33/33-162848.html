BIOE 6374 - Integrative and Complementary Health

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Enrollment in MIPH program, or consent of instructor.

Integrative and Alternative Medicine - An overview; Integrating Western and Oriental Medicine; Integrating Nutrition and Exercise into Healthcare; Integrative Medicine - - Invited Practitioner Talks; Traditional Chinese Medicine and acupuncture; Ayurvedic Medicine, Aromatherapy & Health; Chiropractic approaches; Manual therapies, Moxibusion; Herbals and Dietary Supplements; Meditation, Music Therapy, Spiritual Healing; Lifestyle Medicine - Integrative Health Coaching; Hands-on Practice sessions.
