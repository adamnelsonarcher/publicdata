MTLS 6320 - Nanomaterials Engineering

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Cross-Listed As: Crosslisted with CHEE 6320 .

Prerequisite: Enrollment in Materials Engineering graduate program.

Engineering of nanomaterials with emphasis on structural, optical, photonic, magnetic and electronic materials. Synthetic methods and analytical characterization with design for applications will be emphasized. (Crosslisted with CHEE 6320 .)
