LAW 6332 - Written Advocacy

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

The study of written advocacy for litigators; intended for students who wish to improve their persuasive writing skills, especially for civil litigation practice.
