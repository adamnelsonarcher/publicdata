CUIN 6394 - Beginning Teacher Induction I

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: CUIN 6394 - Practicum for First Year Teachers.
Prerequisite: Admission to Masters Induction Program.

Induction support for beginning teachers. Emphases on inquiry-based practices, student-centered approaches, teacher reflection, cooperative problem solving, classroom observations, and mentoring.
