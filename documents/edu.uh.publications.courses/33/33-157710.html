PETR 6308 - Advanced Petroleum Production Operations

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: PETR 6304 - Evaluation of Petroleum-Bearing Formations I.
Prerequisite: PETR 6372 and PETR Graduate Standing or Consent of Program.

Advanced topics including diagnostic methods including well testing, production logging, and decline curve analysis. Conformance application. Well stimulation by acid and hydraulic fracturing. Analysis of fracturing tests. Horizontal well performance.
