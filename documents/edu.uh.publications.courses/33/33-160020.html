SOCW 8322 - Research Methods II: Applied Quantitative Research on Evidence-Based Social Work

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: SOCW 8311 and SOCW 8424 or permission fron instructor.

Building upon the fundamentals learned in the first research course (Research Mthods I), This Seminar emphasizes the application of this knowledge to specific research topic and providing and receiving critical appraisals of each others assignments.
