PEP 8340 - Pedagogical Philosophies

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Doctoral standing or instructor permission.

The art and science of successfully providing instruction in the modern college classroom. Content organization and assessment techniques for the face-to-face, traditional online, virtual and MOOC classrooms will be addressed.
