BIOE 6377 - Technologies for Disease Prevention & Management

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Enrollment in MIPH program, or consent of instructor.

Overview of healthcare technology advances ; current role and future perspectives; Clinical imaging for disease screening and diagnostics; Endoscopy for disease screening and diagnostics; Fitbits and health monitors; Wearable Technologies like Google Glass; Point of Care Devices, Home Testing & Digestible Sensors; Currently available OTC technologies for health monitoring; Diagnostics for Low-resource settings; 3D Printed Biological Materials; Implantable organoids ; pacemakers, artificial pancreas, kidney, etc.; Optogenetics; Remote patient monitoring, Data Communication & Cloud based technologies; Genetic Risk Profiling; Biomarkers for identifying high-risk individuals; Advances in Prosthetics; Brain-computer interface.
