MATH 6369 - Mathematical Hemodynamics II

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Math 6326 or consent of instructor.

Basic fluid dynamics and Navier-Stokes equations, mathematical models of arterial walls (elasticity of thin and thick structures), fluid-structure interaction, reduced models for blood in elastic/viscoelastic arteries (the 1D hyperbolic model), full 2D and 3D models of fluid-structure interaction between blood flow and arterial walls: topics in mathematical theory and numerical method development.
