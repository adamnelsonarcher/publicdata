PHOP 8365 - Advanced Seminar in Physiological Optics & Visual Sciences

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

Reading scientific papers in a topical area of physiological optics and visual sciences; preparing written reviews and making oral presentations; critiquing papers, reviews, and presentations made by others.
May be repeated for credit.
