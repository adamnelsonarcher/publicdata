SPEC 6385 - Meeting the Social and Emotional Needs of Students with Gifts and Talents

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: CUIN 6385 Meeting the Social and Emotional Needs of Gifted and Talented Education
Prerequisite: SPEC 6349 .

Study the particular developmental aspects of gifted students in order to identify their unique social and emotional characteristics. Examine psycho-social concepts related to personality, maturation, and creativity.
