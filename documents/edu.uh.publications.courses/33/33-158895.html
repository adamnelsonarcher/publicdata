OPTO 5171 - Clinic Practicum I

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: concurrent enrollment in OPTO 5271 .

Practical instruction in diagnostic and therapeutic techniques used in primary care optometric practice. Introduction to ocular health assessment techniques and use of opthalmic diagnostic agents.
