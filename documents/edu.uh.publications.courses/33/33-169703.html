MUSI 6206 - Introduction to the Organ

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing in Music.

An introduction to the pipe organ as a musical and mechanical instrument; fundamentals of registration, construction, and maintenance will be covered.
