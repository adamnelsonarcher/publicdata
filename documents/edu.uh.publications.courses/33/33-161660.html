PEP 8308 - Genetic and Physiological Basis for Obesity and its Comorbidities

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None

To examine the role of genes in mediating the physiologic mechanisms linking obesity and its comorbidities. To survey common laboratory methodology used to assess biomarkers of obesity and disease.
