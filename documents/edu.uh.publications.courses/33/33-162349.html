MATH 7382 - Monte Carlo Statistical Methods

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Math 6383 or consent of instructor.

Generating of random samples and sample paths, variance reduction techniques, statistical analysis of simulation experiments, quasi-Monte Carlo, and sensitivity analysis, Markov chain Monte Carlo, Gibbs sampling, hidden Markov models and EM algorithms and Bayesian statistical methods.
