LAW 6307 - Sports Law

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Analysis of topics such as representation of the professional athlete in contract negotiations and endorsements, the player-club contractual relationship, anti-trust and collective bargaining issues in amateur and professional sports, sports tort liability, and representation of the recreational amateur and professional in sports injury litigation.
