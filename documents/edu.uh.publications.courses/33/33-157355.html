SPEC 8354 - Seminar in Gifted and Talented Education

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: CUIN 8354 Seminar in Gifted and Talented Education
Prerequisite: SPEC 8353 .

A topical seminar that examines issues like identification of students with gifts and talents, equity in gifted education, and the effects of No Child Left Behind.
