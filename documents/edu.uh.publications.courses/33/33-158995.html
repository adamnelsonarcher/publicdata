PHOP 6X57 - Research Practicum B

Credit Hours: 1.0 - 6.0
Practical training in vision research methods and procedures. Provides current in-depth training on cutting-edge methods and procedures for biological research in vision. Includes training in cell and molecular, biochemical, morphological, and physiological methods and procedures for vision research.
