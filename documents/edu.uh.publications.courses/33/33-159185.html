PHCA 7307 - Research Methods in Pharmaceutical Health Outcomes and Policy

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: PHCA 6307 Research Methods in Pharmacy Administration
Prerequisite: PHCA 7308 or Consent of Instructor.

Principles of research methods and its application to conduct and analyze research topics in pharmaceutical health outcomes research. Included are computer applications, design of survey questionnaires, and proficiency in analyzing programs/policies.
