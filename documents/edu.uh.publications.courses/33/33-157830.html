ECE 6324 - Digital Telephony

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ECE 4371 or consent of instructor.

Voice communications as well as application and operational aspects of digital communication systems design. Specifics treated are voice digitization, digital transmission, digital switching, network synchronization, network control, network analysis, and communications networks design.
