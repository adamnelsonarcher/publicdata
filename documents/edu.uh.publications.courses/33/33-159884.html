PSYC 8320 - Field Work in Org Development

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: graduate standing.

Best practices in applying organizational behavior, human resources, and organization development research to organizational problems; content varies in order to leverage emergent field opportunities.
May be repeated with instructor approval.
