GEOL 6319 - Rocks and Minerals

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 3
Prerequisite: Consent of faculty advisor.

Minerals and megascopic petrography of common rocks. Classification, identification, description, and interpretation of rocks in hand specimen.
Note: Due to its remedial nature, this course will not count toward a graduate degree.
