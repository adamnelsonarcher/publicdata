CUIN 8335 - Doctoral Seminar in Social Education

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: None.

This course is offered as an opportunity to engage in an in depth investigation of issues and research in social education. Participants will engage in a critical constructivist endeavor to examine ideas and issues regarding social education. The members of the class will therefore construct meaning as a community of learners. Course Notes: Admission to the EdD or PhD in Curriculum and Instruction or consent of the instructor.
