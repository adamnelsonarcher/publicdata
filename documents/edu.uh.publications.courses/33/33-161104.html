OPTO 6115 - Clinical Integration

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: OPTO 6291 or permission of instructor.

Corequisite: OPTO 7493

Clinical case-based scenarios used to facilitate classroom activities that emphasize clinical reasoning, prioritized clinical problem solving, doctor-patient communication, inter-professional communication, and self-directed learning. Taught in interactive seminars.
