MATH 7323 - Reproducing Kernel Hilbert Spaces

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MATH 6320 -6321or consent of instructor.

Reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces are Hilbert spaces of actual functions. This course studies their theory and applications in statistics, machine learning, complex analysis and integral operators.
