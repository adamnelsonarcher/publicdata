BIOE 6373 - Environmental Health

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Enrollment in MIPH program, or consent of instructor.

Introduction to the Environment; Environmental determinants of Human Health; Risk Assessment: Air, Land, and Water; Exposure Assessment; Air Quality & Pollution; Water Quality & Pollution; Other Threats to the Environment - Noise, EM, radiation; Environmental Toxicology; Solid and Hazardous Wastes; Occupational Health & Ergonomics; Environmental Protection; Energy, transportation and urbanization; Global Environmental Health; Technologies for; Environmental Monitoring; Environmental Law & Policy; Invited Lectures from Leaders in the Field.
