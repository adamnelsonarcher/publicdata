ELET 6325 - Practicum in Engineering Technology

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Completion of 12 graduate credits, a 3.25 GPA and consent of advisor.

Work experience in a pre-approved industrial site/research faciltity. Analyze, integrate, improve, organize and manage a complex system relevant to the program.
Course may be repeated twice.
