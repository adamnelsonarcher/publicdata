BIOL 6217 - Genome Biology and Technology

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BIOL 3301 or BCHS 4306 or equivalent and graduate standing or consent of instructor

History, execution, and completion of the human genome project and the surprising discovery of non-coding RNAs. Emerging technologies in the post-genome era and their application toward dissecting biology and disease.
