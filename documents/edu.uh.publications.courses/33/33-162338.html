BIOL 6206 - Biological Networks

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Consent of instructor.

This course explores different ways of using networks to represent and analyse biological.systems. Topics include:network. theory, gene networks, social networks, and ecological networks.
