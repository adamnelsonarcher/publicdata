PHAR 5221 - Medication Management Services

Credit Hours: 2
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: 3rd year standing in UH College of Pharmacy or consent of instructor.

This course is an innovative and interactive training program that explores the pharmacists role in providing MTM services to patients which includes evaluation of complicated medication regimens, identification of medication related problems, and making recommendation to patients, caregivers and health care professionals.
