PHCA 7313 - Pharmacoeconomics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: PHCA 6312 Pharmacoeconomics in Outcomes Research
Prerequisite: PHCA 7306 or consent of instructor.

An integrated course that provides students with knowledge and skills in pharmacoeconomics and health outcomes research with application to disease state management.
