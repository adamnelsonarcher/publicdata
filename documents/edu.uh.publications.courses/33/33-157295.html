CUIN 7329 - Technology Content Areas

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Development of strategies for integration of technology into the major content areas, with specific emphasis on planning, teaching, and assessment, as well as evaluation of educational technology resources.
