CUIN 7692 - Internship

Credit Hours: 6.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 6
Prerequisite: CUIN 6358 , CUIN 6359 , CUIN 7302 .

Students gain practical museum education experience within the environment of a specific institution. Internship projects will be determined based on the interests of the students and needs of the host museum.May be repeated for credit.
