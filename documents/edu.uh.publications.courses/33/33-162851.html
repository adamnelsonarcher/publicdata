BIOE 6311 - Advances in Vision Research

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing or consent of instructor.

Understanding the anatomy and development of the eye, molecular signaling, neural pathways, eye diseases, and explore emerging technologies used to correct the visual system.
