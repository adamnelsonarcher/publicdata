PHAR 5677 - Pharmacokinetics

Credit Hours: 6.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 18
Prerequisite: Fourth year standing in the College of Pharmacy.

To provide clinical experience in determining initial drug dosage and subsequent change based on individualized pharmacokinetic parameters for drugs with a narrow therapeutic index.
