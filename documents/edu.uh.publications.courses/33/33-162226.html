MUSI 6367 - French Music 1830-1870

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: MUSI 6300 .

Survey of French music written between the July Revolution and the Franco-Prussian War, emphasizing French romanticism’s development during the July Monarchy and French classicism’s resurgence after Louis-Napoln’s election.
