INDE 7361 - Industrial Robotics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: graduate standing and consent of instructor.

Robot motion planning and control, kinematics, sensors and vision systems, design and analysis of robot systems, control and operation of manipulators critical to robots and their operation.
