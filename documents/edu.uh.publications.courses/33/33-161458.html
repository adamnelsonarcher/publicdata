ECON 6333 - Applied Methods I

Credit Hours: 3
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: Graduate standing in economics.

Introduction to applied economic research methods. Topics include model development, methods for locating and collecting economic data, application of data analysis tools, and writing. Emphasis will be on applying econometric techniques to real-world problems.
