MTLS 6319 - Introduction to Nanoengineering

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Cross-Listed As: Crosslisted with CHEE 6319 .

Prerequisite: Enrollment in Materials Engineering graduate program.

Introduction to nanoengineering. Fundamental concepts underlying various nanotechnologies including nanoscale physics, methology at the nanoscale, and the basics of material synthesis and device fabrication. (Crosslisted with CHEE 6319 .)
