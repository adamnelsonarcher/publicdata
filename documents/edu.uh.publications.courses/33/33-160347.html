BIOL 6215 - Cellular Neuroscience

Credit Hours: 2.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 2 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: BCHS 3305 and BIOL 6214 or equivalents or consent of instructor.

Molecular and cellular mechanisms of information processing in neurons, with emphasis on mechanisms thought to be involved in neuronal plasticities such as learning and memory.
