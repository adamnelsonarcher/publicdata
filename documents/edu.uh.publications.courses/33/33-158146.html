LAW 5112 - Advocates-Hippard Mock Trial

Credit Hours: 1.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 0 Lab Contact Hours: 1
Students seeking credit for this course must participate in the Hippard Mock Trial Competition. The Hippard Competition is an intra-scholastic mock trial competition where students can compete in the Hippard Open or Hippard Novice Competition.
