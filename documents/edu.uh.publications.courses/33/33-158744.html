ENGL 7337 - Second Lang Acquisition

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: ENGL 7334 or equivalent.

Advanced study of linguistic, psycholinguistic, and sociocultural aspects of second language acquisition with particular emphasis on adult learning of English as a second language.
