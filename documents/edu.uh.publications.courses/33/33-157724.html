PETR 6332 - Deterministic Reserves Estimation

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Formerly/Same as: PETR 6332 - Reserves Estimation I.
Prerequisite: PETR 6361, 6362 or BS in Petroleum Engineering and PETR Graduate Standing or Consent of Program.

SPE/PRMS and US/SEC reserves definitions and reporting requirements. Deterministic methods of reserves estimation, including volumetric, analogy, material balance, decline curves, and reservoir simulation. Cash flow analysis and international petroleum contracts.
