COSC 7372 - Adv Computer Graphics

Credit Hours: 3.0
Lecture Contact Hours: 3 Lab Contact Hours: 0
Prerequisite: COSC 6372 or consent of the instructor.

In-depth treatment of topics in computer graphics, including computer animation, photo-realistic image synthesis, volume-rendering, and other subjects of current interest.
May be repeated for credit when topics vary.
